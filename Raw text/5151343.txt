About the position
Job description, responsibilities and duties
As a Global Verint Operations Management (OM) Consultant within ING bank, you will lead the implementation and optimization of Verint (OM) application across international branches and operational units. Your role is critical in enhancing workforce management efficiency, ensuring regulatory compliance, and enabling data-driven decision-making through advanced Verint capabilities. You will act as a consulting advisor, technical expert, and change agent in the bank’s digital transformation journey.
Key Responsibilities
1. Verint OM Implementation & Project Leadership
Independently manage end-to-end Verint applications roll out to support Operations Management across multiple banking units, ensuring alignment with operational goals and compliance standards. Lead/coordinate cross-functional project teams, manage timelines, and ensure successful delivery of Verint solution. Apply structured change management approaches to drive adoption and minimize disruption in regulated environments.
2. Verint application Expertise & Internal Consulting
Serve as the bank’s internal Verint expert, advising on tool capabilities, configuration, and optimization strategies. Deliver tailored implementation, training and coaching to operational teams, enabling self-sufficiency and maximizing tool utilization.
Translate complex business requirements into scalable Verint solution that supports operational transparency and performance tracking.
3. Digitalization & API Integration
Coordinate digital transformation initiatives involving Verint API integrations with core banking workflow systems, CRM platforms, and data warehouses. Collaborate with IT, compliance, and data governance teams to ensure secure, compliant, and efficient data flows. Oversee development and deployment of Verint dashboards that support real-time monitoring and strategic insights.
4. Data Harmonization & Insight Enablement
Promote consistent usage of Verint tool across teams to ensure harmonized data collection and reporting. Support senior management with high-quality, actionable insights derived from Verint dashboards and analytics. Identify opportunities for automation, process improvement, and predictive analytics within Verint-enabled workflows.
