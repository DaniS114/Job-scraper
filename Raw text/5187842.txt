Project Management
Working Student - Technical Project Management (Scrum)
Join us on our exciting journey as we shape the future of telecommunications and pioneer groundbreaking AI solutions!
We are seeking a motivated and talented working student to support our AI Enabler team. In this role, you will play a crucial role in our rapidly growing team and in driving the development of our AI solutions. This is an excellent opportunity to gain hands-on experience in AI product development, agile methodologies, and collaboration with a multinational team.

agreement-based (Temporary jobs)

About
the position
Collaborate closely with developers, team members, and customers to drive the successful delivery of innovative AI product features.
Act as Scrum Master by leading Agile ceremonies, including sprint planning, daily stand-ups, sprint reviews, and retrospectives, ensuring a smooth and efficient workflow.
Support the Product Manager in both strategic planning and day-to-day operational tasks.
Analyze business data sets and create simple scripts for data evaluation and process automation.
Champion user needs throughout the development process, offering guidance on UI/UX design to enhance the user experience.
Serve as a vital link between business and technology, ensuring clear communication and alignment across all stakeholders.

we expect

Personality requirements and skills
You are currently enrolled in a Master’s degree program – ideally in Business Informatics, Industrial Engineering, or a related field at the intersection of IT and business.
You have advanced proficiency in both written and spoken English.
You are passionate about IT, with a strong interest in Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence.
You have initial experience in project management and relevant tools (preferably Jira), and a genuine interest in product development.
You are familiar with Agile methodologies such as Scrum – or are eager to quickly get up to speed.
You possess at least basic Python skills and hands-on experience with data analysis.
You’re an excellent communicator – both verbally and in writing – and enjoy working in a multicultural, collaborative environment.
You’re highly motivated and eager to translate academic knowledge into practical, real-world solutions.
You work independently, take initiative, and enjoy tackling new challenges head-on.
Nice to have: Experience in graphic design, ideally with Adobe InDesign.
