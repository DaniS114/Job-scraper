Job description, responsibilities and duties
We are looking for an experienced
Remote Engineer
to join our team and take responsibility for configuring, powering up, and commissioning RLS and Waveserver devices remotely. This role is essential for seamless optical network activation and integration.
Job responsibilities include:
Remotely configure and commission Ciena 6500 RLS and Waveserver devices according to technical specifications.
Perform software upgrades, including license validation, configuration backups, and package handling.
Manage power-up sequences and activation procedures remotely.
Collaborate with onsite smart hands or installers for physical installations, cabling, and fiber connections.
Monitor system performance and validate optical parameters using system tools.
Troubleshoot and resolve issues related to configuration, commissioning, and power management.
Utilize automation and zero-touch provisioning for efficient deployments.
Document all commissioning activities and configuration changes.
Support link calibration, fiber loss compensation, and connectivity validation.
Coordinate with Network Operations, Engineering, and Customer teams for smooth deployment and handover.
