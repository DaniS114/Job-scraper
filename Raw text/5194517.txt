Senior SAP PM/QM Consultant
Place of work
Remote work
Contract type

POSITION
DESCRIPTION
We are looking for an experienced SAP Consultant with strong expertise in Plant Maintenance (PM) and Quality Management (QM). In this role, you will design, implement, and support SAP PM and QM solutions to ensure efficient maintenance operations and quality control processes across the organization.
WHAT WILL
YOU DO
• Configure and maintain SAP PM (Preventive Maintenance, Work Orders, Notifications) and QM (Inspection Plans, Quality Certificates, Defect Recording).
• Analyze business requirements and translate them into functional specifications.
• Support integration with other SAP modules (MM, PP, SD).
• Provide end-user training and documentation.
• Troubleshoot and resolve issues related to PM and QM processes.
• Collaborate with maintenance, production, and quality teams to optimize workflows.
WHAT WE
EXPECT
• Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in IT or a related field.
• 5+ years of hands-on SAP PM and QM experience in implementation, rollout, or end-to-end migration projects for enterprise clients
• Strong understanding of maintenance and quality processes in manufacturing.
• Experience with configuration, master data, and reporting.
• Knowledge of integration points with MM and PP.
• English language proficiency at upper intermediate (B2) or advanced (C1) level.
• German language proficiency at upper intermediate (B2) level.
• Experience with SAP S/4HANA preferred.
• Project implementation or migration experience.
