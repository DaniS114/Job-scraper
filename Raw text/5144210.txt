Job description, responsibilities and duties
Tasks and Responsibilities :
You act as the functional HR/IT Business Analyst and Solution Designer for all HR/IT Mini-Master and Authority  Management Topics
You align business demands and processes with our IT strategy and consult the business accordingly while acting as the subject matter expert within the respective portfolio element
You will translate the business demands into IT service requirements and ensure proper implementation of your solution design following our IT service management- and IT compliance framework
You think about the 'big picture" within the Assurance strategy and IT key projects, considering end-to-end processess and identify cross-topic dependencies
You collaborate closely with internal stakeholders and external service management teams and drive related activities
Required Knowledge/Skills, Education, and Experience :
Education:
BS/BA in related discipline (or equivalent experience)
Knowledge and Experience:
You hold a bachelor or master degree in a subject related to information technology or business administration
You have a minimum of 3-5 year’s experience with SAP HCM Solutions - idealy time-management
You have a thorough understanding of UI5 based SAP design und understanding of front end technologies and there integration with SAP systems
You have a thorough understanding of Org.Manegement related processes  with focus on Compliance processes and respective IT solutions / integrations
You are interested in regualatory data management to enable workflows
You are experienced working in international and interdisciplinary HR/IT related projects
You have a good knowledge of managing IT service providers
You are familiar with and understand the compliance requirements as they pertain to IT projects in the healthcare industry
Preferred Knowledge/Skills :
You strive to translate new business and operational challenges into value-adding technology solutions
You are a well-structured and detail-oriented worker with a strong ability to think analytically and you don't compromise on quality, even under pressure
You have particularly strong teamworking skills, are proactive and like taking the lead on topics incl. seeing things through to the end
You have a knack for stakeholder management and a high level of service orientation and you are known for your excellent communication skills
You already have extensive experience with international and intercultural cooperation and communicate reliable across cultures and organizational levels
Your English language skills are excellent, both spoken and written; your command of the German language is a plus
