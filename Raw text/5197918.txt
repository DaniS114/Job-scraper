Information about the position
Job description, responsibilities and duties
In a dynamic environment where reliability and smart infrastructure matter, your expertise can make all the difference.
We are seeking a skilled
Systems Engineer
who enjoys hands-on work with infrastructure, servers, and network devices.
In this role, you will be a key part of keeping our data center running smoothly — configuring modems, switches, and Cisco routers, monitoring system health, automating data flows, deploying new hardware, and resolving technical issues. You’ll ensure our systems remain secure, reliable, and up to date, while also bringing fresh ideas to continuously improve the infrastructure.
If you want a position where your work truly shapes how the entire system performs, this is the opportunity for you.
Key Responsibilities
Work directly on complex technical problem identification and resolution.
Operate independently on advanced systems or infrastructure components supporting multiple applications or services.
Manage and maintain DataCenter devices: servers (*nix), *nix-based firewalls, and virtualization environments.
Configure, validate, and deploy network devices (modems, switches, Cisco routers), including new hardware setup, troubleshooting, and functionality testing.
Support and improve infrastructure tooling — including automation, data-flow testing, and custom solutions.
Monitor system health using tools such as Zabbix and Grafana, tracking alerts, performance, temperature, and device status across 3000+ connected units.
Collaborate with fellow systems engineers to optimize and evolve the infrastructure.
Perform additional duties assigned by Digital management as needed.
