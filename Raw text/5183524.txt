Job description, responsibilities and duties
Our opportunity
Senior Test Analyst
A Senior Test Analyst is responsible for delivering all test activities within the assigned projects, and to implement processes and tests accelerating techniques.
Your role
As a Senior Test Analyst you will be accountable for end-to-end Quality Assurance of your assigned projects. Your main responsibilities will involve
Create Test Plans in collaboration with testers and other team members (BAs, developers)
Review and analyze product backlogs and collaborate with team members (Business Analysts, Developers, other testers) to define and/or understand acceptance criteria
Develop test cases and test scenarios based on the system requirements, run peer-reviews and script the test cases as much as possible
Participate in walkthroughs with respective business stakeholders
Define and create test data for test execution
Execute test cases as per the agreed testing plan and defined priorities, log and retest defects
Assist with Root Cause Analysis and troubleshooting of defects with developers and BAs
Lead and participate in defect triage meetings and create test status and summary reports
Training of other testers joining the team and support business user testing
Report significant issues or concerns to other stakeholders (Product Owner, Technical lead, Test manager) before they escalate
Lead and manage a team of testers to work on functional test preparation and execution
Back up for some Test management activities when test manager is absent
