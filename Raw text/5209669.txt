Job description, responsibilities and duties
Join a fast-growing international group with over 80+ well-known brands across Europe, currently undergoing a major digital transformation.
The Data & Analytics team sits at the heart of this change — unifying data from multiple systems across the group into a single, modern data fabric that powers decision-making at all levels of the business.
What You’ll Do
Design and develop modern Power BI dashboards that turn complex data into actionable insights
Collaborate with internal stakeholders and external partners to enhance a Microsoft-based data platform
Work hands-on with technologies such as Microsoft Fabric, Azure Synapse, Databricks, and Power Platform
Play a key role in the group-wide rollout of Power BI and the evolution of self-service analytics
Contribute to the continuous improvement of data quality and reporting efficiency
What We’re Looking For
Proven experience building impactful Power BI dashboards (including DAX, data modeling, and visualization best practices)
Strong SQL skills and familiarity with data transformation or ETL concepts
Exposure to the Azure ecosystem (Synapse, Databricks, Logic Apps, Power Platform) is a plus — curiosity and eagerness to learn are even more important
Excellent communication skills and the ability to collaborate effectively with IT, Finance, and Operations teams
Fluent English and a proactive, problem-solving mindset
What’s on Offer
A truly international environment – work from home
Exposure to cutting-edge BI and cloud technologies
Real opportunity for career growth, whether toward data engineering or analytics strategy
Work on high-impact transformation projects shaping the future of the group
Open and collaborative culture that values innovation and initiative
