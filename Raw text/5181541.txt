Data Scientist
We are seeking an experienced and Data Scientist to join our team. The Senior Data Scientist will be responsible for leading complex data science projects and developing innovative solutions that drive business growth and efficiency. This role requires a deep understanding of statistical modeling, machine learning, and advanced analytics, as well as the ability to communicate findings to both technical and non-technical stakeholders.

About
the position
Lead and execute data science projects from conception to deployment, ensuring alignment with business objectives.
Develop and implement advanced statistical models and machine learning algorithms to solve complex business problems.
Analyzing, assessing and evaluation complex data structure and processes, developing viable and effective data models.
Analyze large, complex datasets to extract actionable insights and identify trends and patterns.
Oversee data extraction, transformation, and loading (ETL) processes to ensure data quality and integrity.
Assuming E2E responsibility for strategically aligned data science projects, from taking stock of requirements and advising through to go-live
Collaborate with cross-functional teams to integrate data science solutions into business processes and enhance decision-making.
Design experiments and validate models to ensure robustness and reliability.
Communicate findings and recommendations to stakeholders through clear and compelling visualizations and reports.
Preparing cost cases and techno-economic evaluations
Mentor and provide guidance to junior data scientists and analysts, fostering a culture of learning and growth.
Stay updated on the latest data science techniques and industry trends to continuously improve methodologies and tools.
