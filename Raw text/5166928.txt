Job description, responsibilities and duties
Are you a technology fan impressed by aircrafts and ships, or fascinated by nature and agriculture? Do you want to be part of the energy transition to renewables, or see yourself as a cyber guardian? About the Role
In this pivotal position, you'll drive requirement engineering and quality assurance for our sophisticated data tools and platforms. We're looking for someone with an entrepreneurial spirit, pragmatic hands-on mindset, and an intellectually curious approach to problem-solving.
Key Responsibilities
•	Collaborate with Product Managers to collect, maintain, and understand business requirements
•	Translate complex business terminology into technical language and vice versa
•	Create clear, concise requirements and acceptance criteria that are unambiguous, complete, consistent, and verifiable
•	Master data models and their business context through deep understanding
•	Own the end-to-end data cycle process with accountability and precision
•	Transform raw data into actionable reports and visualizations that meet business goals
•	Analyze data proactively to identify problems, answer key questions, and develop quantitative and qualitative insights
About the Team
If tackling complex challenges across diverse industries excites you, then our innovative team is the perfect fit. We stand at the forefront of creating ingenious solutions through collaborative approaches.
Our team serves as an empowered tech counterpart for business units, responsible for designing and implementing inventive data projects. We develop cutting-edge analytics capabilities and enhance Swiss Re's risk assessment frameworks.
Joining us means learning not just how insurance premiums are modelled and how data analytics enriches business operations, but also improving your stakeholder management skills, agile development practices, and proficiency with tools that drive impactful projects.
