Job description, responsibilities and duties
We are seeking a
QA Automation Engineer
to take a key role in driving our quality assurance strategy. This position will serve as the technical lead within the QA function, responsible for overseeing software testing, ensuring product quality, and guiding the transition from manual to automated testing processes. You will evaluate tools, build automation frameworks, lead CI/CD integration, and set standards that shape the overall quality assurance practice across multiple projects.
Key Responsibilities
Collaborate with product and software teams to define QA parameters.
Lead the QA team in designing, implementing, and improving automated test procedures.
Evaluate and recommend appropriate test automation tools for one or more projects.
Define and build scalable, maintainable test automation frameworks and strategies.
Integrate testing into CI/CD pipelines and oversee continuous test coverage.
Drive QA documentation including test plans, test cases, and reports.
Analyze and resolve quality issues; recommend improvements to QA processes.
Define and track KPIs to monitor product quality and QA effectiveness.
Ensure quality standards are met before deployment and release.
Mentor and support QA Engineers and testers, particularly in automation best practices.
Collaborate with stakeholders to align QA goals with overall product strategy.

Personality requirements and skills
Required Qualifications
Proficiency in English (B2 level or higher); German language skills are a plus.
Strong hands-on experience with at least one object-oriented programming language (Java preferred).
Proven experience in agile environments (SCRUM and/or SAFe).
Expertise in API test automation and UI automation using tools such as Selenium, Selenide, TestNG, Playwright, RestAssured, Hoppscotch.
Familiarity with test reporting and management tools such as Allure and Xray.
Working knowledge of Gradle, Git, and JIRA.
Experience designing and managing GitLab/Jenkins CI/CD pipelines.
