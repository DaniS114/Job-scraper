Job description, responsibilities and duties
Our inspiring and caring environment forms a global community that celebrates diversity and individuality. We encourage you to step beyond your comfort zone, offering resources and flexibility to foster your professional and personal growth, all while valuing your unique contributions.
Apply now for the position of
Solution Engineers
, and you will be working as a member of the customer service organization to implement diagnostic imaging solutions in/for hospitals and clinics of various sizes across the globe.
Tasks and responsibilities:
You will develop and prepare concepts, implementation and process documents to execute innovative Healthcare IT solutions in conjunction with customer projects mainly for our Imaging driven Products (particularly in the areas of Syngo Virtual Cockpit)
You will contribute to define training concepts strategies and workshops to prepare our global implementation engineers and partners for their tasks
You will carry out training sessions, workshops or briefings and advise colleagues as well as internal and external partners on implementing products from our Healthcare IT solution portfolio in customer projects
You will support describing the integration of products from our Healthcare IT solution portfolio with products from other manufacturers
You will support defining, collecting and assessing product requirements for new product versions, mainly in the areas of suitability for implementation, user-friendliness, ease of maintenance and integration, and communicate these to the development department and track their implementation
You will support solution design and the implementation of complex customer projects and help to process support requests from the field during the implementation phase
For the price book you will determine the content and scope of services for implementation
You will assist with planning, implementation, support and documentation for clinical tests on the customer's site and will be responsible for ensuring that structured feedback relating to the quality of products, implementation times and outlay are communicated to product management and R&D
You will contribute to the continuous improvement of implementation processes and methods
Expected to travel up to 6x per year, including trainings, team collaboration meetings in Germany, and visits to customer sites worldwide. Each trip is expected to last approximately one week
Your qualifications and experience:
You have successfully completed a course of studies in IT, medical systems, or a comparable discipline
You can offer relevant professional experience in an IT environment in the field of Healthcare, and have ideally already gained experience of implementing complex IT customer projects
You are very familiar with clinical and radiological workflows, especially for MR and CT modalities.
You are familiar with the modality options offered by SHS
You have knowledge of IT network standards, image signal processing and experience in dealing with cloud technologies, containerization and virtualization
You should also be familiar with requirement engineering & scoping processes
Your attributes and skills:
Cooperation with colleagues and partners in other countries is second nature to you
You are business-fluent in English (German is an advantage) and have very good communication skills. Your analytical and structured way of working is also an asset
You enjoy working in project teams and are familiar with project management methods
