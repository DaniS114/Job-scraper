Job description, responsibilities, and duties
You’ll take ownership of our company’s web infrastructure, leading a small but skilled internal web team while managing cross-functional projects and collaborating with clients on integration solutions.
Responsibilities:
Web Team Leadership
Lead and mentor our in-house team of 3–4 web developers working on LiveAgent.com, PostAffiliatePro.com, and FlowHunt.io
Coordinate tasks, plan sprints, and oversee deliverables using GitHub Projects and Slack
Review code, ensure quality, and drive continuous improvement in development standards
Collaborate closely with marketing, design, and product teams to align on priorities
Monitor and optimize website performance, UX, and SEO consistency
FlowHunt.io Client Consulting & Advisory
Act as a technical consultant for potential and existing FlowHunt.io clients
Understand client needs and design integration or automation solutions using FlowHunt
Advise on API-based integrations, XML feeds, and alternative approaches
Collaborate with FlowHunt’s product and dev teams to deliver tailored client solutions
Support pre-sales activities by explaining integration options and technical feasibility
Web Migration Project Management
Manage the final stage of the company-wide migration of our websites from WordPress to HUGO
Define milestones, manage resources, and track project progress
Oversee template adaptation, content migration, redirects, and SEO retention
Coordinate with developers, designers, and SEO specialists to ensure a seamless transition
Document processes, mitigate risks, and maintain clear stakeholder communication
