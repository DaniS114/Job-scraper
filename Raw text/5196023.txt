Project Description:
The Senior Software Commissioning Engineer position focuses on planning, organizing, and testing complex technical projects involving system integration and client-specific solution development. The engineer will be responsible for leading projects from initial concept through final implementation, including work on software verification of Fortna WES (Warehouse Execution System) products. The position requires frequent travel to client sites (75% of time) and close collaboration with development, testing, and project management teams.
