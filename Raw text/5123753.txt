Information about HUB:
The
HUB DAS (DOM (EOS)
and
API Decoupling Layer)
is responsible for the development of various services in the Magenta Business environment in the Unassisted Sales Channel, Assisted Sales Channel and Magenta Business Collaboration. Specifically, development in the API environment focuses on connecting the side (unassisted sales, assisted sales channel) with the underlying production lines, e.g. SeCe-IT, CRM-T, etc., in the Magenta Business environment.
The HUB is also developing the unified standardize order management platform for the service organization.
