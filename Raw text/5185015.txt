Information about the position
Job description, responsibilities and duties
Are you passionate about transforming complex business needs into elegant technical solutions? Join our innovative team where your Java expertise will help shape the future of our digital landscape! We're looking for a talented problem-solver who can architect and develop robust applications that drive our business forward in this exciting technology-focused role.
About the Role
As our Java Application Engineer, you'll design and implement applications using Java and Spring framework for our Contract Information Hub - the central platform for P&C Treaty and Facultative contract administration. You'll deliver scalable solutions that enable efficient contract information management while collaborating with teams across the organization.
Key Responsibilities
•	Design and develop business and integration applications for the Contract Information Hub, serving both internal users and systems
•	Review and approve pull requests to ensure code quality, consistency, coding standards, and maintainability
•	Guide and mentor development teams on involved technology stacks, sharing your expertise to elevate overall team performance
•	Solve complex problems by leveraging your experience and technical knowledge to optimize software performance
•	Research and implement emerging technologies to continuously improve our Contract Information Hub solutions and stay ahead of industry trends
About the Team
Our team develops, extends and maintains business applications with a primary focus on the Contract Information Hub - Swiss Re's central platform for P&C Treaty and Facultative contract administration. This system serves as one of the key building blocks in our P&C's Hub Architecture. We closely collaborate with other teams around the globe to ensure seamless integration within our ecosystem.
