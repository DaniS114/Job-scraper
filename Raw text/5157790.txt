Place of work

POSITION
DESCRIPTION
Take the next step in your Pega career and drive innovation in enterprise-scale digital banking systems.
As a Senior Pega Developer, you’ll play a key role in designing, enhancing, and maintaining a mission-critical application supporting the client’s Lending Value Chain. Working in an agile setup, you’ll ensure the technical excellence, scalability, and long-term sustainability of a platform central to the client’s Digital Ambition Strategy
WHAT WILL
YOU DO
•	Lead the design and implementation of advanced Pega workflows and system components.
•	Evaluate and refine existing system architecture, ensuring maintainability and performance.
•	Identify and resolve technical gaps, undocumented logic, and system bottlenecks.
•	Drive code quality and standards, overseeing development practices and reviews.
•	Collaborate closely with product owners, architects, and cross-functional teams to align business and technical goals.
•	Integrate with enterprise services through Kafka, REST, and SOAP APIs.
•	Mentor junior developers, promote best practices, and support knowledge transfer across teams.
•	Actively contribute to agile processes and continuous improvement initiatives.
WHAT WE
EXPECT
•	5+ years of professional experience in Pega development or low-code enterprise platforms.
•	Pega Certified Senior System Architect (CSSA) is required; Lead System Architect (LSA) certification is highly valued.
•	Experience with Constellation-based development and enterprise modular reuse patterns is a strong advantage.
•	Hands-on expertise with Kafka, REST API integrations, and SOAP.
•	Proficient in data modeling, XML (XSD, XSLT, XQuery, XPath), and UML.
•	Solid knowledge of BPM concepts, agile delivery, and DevOps practices.
•	Excellent communication and problem-solving skills.
•	Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in IT, business, or a related field.
•	English proficiency (B2–C1); German language is an advantage.
•	A technically driven professional who takes ownership, mentors others, and ensures delivery quality at scale.
