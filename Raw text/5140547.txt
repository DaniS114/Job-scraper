Informácie o pracovnom mieste
Job Opportunity:
Excited by digital technology? Solar Digital Slovakia is looking for an experienced Software Engineering Manager to lead our team in creating innovative digital solutions. You'll oversee the development of advanced software systems, guide talented engineers, and ensure project success from design to deployment.
We value leaders who build strong teams, drive results, and champion continuous learning. In return, we offer excellent benefits and opportunities for professional growth.
Join us and help shape the digital future at Solar Digital Slovakia!
Job Purpose:
The Software Engineering Manager provides leadership in design, development and deployment of Solar Digital's software solutions, systems and/or applications as well as supervising and coordinating multiple engineering teams.
Job Responsibilities:
Provides leadership, and direction to teams to develop business and technical solutions
Evaluate and acquires resources necessary to accomplish work on time
Oversees team and individual performance, manages personnel issues, conducts evaluations, and fosters employee satisfaction
Identifies and addresses staff training and development needs
Coordinates walkthroughs and reviews; ensures conformance to standards and adherence to design specifications
Develops detailed work plans, schedules, project estimates, resource plans, and status reports
Monitors and troubleshoots performance and schedule issues
Facilitates cross-functional collaboration by gathering and analyzing information on how software products and services interconnect with other systems and offerings across the organization
Communicates the current status of software initiatives, outlines delivery options, and shares updates on upcoming product and service enhancements to ensure transparency and alignment
Leads software development using modern tools and structured methodologies to deliver high-quality products aligned with strategy. Oversee product design and architecture, ensuring alignment with requirements, and drives quality assurance through effective testing practices
Follows general policies and guidelines for projects under his/her control to ensure specific management goals are met, and desired results are achieved. Impacts key quality goals including customer satisfaction, continuous improvement, and reoccurring revenue, timeliness, accuracy, efficiency, cost savings, process quality, etc.
Support IT Operations and ensure Information Security standards.
Employee is also responsible for performing other job duties as assigned by Solar Digital management from time to time.
Qualifications:
Position requires a four-year degree from an accredited college or university in a relevant field (such as Computer Science, Technology Management)
10 years or more of software engineering experience and/or hands-on software development team management role
5 years or more of experience leading software development projects and managing cross-functional teams
Strong technical skills in software development, architecture, and design.
Experience working with Agile methodologies and tools.
Excellent communication, leadership, and problem-solving skills.
Fluent in English and Slovak, both written and spoken.
Top candidates will also have:
Experience in public cloud technologies such as AWS or Azure.
Demonstrated leadership on medium or larger projects impacting strategic priorities.
Ability to cope with shifting priorities, demands and timelines through analytical and problem-solving skills.
Ability to effectively prioritize, delegate responsibilities and execute tasks in a high-pressure environment.
Passion for technology and innovation and building high performance teams.
Extensive knowledge of department processes
Detail oriented mentality and excellent analytical and creative problem-solving skills.
