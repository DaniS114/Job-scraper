Information about the position
Job description, responsibilities and duties
Our client is a globally focused fintech company based in Bratislava that aims to transform the way people understand and manage their finances.
Role summary of Product manager
Own a product area from discovery to scaled impact. You’ll align user needs and business goals, prioritize ruthlessly, and ship outcomes with engineering and design.
What you’ll do
Discovery:
Run customer      interviews, map journeys, quantify pain via data.
Strategy &      roadmap:
Define vision, KPIs/OKRs, and a clear sequence.
Execution:
Write PRDs,      refine backlog, manage trade‑offs and scope, and drive quality releases.
Metrics &      experiments:
Define north‑star and guardrail metrics; design A/B tests; share      readouts.
Stakeholder      comms:
Align Sales/Success/Marketing; create crisp updates and launch      plans.
Platform/AI:
Partner on      APIs, permissions, and AI‑powered features with safety and cost      guardrails.
