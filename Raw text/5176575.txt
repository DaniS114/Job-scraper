Information about the position
Job description, responsibilities and duties
As a Fullstack .NET Developer, you’ll play a vital role in delivering high-quality, scalable web solutions in the real estate domain. You'll join a team of skilled professionals working on long-term projects for established Austrian clients. This position provides an opportunity to grow both technically and professionally through mentorship and active involvement in the company’s technical direction.
Key Responsibilities
- Solution Design and Implementation: Design, develop, and implement robust, maintainable solutions using .NET, Angular, AngularJS and TypeScript.
- Database Design: Design and manage database schema and models using Entity Framework, optimizing for performance and scalability.
- Bug Fixing and Code Optimization: Identify, analyze, and resolve issues to improve application performance and user experience.
- Mentorship and Team Support: Mentor colleagues, providing guidance on technical challenges, best practices, and professional growth.
- Technical Participation: Actively contribute to solution design and technical discussions, participating in regular technical meetings to drive project decisions.
- Code Reviews: Perform code reviews to maintain high standards in code quality, extensibility, and scalability, ensuring adherence to industry best practices.

- Competitive salary with 12-14 payments per year.
- Partial home office (40%) to support work-life balance.
- Teambuildings - development conferences, company breakfasts, summer and christmas event, sport events
- The opportunity to contribute to the strategic direction of the company’s product development.
