Job description, responsibilities and duties
We are looking for a
Knowledge Manage
r who will support the IT Services Manager.
You will be responsible for:
Reporting directly to IT Service Manager for SENSE
Maintaining and improving the effective, efficient, and convenient use of information and knowledge across the organization (Ex SENSE Wiki)
Continual Improvement of the Knowledge available for Support Agents and End Users
Knowledge Owners and Trainers within the SENSE Environment
Managing and developing Global Knowledge Management towards Business demand and objectives for the different SENSE Value Streams
Driving Knowledge Management within the SENSE Environment Value Streams
Guiding Knowledge Owners to create high quality KB Articles which are easy to handle by Support Agents and End Users
Identifying and implementing Improvement initiatives to make the KB more friendly for End Users and Agents
Promoting of Knowledge User Feedback
Driving the detection of automation possibilities in combination with the Virtual Agent Topic Flows
Assisting to implement and drive a reduced “Live Agent” contact because of high quality Virtual Agent
Driving high Support Agent Knowledge by high quality trainings and communication
Driving high Support Agent Knowledge by detecting Agent Knowledge gaps
Focusing on Incident Avoidance, Inbound Avoidance and MTtR Reduction
Performing Routine inspections/audits to verify process adherence
Preparing and presenting Management Reports with corresponding improvement actions
Presenting (progressing on) Improvement Actions in customer (internal/external) meetings
