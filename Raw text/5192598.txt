The Kistler Group is a Swiss high-tech company and the global market leader in dynamic measurement technology.
We develop and supply sensors, electronics and software backed up by a full range of services. In Bratislava we develop software for the next generation of IoT devices. Our software helps customers to manufacture higher quality products, to develop cleaner cars and to reduce congestions on the highways. Behind our success stand our passion for technology, our focus on innovation and a work environment that promotes teamwork. You can help us develop vehicles that people can trust and create measurement systems that save lives. Come shape a better future with us.
Ensure the effective implementation of tests and testing strategies in collaboration with Product Owner and other technical roles (e.g. test developers, software developers, product managers etc.)
Design test cases based on requirements and use cases
Create and perform automated and manual tests
Establish and maintain the test environment
Maintain traceability of tests to requirements
Report defects and retest the bug fixes
Helps to ensure that developed project adheres to non-functional requirements by executing tests targeted to these business values (stability, performance, error handling, etc.)
Create documentation required by Corporate Guidelines for testing (test plan, test report, etc.)
Reports test results to the Test Manager informed of depth / coverage of testing (for Quality Assurance purposes)
Provides feedback on quality and consistency of requirements and specifications (testability of requirements, usability of user interface, more accurate effort estimations, etc.)
Assists Product Owner to solve business issues related to testing
Introduction to maXYmos testing for new team members
Create Robot Framework test automation
Maintain Robot Framework test automation

Familiarity with testing and test automation tools (Robot Framework preferred)
Experience with scripting languages (e.g. Python, Bash)

Analytical and systematic working style
