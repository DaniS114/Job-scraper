Information about the position
Job description, responsibilities and duties
We are looking for a Frontend Developer to join our team developing Kistler’s advanced industrial IoT solution. In this role, you will design, develop, and maintain intuitive, high-performance web interfaces that deliver exceptional user experiences. Your main focus will be on frontend development using React and related modern web technologies.
Main Tasks:
•	Design and maintain the frontend architecture and technology stack to meet both functional and non-functional requirements.
•	Evaluate and advise on the feasibility of non-functional requirements (e.g., performance, scalability, maintainability).
•	Estimate effort for backlog items and ensure estimates remain accurate and up to date.
•	Implement and test features according to the Definition of Done.
•	Debug and resolve issues in existing functionality.
•	Ensure continuous integration and maintain build quality within the frontend ecosystem.
•	Collaborate closely with other technical roles to improve development infrastructure and automation.
•	Contribute to quality assurance activities to ensure products meet design and usability expectations.
•	Identify and communicate technical debt, ensuring it is visible and properly prioritized in collaboration with the Product Owner.
•	Promote and apply frontend best practices and coding standards across the team.
•	Validate that the implemented solution is technically ready for production.
•	Support and manage the technical transition of the solution into live operation.
