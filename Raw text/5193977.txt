Solution Designer for Payment

Košice Region, Slovakia, Košice region (Job with occasional home office)

We are looking for a Solution Designer to support our Lynx team. You will play a key role in further developing of services, drive demands into technological solutions and support the business side from IT perspective.
Application
The application is our responsibility - The Magenta Payment System (MPS) is DT'S Payment platform for paying with Paypal, Creditcard, ApplePay, GooglePay etc. Furthermore, we provide additional API to give Customer the ability to prevent pay for outstanding debt and payment APIs to make our functionality available to all touchpoints.
What will you do?
clarify the demands with our customer so that development team can understand what the outcome should be
have full analytical ownership of a demand including time plan, technical agreements with other teams involved in demand
supporting product owner (e.g. creation of EPIC’s)
provide the connection between customer and development team
creation of user stories in Jira and documentation in Confluence wiki
support a software development team focused on building new applications
document and demonstrate solutions using flowcharts, layouts, diagrams and thorough documentation
independently manage the full software development lifecycle from demand to deployment
design integrations between applications and existing systems
collaborate effectively with developers, testers, and other team members
collaborate with security teams to ensure compliance with payment industry standards (e.g., PCI DSS)
You will succeed if you:
hold a Master’s degree in Information Technologies
previous experience as Business Analyst / Product Owner
have knowledge in object-oriented analysis and UML
experience in REST API design
have SQL knowledge, experience with databases
communicate clearly and present ideas with confidence
have strong analytical and communication skills
have structured way of working
have a general overview for IT applications architecture, technologies, environments, and security
are able to work as a team player not as an individual
are transparent and proactive in identifying and preventing potential problems
have agile mindset
are commitment, courage, focus, openness, and respect
have willingness and drive for continuous learning
Requirements Engineering:
Extensive experience in gathering, analyzing, documenting, and managing IT and business requirements, in close collaboration with stakeholders, Product Owners, and development teams.
Skilled in translating business needs into clear and actionable user stories and functional specifications
End-to-End Process Expertise:
Deep understanding of end-to-end business processes, from order capture through fulfillment and service delivery, including support and incident management.
Experience in analyzing and optimizing process flows with cross-functional impact.
Agile & Frameworks:
Extensive hands-on experience with Scrum and agile development practices, including backlog refinement, sprint planning, reviews, and retrospectives.
Proficient in working within scaled agile frameworks such as SAFe or LeSS, with a strong commitment to cross-functional collaboration and continuous improvement.
Comfortable acting as a bridge between business and IT in fast-paced agile settings.
Optional Skills:
experience with creation of test scenarios
knowledge in data security and data privacy (PSA, PSI, SoC)
Tools & Technical Know-how:
Tools: Jira, Confluence, Hoppscotch, Miro
Modeling: BPMN 2.0, UML, Use Case Modeling
Documentation: User Stories, Epics, Acceptance Criteria
Other: Understanding of RESTful APIs, DevOps processes, CI/CD principles
