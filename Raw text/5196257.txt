Information about the position
Job description, responsibilities and duties
We are looking for a Mid-level Network Engineer who will take ownership of the implementation and operation of our network infrastructure. This role is ideal for someone with solid hands-on experience who can work independently, contribute to architecture decisions, and help drive network reliability and security.
Key responsibilities:
Design, configure and maintain LAN/WAN networks across multiple sites and DCs
Manage and optimise network devices (switches, routers, firewalls, Wi-Fi controllers & APs)
Monitor network performance and proactively resolve issues and incidents (L2/L3 support)
Implement and maintain network security (VLANs, ACLs, segmentation, VPN, etc.)
Participate in network projects (upgrades, migrations, new branches/sites, integrations)
Create and maintain up-to-date technical documentation and network diagrams
Collaborate with internal IT teams and external vendors/partners
Provide technical guidance to junior colleagues when needed
​Participate in a regular on-call rotation
for critical incidents outside business hours​
Strong knowledge of:
IP networking with OSI model, TCP/IP
VLAN, STP, DHCP, DNS, VPN (IPsec/SSL)
OSPF – design & configuration (areas, adjacency, LSAs, summarisation, etc.)
BGP – iBGP/eBGP, route recectors, path selection, attributes (Local Preference, MED, AS-Path, communities, etc.)
