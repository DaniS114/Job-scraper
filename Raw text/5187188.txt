Job responsibilities:
Quality Assurance Leadership
Define, maintain, and continuously improve QA strategies and test processes tailored to automotive-grade software
Manage QA activities within our core product, ensuring alignment with business goals and release schedules
Own and drive quality at every stage of the development process- from concept to release
Hands-On Execution & Team Collaboration
Perform manual and automated testing as needed, especially in critical or complex areas
Support and mentor QA engineers; create learning-oriented team culture
Work closely with engineering teams to ensure clear requirements, smooth releases, and quick turnaround on defects
Stakeholder Engagement & Product Understanding
Collaborate with product managers, engineering managers, and business stakeholders to fully understand product requirements and quality expectations
Ensure that QA priorities align with both immediate needs and long-term product goals, especially for OEM partners
Innovation, AI, and Continuous Improvement
Drive continuous improvement initiatives in testing efficiency, coverage, and tooling
Research, propose, and lead the implementation of
AI-driven testing approaches
Stay updated on new tools, trends, and practices in QA and test automation

Brief description of the company
We are Sygic and we have been changing the way people travel since 2004.
We are known for the Sygic GPS Navigation mobile application, the first navigation app on the Appstore and the second on Google Play, but today we are much more! After all these years, we extended, but the focus and start-up mentality remain.
Our users are considered part of our development family. We listen to their suggestions and try to ensure that our product still brings them added value.
As part of the Eurowag Group, which comprises a dynamic portfolio of innovative companies, including us – Sygic but also fireTMS, Inelo, WebEye, Princip, KomTeS, and CVS, we collectively play pivotal roles in creating sustainable financial and technological solutions. Together we work to simplify the life of fleet companies worldwide.
At Eurowag Group, we are committed to creating a diverse and inclusive workplace. We encourage women, men, and individuals of all backgrounds to apply and join our team.
