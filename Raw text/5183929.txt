We’re looking for a
curious and motivated individual who doesn’t settle for “it works” — but always asks why it works.
As part of our
DevOps Academy
, you’ll go through multiple hands-on chapters covering core technologies like Linux, Git, Docker, AWS, and Kubernetes, while working toward certifications as an
AWS Solutions Architect
and
Certified Kubernetes Administrator
. In your first two years, you’ll gain real-world experience across a range of internal and client projects, always with the support of your mentor.
This role is designed for or early-career developers who want to build a strong DevOps foundation and grow into cloud infrastructure professionals.
​
‍Roles and Responsibilities
​
As a Junior DevOps Engineer, you’ll contribute to both internal and customer projects while learning the ropes of modern DevOps practices in our
DevOps
Academy
. Your responsibilities will grow as your skills do, but from the start, you’ll:
Assist in the deployment and configuration of development tools and environments (e.g. CI servers, version control, test automation)
Help manage and maintain cloud infrastructure — primarily in
AWS
, but also in Azure
Collaborate with the team to troubleshoot issues related to infrastructure, deployments, and services
Write and maintain scripts and automation tools to improve deployment and operations
Contribute to real project work — not dummy tasks — with support from senior engineers
Continuously build your understanding of modern DevOps tools, trends, and best practices
