About the role
We are seeking a
Senior Transaction Switch Specialist
, a key role responsible for ensuring the reliability, performance, and continuous enhancement of our transaction-processing applications. In this position, you will take ownership of selected systems within our portfolio, applying your strong technical expertise and deep CMS knowledge to support application teams and business stakeholders.
You will collaborate on — and occasionally lead — both project and operational activities, while continuously advancing your knowledge of new technologies and designing effective application solutions that strengthen our overall platform.
If you are driven by technical excellence, enjoy solving complex challenges, and want to make a real impact in a dynamic environment, we would be excited to meet you.

S/he acts as the technical owner of the application.
Analyses release notes from third parties and vendors.

Designs and implements application improvements, performance, maintenance, high availability modes and other operational models.

Collaborates with developers, analysts and testers within the team/ company and provides support to them through analysis, designing technical test scenarios, etc.

Who we are
Established in 2000 as a division of Tatra banka, RPC is a shared service centre of Raiffeisen Bank International (RBI).
In 2008, we evolved into a separate company, solidifying our position as a strategic hub for centralizing the group's card payment operations with specialization in card issuing, acquiring, fraud risk monitoring, and digital payments.
We bring fresh, innovative payment technologies across the European region, which guarantees our employees to be part of many exciting projects in multiple CEE countries.
Besides Tatra banka in Slovakia and UBB in Bulgaria, we are proud to have among our clients Raiffeisen banks from Austria, Albania, Czech Republic, Croatia, Romania, Hungary, Serbia, Ukraine, and Kosovo, which makes us one of the largest processing companies in the region.
Come be a part of RPC as we shape the future of payment technology! If you're passionate about innovation and want to join a collaborative and international team, we'd love to have you with us.
