Senior SAP CO/MM Consultant
Place of work
Remote work
Contract type

POSITION
DESCRIPTION
We are looking for an experienced SAP Consultant with strong expertise in Controlling (CO) and Materials Management (MM). This role involves implementing, configuring, and supporting SAP CO and MM modules to ensure efficient financial and supply chain processes across the organization.
WHAT WILL
YOU DO
• Configure and maintain SAP CO (Cost Center Accounting, Profit Center Accounting, Internal Orders) and MM (Purchasing, Inventory Management).
• Analyze business requirements and translate them into functional specifications.
• Support integration between CO, MM, and other SAP modules (FI, SD, PP).
• Provide end-user training and documentation.
• Troubleshoot and resolve system issues.
WHAT WE
EXPECT
• Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in IT or a related field
• 5+ years of hands-on SAP CO and MM experience in implementation, rollout, or end-to-end migration projects for enterprise clients
• Strong understanding of financial controlling and procurement processes.
• Experience with configuration, master data management, and reporting.
• Knowledge of integration points between CO, MM, and FI.
• English language proficiency at upper intermediate (B2) or advanced (C1) level.
• Experience with SAP S/4HANA preferred.
• Project implementation or migration experience.
