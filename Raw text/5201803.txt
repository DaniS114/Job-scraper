Job description, responsibilities and duties
Role Overview
We’re looking for a Senior AI Engineer to enable successful implementation of AI solutions. You will design and deploy AI solutions and help integrate AI into products and processes of our clients.
What you will do:
Design and deliver AI solutions based on LLMs (e.g., OpenAI, Gemini), RAG/CAG, and agentic systems.
Develop and maintain backend services for various AI solutions and data processing pipelines for NLP.
Guide teams on practical AI use cases, integration patterns, and best practices.
Collaborate with product and IT teams to embed AI into business workflows and customer-facing solutions.
Run experiments to improve AI performance, scalability, and industry-specific adaptation.
Share knowledge through documentation, demos, and internal community enablement.
What you will bring:
Strong experience with LLMs, retrieval/connected agent systems, and NLP applications.
Solid programming and data engineering skills with common AI frameworks.
Ability to design experiments, optimize models, and deliver working AI solutions in production.
Understanding of enterprise IT environments, ideally Azure Cloud or AWS.
Effective communication and upskilling capability.
English and Sovak or Czech proficiency.
Preferred:
Experience deploying and scaling AI in the cloud.
Automation and workflow integration experience (case/document/data systems).
Proven delivery across diverse business use cases.

Brief description of the company
Who we are
Ableneo is a partner for AI and technology-driven transformation. We combine deep software engineering with business consulting to help organisations implement intelligent systems that deliver measurable results and reduce the risk of failed innovation.
What we do
• AI & Data Enabling. We deploy chatbots and LLM solutions, automate documents and workflows, and work with Computer Vision. Our focus is on solving concrete business problems with AI and achieving a high success rate in real use cases.
• Business Optimisation & Efficiency. We streamline processes, reduce delays, automate operational work, and set up more efficient ways of working.
• Product Design & Development. We design and build digital products, customer journeys, and scalable platforms that support long-term growth.
• Software Engineering. We offer flexible delivery models. From specialised roles to full teams, supporting both fast-growing companies and established enterprises.
Why join us
We have years of experience and strong references across startups, scale-ups, and large organisations. We work on assignments where AI makes real impact. For example, in the banking sector we validated an LLM solution with a high success rate in a real proof-of-concept.
At Ableneo you will work on projects that push the boundaries of AI and software engineering. You will solve complex problems, collaborate with decision-makers, and help build systems that have immediate business value.
