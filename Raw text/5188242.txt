Job description, responsibilities and duties
What will you do?
As the Senior Project Manager, you will oversee the planning, organization and implementation of complex digital projects in international environment. This role requires great communication and organizational skills to collaborate with remote teams & manage end to end projects coordination effectively.
For this role, we are looking for skilled Project Manager with proven record of leading large SW development projects from the beginning to the end.
Your responsibilities will also be:
• Managing the day-to-day activities of projects, including overseeing project responsibilities and schedules.
• Developing contingency plans for potential risks.
• Evaluating and managing deliverables to ensure that service expectations are achievable, developed, and met.
• Directing teams and monitoring project results for significant deviations.
• Ensuring adherence to quality standards and processes.
• Work on complex projects, application systems and infrastructure components, providing problem identification and resolution.
• Independently work on business valuable features.
• Communicate with end users and internal customers to ensure accuracy and integrity and completeness of the product.
• Perform integrated testing and customer acceptance testing of the product.
• Stay up-to-date with the latest trends and advancements in IT development.
• Collaborate with engineers and other departments.
