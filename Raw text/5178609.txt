Information about the position
Job description, responsibilities and duties
About the Role:
As a DevSecOps Engineer, in a central DevSecOps team, you will be responsible to design, assess and implement solutions to address our infrastructure and security requirements for central CI/CD Platform. Platforms and tools we use at Swiss Re include Microsoft Azure, Azure DevOps, Docker, Kubernetes, AquaSec, Terraform, Python, GitHub just to name a few.
About the team:
"DevSecOps Platform" team is part of Group Technology Servies(GTS), in SwissRe. Our team is globally distributed. And our vision is to revolutionize software delivery by empowering developers with cutting-edge solutions, promoting collaboration, agility, and excellence. We aim to elevate the developer experience through innovative approaches that prioritise security, compliance, and seamless integration.
