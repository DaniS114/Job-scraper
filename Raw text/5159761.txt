Full-Stack Developer - Python & JavaScript
Job description, responsibilities and duties
About Us:
At PwC, we empower the world's leading companies by shaping the future of technology consulting. Join our Advanced Technology Solutions team in Bratislava and Košice to work on transformative projects with international reach, offering continuous learning and meaningful impact. We are excited to bring on board motivated
Full-Stack Developers
, fluent in
Python
and
JavaScript
, who are ready to hit the ground running and make a real difference from day one.
As a
Full-Stack Developer
, you won’t just write code; you’ll develop innovative technology-driven solutions that enhance customer experiences and optimize business operations. Your contributions will drive significant results for our clients, and you will collaborate with a dynamic team dedicated to growth and success.
Key Responsibilities:
Collaborate with engineers and business analysts to deliver high-quality enterprise applications.
Design robust APIs and microservices, and create sophisticated software solutions.
Prepare detailed technical specifications throughout project phases.
Integrate third-party applications and data sources to ensure seamless functionality.
Utilize workflow, document and content management, portal systems, and relevant frameworks.
Write clean, efficient code that adheres to enterprise standards.
Support diverse project elements, offering innovative solutions in design patterns and deployments.
Participate actively in scrum meetings, team discussions, and communicate progress effectively.
Qualifications & Requirements:
Experience:
Ready to dive in with a solid foundation in full-stack development, evidenced by practical or academic experience. We are looking for juniors to more experienced members.
Technical Skills:
Proficient in Python, JavaScript, and TypeScript.
Analytical Skills:
Strong analytical mindset and sharp logical thinking.
Education:
Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in IT Engineering, Computer Science, Applied Sciences, or a related field.
Language:
Fluent in English, with a proficiency level of B2 or higher.
Why Join Us:
Engage with prestigious clients and participate in projects that make a lasting impact.
Work in a vibrant, international team environment with opportunities for professional growth.
Be at the forefront of technological innovation, driving change and improving business outcomes.
If you are ready to embrace challenges, drive innovation, and make a lasting impact, this is the perfect opportunity for you. Join us and become a part of a team that is passionate about transforming the future of technology consulting.
