Job description, responsibilities and duties
​Are you ready to contribute to a project that reaches customers worldwide? We are looking for a
Experienced Software Test Engineer
to strengthen our international team and help ensure the highest quality of our software solutions. This is your chance to work with modern technologies, drive innovation, and see your work make a global difference.​
As a
Software Test Engineer
, you will play a key role in ensuring the quality and reliability of our applications and platforms. You will work on both manual and automated testing, with a focus on integration, performance, regression, and acceptance testing. Your contribution will directly influence the success of large-scale projects and the satisfaction of our global clients.
Key responsibilities
Perform testing of new and existing software products (manual and automated)
Manage test cases, defects, and quality assurance tasks independently, including complex system areas
Apply knowledge of regression, integration, performance, and user acceptance testing
Analyze business requirements to design and execute test cases
Document and track defects, ensuring timely resolution through bug tracking tools
Collaborate with product owners to clarify requirements, enhancements, and upgrades
Support end users and internal teams in debugging and verifying software functionality
Plan and perform integration and acceptance testing with focus on quality and timely delivery
Prepare test plans with clear scope, objectives, deliverables, and risk identification
Track and report test metrics to stakeholders
Ensure testing processes are efficient, repeatable, and effective
