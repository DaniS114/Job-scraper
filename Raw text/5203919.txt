About the role
You will join the team of developers and analysts, responsible for integration layer. Their team motto is:
"Every day for us something new, open mind for a different view"
This position is for maternity leave replacement but don't let the temporary nature of the position deter you – at our company, we value talent and dedication, and we always strive to retain exceptional team members!
You will be responsible for following areas:
Analysis of the impact of changes of change requirements for system, specified on business or IT requirements
Implementation of changes using configuration tools
Communication with business analysts, IT departments of our clients (RBI banks) and external vendors of system
Management (analysis, changes, maintenance) of assigned interfaces between RPC and banking systems (online, files)
Collaboration within projects with IT analysis (impact analysis on interfaces, integration with the bank)
Participation on data migration (fields analysis, impacts to interfaces, preparation of migration tools)
Close cooperation with quality assurance team

Who we are
Established in 2000 as a division of Tatra banka, RPC is a shared service centre of Raiffeisen Bank International (RBI).
In 2008, we evolved into a separate company, solidifying our position as a strategic hub for centralizing the group's card payment operations with specialization in card issuing, acquiring, fraud risk monitoring, and digital payments.
We bring fresh, innovative payment technologies across the European region, which guarantees our employees to be part of many exciting projects in multiple CEE countries.
Besides Tatra banka in Slovakia and UBB in Bulgaria, we are proud to have among our clients Raiffeisen banks from Austria, Albania, Czech Republic, Croatia, Romania, Hungary, Serbia, Ukraine, and Kosovo, which makes us one of the largest processing companies in the region.
Come be a part of RPC as we shape the future of payment technology! If you're passionate about innovation and want to join a collaborative and international team, we'd love to have you with us.
