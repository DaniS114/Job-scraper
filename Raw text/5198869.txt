About the team
Ready to shape the future of product selection and make a real commercial impact?
The Perfect Portfolio team is looking for a curious, data-savvy, and forward-thinking professional to help us rethink how we manage and optimize product choices across markets. We operate like a start-up within the company—agile, innovative, and focused on delivering results. If you're passionate about turning insights into action and want to be part of a high-impact journey, we’d love to hear from you.
Your role
Work with teams across the business to improve our product mix—balancing top sellers, new launches, and streamlining less popular items.
Use Alteryx, Excel or other tools to clean and analyze large datasets across markets and channels, supporting key portfolio decisions.
Help build and update Power BI dashboards to track performance and guide strategy—starting small and growing your impact over time.
Support the creation of trade stories, tailored assortments, and in-store execution tools that bring our plans to life.
Your skills
Curious, analytical, and detail-oriented mindset with a proactive attitude and passion for problem-solving
Flexible, resilient, and comfortable working in fast-changing, cross-functional environments, collaborative and team-oriented
Strong communication skills in English (spoken and written)
Degree in Business, Economics, Data Analytics, or a related field
Confident working with data; strong Excel skills, familiarity with Alteryx and Power BI is a plus (or willingness to learn)
Open to junior candidates – we value potential, learning mindset, and initiative
