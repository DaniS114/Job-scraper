Information about the position
Job description, responsibilities and duties
As an IT Project Manager, you will be responsible for delivering complex, business-critical IT projects within Global Blue. You will lead projects from planning to closure, ensuring alignment with stakeholders and adherence to corporate project management standards.
A glimpse into your role. What you will do:
Deliver projects on time, within budget, and to the highest quality standards
Drive project scoping and setup in close collaboration with project owners and stakeholders
Manage and guide internal and external project teams
Maintain clear documentation and provide regular status reports
Resolve project-related conflicts with a solution-oriented approach
Contribute to the continuous improvement of project and portfolio management methodologies
