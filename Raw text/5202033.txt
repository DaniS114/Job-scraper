Information about the position
Job description, responsibilities and duties
Become a part of the team that is closely involved with the most important solutions and processes for Swiss Re Asset Management. We are responsible for owning many of the critical applications in the end-to-end chain, for their proper management, governance and fulfiling the IT security requirements. We are also the gatekeeper for the introduction of new trading products and ensure full e2e testing by coordinating all affected Asset Management teams.
About the Role
In this role you will be responsible for:
•	IT ownership of multiple applications, understand their purpose, the setup and infrastructure
•	Work closely with various internal teams including IT resilience, Governance and IT security to be aware and fully invested in the latest initiatives
•	Manage the roadmap to ensure all defined deliverables are delivered within designated timelines
•	Communicate and negotiate the requirements with the application teams (business owners) to ensure they are understood. The business owners need to be engaged on all topic areas of cybersecurity, disaster recovery, governance, access management etc.
•	Consult application teams and work closely with them to improve application lifecycle processes. This includes the DGF process which is a formal set of steps to introduce new software into Swiss Re.
•	Complete configuration reviews and coordinate penetration testing activities
•	Become an expert on selected front and middle office trading business applications and processes to ensure a good understanding of our E2E processes
•	Ensure KPIs and service level agreements are monitored and kept within desired thresholds
•	Work closely with audit teams, if relevant, for applications in your scope
•	As part of the tasks for new product E2E testing, you will manage the overall coordination and testing for all impacted teams. These new product initiatives are treated as mini projects and you will learn to understand the product, the trading system and all impacted downstream teams, systems and processes.
•	You will also be engaged in the technical onboarding of new investment managers which includes meetings with new managers, setting up the sftp gateway, building new interfaces and testing new incoming and outgoing files.
•	Be prepared to show initiative, to challenge our processes and to suggest improvements to our landscape
About the Team
We are a small, focused team, part of Group Digital & Technology (GDTO) serving the Asset Management domain within Swiss Re. The team is located in Bratislava, collaborating with business and IT teams in numerous locations worldwide including New York, London and Zurich.
