Job description, responsibilities and duties
As a Senior Data Engineer, you'll be the architect behind our company's data foundation. You'll design elegant data models and build robust pipelines that deliver trusted, high-quality data to drive business decisions across the organization. You'll join us at an exciting time as we're currently building and expanding our data team, offering you the opportunity to shape our data culture from the ground up.
Building the core data infrastructure that powers everything from reporting to advanced analytics. Our work brings together data from all systems and applications across the business, creating a unified data ecosystem that enables informed decision-making at all levels. We're currently in an exciting growth phase, expanding our team to meet increasing data demands.
Responsibilities
Lead and design scalable data architectures and pipelines that assemble complex datasets supporting critical business objectives
Contribute to building our growing data team by sharing knowledge and supporting team members
Collaborate with stakeholders to understand data needs and develop innovative ways to leverage data in daily business processes
Share expertise and help establish best practices that drive innovation across our expanding Data Architecture & Engineering team
Build and optimize data models that ensure consistency, accuracy, and accessibility across systems
Partner cross-functionally with Data Architects, Data Engineers, and Data Analysts to deliver cohesive data solutions
Help establish standards for team operations as we grow and foster a culture of continuous improvement
