Náplň práce, právomoci a zodpovednosti
The SAP Operations Lead and IT Support is a senior technical role responsible for managing SAP systems operations and providing comprehensive IT infrastructure support for the Trnava manufacturing plant. This position serves as the bridge between business operations and IT systems, ensuring seamless integration of SAP modules with plant operations while leading IT support activities and team coordination.
SAP OPERATIONS MANAGEMENT (
70%)
System Administration & Optimization
Manage daily SAP operations ensuring 99.5%+ system availability for production environment
Administer SAP modules with focus on MM (Materials Management), PP (Production Planning), QM (Quality Management), and WM (Warehouse Management)
Perform system monitoring, performance tuning, and preventive maintenance activities
Coordinate SAP upgrades, patches, and enhancement implementations with headquarters IT
Manage SAP user accounts, authorizations, and security profiles in compliance with automotive industry standards
Business Process Support
Support production planning, inventory management, and quality control processes through SAP
Collaborate with Production, Quality, Logistics, and Procurement departments to optimize SAP workflows
Design and implement custom reports and dashboards for operational KPIs monitoring
Troubleshoot SAP functional issues and coordinate resolution with SAP functional consultants
Train end-users on SAP best practices and new functionalities
Integration & Data Management
Ensure seamless integration between SAP and MES (Manufacturing Execution System)
Manage interfaces between SAP and third-party systems (EDI, quality systems, warehouse automation)
Monitor data quality, consistency, and integrity across integrated systems
Support digitalization initiatives and Industry 4.0 projects involving SAP connectivity
IT INFRASTRUCTURE & SUPPORT (
25%)
IT Systems Management
Oversee plant IT infrastructure including servers, networks, workstations, and mobile devices
Manage IT service desk operations and coordinate resolution of technical issues
Ensure cybersecurity compliance following IATF 16949 and ISO 27001 guidelines
Maintain IT documentation including network diagrams, procedures, and asset inventory
Coordinate with headquarters IT for infrastructure projects and standardization initiatives
Technical Support & Problem Resolution
Provide Tier 2/3 technical support for complex IT and SAP issues
Manage incident and problem management processes using ITIL framework
Coordinate with external vendors and service providers for specialized support
Implement backup and disaster recovery procedures for critical systems
Support production equipment connectivity and industrial automation systems
IT Security & Compliance
Implement and monitor IT security policies and access controls
Conduct regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
Ensure compliance with automotive industry cybersecurity standards (ISO/SAE 21434) Manage antivirus, firewall, and network security systems
Train employees on IT security awareness and best practices
