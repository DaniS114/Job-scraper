Information about the position
Job description, responsibilities and duties
Are you passionate about transforming compliance through data and innovation? Join our dynamic team where your analytical skills will help shape the future of compliance solutions! This exciting role combines data analytics expertise with AI exploration to create cutting-edge tools that enhance compliance effectiveness across our global organization.
About the Team
You'll join our global Compliance Digital & Analytics team, based in Zürich and Bratislava! The team is responsible for improving the usage of technology and data for the benefit of Compliance across Swiss Re Group and driving Compliance insights for management and key Compliance stakeholders. Our services include business analysis, development and management of Compliance-specific tools and data analytical solutions. The development of digital & analytics capabilities is a key strategic direction for Compliance.
About the role
As a Compliance Data Analyst, you'll be developing solutions that revolutionize how compliance work is performed. You'll have the opportunity to design, enhance, and test digital compliance solutions while exploring innovative ways AI can simplify and improve compliance processes.
Key Responsibilities
•	Develop and maintain Compliance-specific analytics solutions using our advanced data analytics platform
•	Contribute to enhancement and testing of digital Compliance tools and dashboards
•	Prepare, model, and visualize data to support effective monitoring of key Compliance risks
•	Collaborate with colleagues and partners to ensure data quality and alignment
•	Explore, test and implement AI solutions to advance Compliance, ensuring appropriate quality
•	Design and refine prompts, workflows, and prototypes using available AI capabilities
•	Partner with IT and analytics teams to ensure alignment and knowledge sharing
