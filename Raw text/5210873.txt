What will your role in the team be?
Since summer 2023, mobile products at O2 have been managed by agile cross-functional teams made up of colleagues specializing in CX, Processes, UX/UI design, data analytics, marketing communication, solution architecture, IT FE&BE development, and testing. We’re currently looking for an experienced Scrum Masters to join some of these end-to-end product teams.
If you're excited about taking on this role, let us know – and join teams that care:). #pretozenamzalezi
Your responsibilities will include:
Leading and facilitating agile ceremonies
Removing obstacles that hinder the team’s progress
Closely collaborating with the Product Owner on backlog management and task prioritization
Coaching the team in agile principles and supporting self-organization
Ensuring smooth development processes and continuously improving them
Shielding the team from outside disruptions to maintain their focus
Promoting transparent and effective communication within the team
Monitoring and evaluating team performance metrics, and supporting planning
Čo od vás čakáme
Strong knowledge of agile methodologies, especially Scrum
At least 2 years of experience as a Scrum Master
Scrum Master certification is a plus
Experience in people and team development (e.g. Management 3.0, Coaching, Liberating Structures, etc.)
A technical background is a plus
Experiences in launching new teams
Experiences in delivering complex projects is a plus
Ability to lead and motivate a team
Skilled in facilitating improvements and removing impediments
Excellent communication and organizational skills
A team player who fosters transparency and collaboration
Fluent in English, both spoken and writte
