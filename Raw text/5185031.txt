Job description, responsibilities and duties
About the Role
As the Data Architect & Product Owner, you will drive both the technical architecture vision and strategic platform evolution, ensuring business-as-usual operations run smoothly while continuously improving governance, performance, and usability of the data ecosystem.
You will lead the BAU and continuous improvement of the Foundry-based Data Products Platform, balancing architecture stewardship with agile product ownership.
Your responsibilities cover data modeling, platform governance, performance optimization, and enablement.
At the same time, you will act as an Asset Management Domain Data Architect, owning and evolving Asset Management data architecture further.
Curious to learn more? Let’s dive in!
Your key responsibilities:
As a Product Owner
•	Own the roadmap, backlog, and priorities for platform stability, security, and feature evolution
•	Ensure the platform remains aligned with architectural best practices and enterprise standards
•	Balance operational reliability (incident management, monitoring, runbooks) with strategic development (enhancements, automation, and innovation)
•	Prioritize improvements that deliver sustainable business value, such as reusability, maintainability, and governance automation
•	Monitor and optimize platform performance, ensuring critical SLAs (e.g., KEP, KWP) are consistently met
•	Coordinate closely with use-case oriented squads to ensure platform scalability, enablement, and smooth onboarding of new use cases
•	Develop and maintain reusable components, templates, and patterns to accelerate delivery of new data solutions.
•	Create and share best-practice documentation, onboarding materials, and training to uplift data engineering maturity across the organization
As an AM Data Architect
•	Define and maintain data models (structured and unstructured) across the Asset Management landscape (AMADM, Markit EDM, and related schemas)
•	Govern schema, metadata, and lineage standards across Foundry; review and approve model changes for consistency and compliance
•	Define integration patterns, data flow design, and architectural guidelines for cross-domain interoperability
•	Ensure all changes adhere to data governance principles, including classification, retention, and metadata completeness
•	Collaborate with other teams to define and evolve data architecture blueprints, governance frameworks, and performance guidelines
•	Provide architectural oversight for Foundry and other use cases, ensuring solutions adhere to AM data architecture principles and Swiss Re standards
•	Represent the squad in architecture and data governance forums, advocating for standardization, interoperability, and continuous improvement
About the Team
At the heart of Data & Tech Asset Management, Platform Excellence team ensures that Foundry Data Products use-cases run securely, efficiently and in line with enterprise data architecture principles. We ensure that clients get best support with data questions or platform expansion requests.
