Job description, responsibilities and duties
In this role your tasks will include day-to-day operational work but also include project-related work in a multinational environment where you can count on an established team that seeks additional engineering support and expertise. We prioritize agility, platform stability, and a passion for continuous learning, providing exposure to essential financial processes globally.
Responsibilities
- Ensure that systems and infrastructure are safe and secure against cybersecurity threats
- Perform root cause analysis and resolve technical issues
- Work with software developers and software engineers to ensure that end to end development follows established processes and works as intended
Implement and deploy software, updates, fixes, and provide Level 3 technical support to improve customer experience by reducing the occurrence of errors
- Design procedures for system troubleshooting and maintenance
- Create & Operate cloud infrastructure (IaC, performance/cost optimizations,…)

Personality requirements and skills
You possess fluency in English, a results-oriented mindset, and are comfortable with adhering to deadlines, which can sometimes be tight. You demonstrate a willingness to enhance your skills and knowledge concerning the platforms you'll work on, understanding both their technical aspects and their business applications from our partners' perspective.
