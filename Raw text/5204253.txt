Job description, responsibilities and duties
Position Overview
The Software Developer will design, implement, and maintain advanced vision application solutions.
The role requires full-cycle development capabilities: requirements analysis, architecture, coding, testing, deployment, and maintenance.
The developer must be able to deliver solutions comparable in quality and functionality to industry leaders in machine vision.
Key Responsibilities
Develop software for image acquisition, processing, and analysis.
Integrate vision systems with industrial hardware (cameras, sensors, robotics).
Implement algorithms for object detection, recognition, and tracking.
Optimize performance for real-time applications.
Collaborate with hardware engineers and product managers to define requirements.
Maintain documentation and ensure compliance with industry standards.
Provide technical support and troubleshooting for deployed solutions.
