Job description, responsibilities and duties
We are looking for Azure DEV OPS Engineer to extend current remote team providing support within Azure environment. If you are good skilled in Azure DEV OPS, you maybe are the right one for us.
You can work in a combination of : Nitra+Homeoffice/Presov+Homeoffice/remote by agreement
Key Responsibilities:
- You will create value by developing infrastructure as code for new applications or applications migrated to the cloud
- You are working on platform layer
- You will consult and train customers
- You will work in a strictly agile team together with customers
- You are self-driven and able to manage your time
- You create and operate reliable, scalable & sustainable solutions
- You write documentations and keep them up to date
