Job description, responsibilities and duties
Job Qualifications
You’re good at what you do and possess the required experience to prove it. However, equally as important – you have a growth mindset; keen to drive your own personal and professional development. You are customer-focused – someone who prioritizes customer success in their work. And finally, you’re open and borderless – naturally inclusive in how you work with others.
Required Skills and Experience
We are seeking a skilled
SAP Sales & Distribution (SD) Consultant
to join our team. The ideal candidate will bring strong functional expertise in SAP SD processes, a solid understanding of integration with other SAP modules, and hands-on experience with implementation, configuration, and support. You will play a key role in analyzing business requirements, designing solutions, and delivering enhancements to improve our SAP landscape.
Key Responsibilities
Gather business requirements and translate them into functional specifications.
Configure and customize SAP SD modules (Order Management, Billing, Delivery).
Support SAP SD integration with MM, FI/CO, and other modules.
Provide end-user support, resolve incidents, and ensure system stability.
Lead workshops, training sessions, and documentation efforts.
Collaborate with business stakeholders and technical teams for new implementations, rollouts, and upgrades.
Ensure compliance with best practices, testing standards, and project governance.
Qualifications
Bachelor’s degree in Information Technology, Business, or related field.
Minimum 5 years of SAP SD functional experience.
Strong knowledge of SD processes (Order to Cash cycle, pricing, billing, credit management).
Experience in full lifecycle SAP implementations and/or global rollouts.
Excellent communication, problem-solving, and stakeholder management skills.
Preferred Skills
Experience with SAP Fiori applications for SD.
Exposure to SAP CRM, Logistics Execution, or Transportation Management.
Ability to work in Agile/DevOps environments.
