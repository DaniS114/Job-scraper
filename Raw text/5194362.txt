Job description, responsibilities and duties
We are the Microsoft Data Platform Competence Center – leading experts in Microsoft data technologies. Our team has hands-on experience with the full spectrum of modern data solutions including Azure Data Factory, Synapse Analytics, Microsoft Fabric, Power BI, Purview, Azure SQL Database, Databricks, Palantir, and more.
We constantly learn, experiment, and refine our engineering practices, implementing new innovations across the data ecosystem. People are at the core of our success –
that’s why we offer our data engineers up to one month per year dedicated purely to learning and professional growth.
Our team has grown to 40 specialists operating in both
the Czech Republic and Switzerland
.
What you will do as a Senior Power BI Engineer
Design, develop, and deliver scalable, high-performance Power BI datasets and reports, leveraging Databricks (Gold layer) and SQL-based metadata.
Work closely with business stakeholders to translate complex requirements into efficient and maintainable Power BI solutions.
Optimize large-scale data models and refresh pipelines using incremental refresh, partitioning, and query folding.
Maintain and enhance ADF pipelines, Azure Functions, and YAML-based CI/CD processes in Azure DevOps to support automated testing and deployment workflows.
Ensure data quality, security (including RLS), and governance across the Power BI ecosystem.
Collaborate in a cross-functional environment and support teammates in solving analytical and technical challenges.
