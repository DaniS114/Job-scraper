About
We are looking for a Product Owner to join our Scrum team and take responsibility for the economic success of our products. You will maximize the value and quality of the product by managing and prioritizing the Product Backlog, guiding the development team on what to build next. Acting as the main interface between stakeholders and the team, you will ensure the product vision is clear and aligned with customer needs.
