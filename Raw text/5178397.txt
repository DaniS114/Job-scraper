Náplň práce, právomoci a zodpovednosti
We are looking for a skilled
Architect / Senior SAP MM Consultant
to join our dynamic IT team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for designing, modeling, and configuring SAP processes using SAP application stack. You will work closely with customers, IT architects, software engineers and other consultants to deliver state of the art SAP solutions that drive business success. You will specialise in the
metals industry
, gaining deep expertise in SAP solutions while also developing strong business know how.
Key Responsibilities:
1. Solution Design & Architecture:
Design SAP MM-based architecture for procurement and inventory processes tailored to industry-specific requirements.
Develop integration concepts across SAP modules (e.g., SD, PP, FI/CO) and third-party systems.
Lead blueprint and solution design workshops with internal and external stakeholders.
Provide architectural guidance during implementation phases and ensure scalability and maintainability of solutions.
2. Project Implementation & Delivery:
Manage and execute configuration of SAP MM components.
Act as a senior expert and hands-on consultant in SAP S/4HANA and ECC projects.
Support and guide cross-functional teams through end-to-end MM transformations.
Collaborate with ABAP developers on functional specifications and enhancements.
3. Advisory & Continuous Improvement:
Identify opportunities for business process optimization using SAP best practices. portfolio.
Serve as a trusted advisor to clients and contribute to the evolution of the MetalsXP solution portfolio.
Stay informed on the latest SAP innovations (RISE with SAP, SAP BTP, Fiori, etc.).

Inzerujúca spoločnosť
Stručná charakteristika spoločnosti
Saiworx s.r.o. is a successful consulting firm in the heart of Bratislava and a proud member of the MetalsXP Group. We specialize in IT consulting, software development, test automation, and the implementation of advanced SAP solutions. Our expert team designs customized software solutions that target innovative software architecture and development.
Metals Experience GmbH, founded in 2024 in Germany, was formed through a collaboration of experienced entrepreneurs, software architects, and business consultants. With over 1250 years of practical experience in the mining and metals industry combined, our team develops industry-specific solutions that cover the entire value chain of the metal industry. MetalsXP is a leader in master and metadata management and offers state-of-the-art, flexible systems that enable dynamic processes and smooth production flows.
