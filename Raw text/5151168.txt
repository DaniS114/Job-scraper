Information about the position
Job description, responsibilities and duties
Are you passionate about protecting critical systems, uncovering vulnerabilities, and staying ahead of cyber threats?
We are looking for a
Cybersecurity Analyst
who will work at the intersection of
defensive measures, security monitoring, and incident response
. In this role, you’ll be hands-on with vulnerability management, penetration testing, risk assessments, and the design of secure solutions that protect enterprise infrastructure, cloud environments, and applications.
You will collaborate with developers, engineers, and security leaders to create a safer digital environment, while also driving a culture of cybersecurity awareness across the organization.
Job Duties
Plan, implement, monitor, and upgrade security measures to safeguard company data, systems, and networks.
Perform vulnerability assessments, penetration testing, threat modeling, and log analysis to uncover and mitigate risks.
Support the secure software development lifecycle (S-SDLC) by automating security controls and guiding development teams.
Design and implement solutions to reduce cybersecurity risks across infrastructure, cloud services, devices, and applications.
Respond to and investigate security incidents, including root cause analysis and remediation.
Review change management items (RWIs) and ensure compliance with security governance and controls.
Stay up to date with standards and regulatory frameworks (SOC-2, ISO 27001, IEC 62443, ISO 42001).
Promote a “cyber-safe culture” by supporting teams and customers with best practices.
Handle daily reporting, communication, and coordination with stakeholders.
