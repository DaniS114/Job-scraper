Job description, responsibilities and duties
Ready to shape the future of legal tech? Freshfields is launching a new IT hub in Bratislava’s Shared Services Centre (SSC)—and we’re looking for talented professionals to join us. Be part of an innovative, international team working on transformative digital solutions. Build your career with Freshfields and make an impact on a global scale!
In this role, you’ll play a key part in ensuring our IT services are delivered to a high standard, supporting both new service transitions and the enhancement of existing ones. You’ll work closely with Service Managers and cross-functional teams to improve processes, documentation, and customer experience.
What You’ll Do:
Collaborate with Service Managers to ensure seamless IT service delivery across the firm.
Contribute to the design and transition of new services and support ongoing improvements.
Work across ITIL practices (Incident, Change, Problem, etc.) to enhance service quality.
Support wider Enterprise Service Management (ESM) initiatives.
Maintain accurate lifecycle documentation and service catalogue visibility.
Act as an escalation point, ensuring timely communication and resolution.
