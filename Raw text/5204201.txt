Our mission:
As a dynamic, flat and international organization, we shape the future of Deutsche Telekom IT in integrated teams consisting of all relevant competencies – it goes without saying that Telekom customers are also part of these teams.
Our Focus:
Architectural Evolution: We're dedicated to transitioning from monolithic systems to a decoupled API architecture, improving efficiency, reliability, and enabling scalability.
Cloud Transition: Our work involves utilizing top-tier cloud solutions including AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud, as well as our private cloud for sensitive data. We're in the process of migrating from on-premise setups to a more robust, scalable cloud infrastructure.
Cutting-Edge Technology: We stay on the forefront of the tech industry by actively employing modern technologies, fostering an environment that encourages learning and professional growth.
Agile Collaboration: We believe in the power of teamwork and adaptability. By implementing the Scrum methodology and SAFe in our biggest projects, we ensure dynamic collaboration and efficient workflow across all levels of our organization.
Innovation & Value Creation: Our teams are constantly pushing the boundaries, redesigning current solutions to fit contemporary standards, and embarking on greenfield projects with high business potential. Through strategic prototyping, we continuously unearth fresh ideas and cultivate a culture of constant innovation.
What will you do?
We are seeking a talented Medior .NET Developer to join our dynamic team in Košice, Slovakia. As a key member of our development team, you will be responsible for designing, implementing, and maintaining robust .NET applications that drive our business forward.
Develop and maintain scalable, high-performance applications using C# and the .NET framework
Collaborate with cross-functional teams to gather requirements and translate them into technical specifications
Design and implement database structures using SQL Server
Write clean, efficient, and well-documented code
Participate in code reviews and contribute to improving our development practices
Troubleshoot, debug, and optimize application performance
Stay up-to-date with the latest trends and technologies in .NET development
Contribute to knowledge sharing within the team
Participate in Agile ceremonies and contribute to sprint planning and estimation
You will succeed if you:
have Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or a related field
3-5 years of professional experience in .NET development
Strong proficiency in C# and the .NET framework
Experience with ASP.NET Core and web application development
Solid understanding of object-oriented programming principles and design patterns
Proficiency in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
Experience with SQL and database management systems
Familiarity with version control systems, preferably Git
Knowledge of Agile methodologies and software development best practices
Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail
Excellent communication and teamwork abilities
Fluency in English (working language)
Knowledge of Slovak is a plus
Microsoft certifications (e.g., MCSA, MCSD) are advantageous
Tech Stack
We are searching for Fullstack developers with medior backend .NET skills and some frontend skills (Angular is advantage)
Additional preferred knowledge/experience (as an advantage): any testing tool/framework, Kubernetes, Docker, Microservices, Git, cloud technologies (AWS, Azure, Google), DB.
