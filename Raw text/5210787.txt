About the role
We are hiring a junior or senior IT Test Analyst. Do you have experience in testing and want to join an innovative and growing company? Let us know about you!
Designs, maintains and updates test cases (based on analysis of requirements, specifications, knowledge of the systems under test)
Designs test scripts for both manual and automated test cases. Approximately 60% of current TCs are executed manually
Prepares effort estimations for testing requirements based on provided inputs
Participates in the preparation of test data for the efficient execution of tests
Collaborates in the preparation of specific test scenarios and test cases for load testing and other non-functional testing
Collaborates in the analysis of the necessary setup of test environments
Executes and analyzes the results of executed tests, and reports defects found to the person responsible for their resolution
Evaluates acceptance criteria (for the preparation of the acceptance protocol)
Performs post-implementation validations of changes to production systems
Participates in the creation of test plans, test strategy, suggests test improvements
Responsible for testing the application and its regular updates in all phases of testing in accordance with applicable standards
Follows the agreed plan, methodology, standards and chosen test strategy
Maintains all necessary documentation related to TCs and reporting of results.
Provides support to team members and other teams by advising on the resolution of specific problems and the execution of acceptance tests
Actively participates in problem solving in case of production incidents/problems
Participates on internal and external projects
Educates himself/herself in both software and testing techniques

Who we are
Established in 2000 as a division of Tatra banka, RPC is a shared service centre of Raiffeisen Bank International (RBI).
In 2008, we evolved into a separate company, solidifying our position as a strategic hub for centralizing the group's card payment operations with specialization in card issuing, acquiring, fraud risk monitoring, and digital payments.
We bring fresh, innovative payment technologies across the European region, which guarantees our employees to be part of many exciting projects in multiple CEE countries.
Besides Tatra banka in Slovakia and UBB in Bulgaria, we are proud to have among our clients Raiffeisen banks from Austria, Albania, Czech Republic, Croatia, Romania, Hungary, Serbia, Ukraine, and Kosovo, which makes us one of the largest processing companies in the region.
Come be a part of RPC as we shape the future of payment technology! If you're passionate about innovation and want to join a collaborative and international team, we'd love to have you with us.
