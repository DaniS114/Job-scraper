Information about the position
Job description, responsibilities and duties
Join a team of cybersecurity professionals and help Swiss Re to fulfil its mission in making the world more resilient. As the Information Protection Specialist, you'll own the technical solutions to protect Swiss Re's sensitive data, monitor alerts related to data leakage, conduct regular analyses and monitor patterns to identify insider threats. What's more, you'll be working in a hybrid setup, perfectly balancing work from home and the office premises.
About the team
The Security Team is the focal point for all security activities across Swiss Re. We are responsible for cyber security engineering and operations, governance, risk & compliance, operational resilience and corporate security. We define and implement the company's security strategy. As a part of the Security Team, the Cyber Security Operations unit is responsible for ensuring a timely response to cyber security threats and their overall management – ensuring a structured and timely resolution of anomalies, attacks, and incidents affecting our global business is in place.
Key Responsibilities
•	Use Swiss Re's Data Leakage Prevention (DLP) technical solutions to protect sensitive data across the organisation
•	Monitor and investigate alerts generated by the DLP solution
•	Interact and collaborate with stakeholders from different teams, regions and seniority levels to identify root cause and severity
•	Monitor activities/patterns to detect any anomalies from typical behaviour that may suggest insider threats
•	Conduct detailed and independent analyses on alerts generated by DLP solutions
•	Record findings on the DLP tool and ensure timely completion of each alert assigned to you
•	Continuously enhance processes to increase efficiency, reduce effort, and ensure robustness
