Information about the position
Job description, responsibilities and duties
Hello, are you a friendly communicator who enjoys helping others and staying organized? We’re looking for a Customer Support Specialist to be the first point of contact for professional end users with questions about pharmaceutical products. If you love creating positive experiences and working in a collaborative environment, we’d love to hear from you. As our new colleague, you will work on this tasks:
Responding to customer enquiries via email and phone in a friendly, helpful, and professional manner
Listening with empathy and addressing customer needs with care and sensitivity
Documenting all enquiries accurately and ensuring timely follow-up
Coordinating with internal teams and Level 2 Support to resolve complex issues
Maintaining a structured and organized approach to managing communication and follow-ups
