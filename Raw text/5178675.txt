Job description, responsibilities and duties
About the Role
Our teams are at the forefront of delivering innovative solutions to support underwriters in the reinsurance industry and our corporate clients. We develop robust applications and analytical products that address a broad spectrum of topics, including contract wording, pricing, and costing services.
We are seeking results-driven and technically proficient Engineering Managers to lead cross-functional teams comprising backend, frontend, quality assurance, and infrastructure engineers. These teams are responsible for building scalable, cloud-native systems leveraging Java and TypeScript, with a strong emphasis on performance, reliability, and maintainability.
Responsibilities:
· Team Leadership & Culture
· Lead, coach and grow a high-performing engineering team
· Foster a culture of ownership, continuous improvement and innovation
· Facilitate open communication and regular feedback loops within the team
Technical Strategy & Execution
· Guide the team in designing and building cloud-native applications using Java and TypeScript
· Ensure adherence to best practices in microservices architecture, CI/CD, observability, and test automation
· Support architecture reviews, code quality, and design patterns aligned with modern cloud systems
· Stay abreast of industry trends in cloud platforms, frameworks, and AI-supported development tools
Project Delivery & Agile Practices
· Own the end-to-end software delivery lifecycle: planning, development, testing, release and operation
· Collaborate with product managers, architects, and UX designers to refine requirements and priorities
· Apply agile methodologies to deliver incremental value and adaptive planning
Recruiting, Development & Performance
· Hire and onboard top engineering talent
· Set goals, and support growth plans for individual team members
· Perform performance reviews and provide mentorship to foster career development
