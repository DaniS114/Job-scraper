Information about the position
Job description, responsibilities and duties
About the Team and our Scope
Our team, a software house within Swiss Re, develops cloud-native (Azure-based) SaaS/PaaS solutions for external Life & Health insurance clients. Our portfolio includes globally established products and those in growth or incubation stages. We utilize the latest technology and prioritize innovation to stay competitive.
Our technical team comprises professionals with multidisciplinary skills, located in various countries, delivering highly available, multi-regional solutions used worldwide.
About role
As a Quality Engineer specializing in automation, you will take ownership of the automation strategy within one of our product engineering team. You will contribute to the design, implementation, and evolution of advanced automated testing frameworks, driving continuous improvement in test quality and coverage. You will collaborate daily with other QAs, Senior QAs, SW Engineers and DevOps to constantly set the standard for quality and innovation across the organization.
Your responsibilities will be:
•	Ensure high-quality deliverables for our end-clients.
•	Identify, report and troubleshoot defects in collaboration with development teams.
•	Work on development, and continuous improvement of scalable test automation frameworks and suites across multiple product lines under L&H solutions.
•	Integrate test automation tools, including build verification systems, IDE plugins, and linting programs, to optimize testing efficiency and reliability.
•	Write and enforce test automation scripts following defined internal coding standards to ensure consistency and maintainability.
•	Collaborate with DevOps and cloud teams to integrate automation into CI/CD pipelines and ensure infrastructure reliability.
