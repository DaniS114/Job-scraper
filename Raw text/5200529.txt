Information about the position
Job description, responsibilities and duties
We are building multiple frontend applications (user, partner, admin) on top of a Spring Boot backend and are looking for a frontend developer who can create clean, modern, and visually polished interfaces. If you enjoy turning wireframes or your own UI ideas into professional-quality designs and then implementing them, this role is for you.
What you will do
– Develop UI features across several frontend applications
– Create polished, modern components and screens
– Turn wireframes or your own visual concepts into functional, responsive interfaces
– Ensure a consistent look and feel across all applications
– Work closely with backend developers to integrate REST APIs
– Contribute to improving UX and overall UI quality
Employee perks, benefits
You will be joining a small, highly technical team to work on the core platform. The technical foundation is a robust Spring Boot backend that exposes REST APIs. Your primary focus will be creating and maintaining our suite of modern frontend applications. This role offers the freedom to influence UI/UX and shape the look and feel of the entire platform.
What we offer
– Work on multiple connected real-world applications
– Freedom to influence UI/UX and shape the look of the platform
– Collaboration with a small, highly technical backend team
– Long-term project with clear roadmap and modern tech stack
– Flexible working setup
