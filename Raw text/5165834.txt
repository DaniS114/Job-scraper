Job description, responsibilities and duties
Your tasks and responsibilities
:
You will manage the solution architecture and the further development of our Data Cloud in coordination with internal stakeholders from various business and business areas
You will design, develop, implement and manage requirements for the Data Cloud Platform, focusing on AI enablement, data architecture, the data interfaces, as well as security, authorization and data access management
You will develop and automate our cloud environments in terms of security, reliability, monitoring and data supply to optimize operational processes
You will analyze innovative new AI-supported data management components and methods and pilot new solutions by designing and implementing initial proof-of-concepts
You can find out more about this business area at
https://www.siemens-healthineers.com/de/products-services
Your qualifications and experience:
You have a degree in (business) informatics, medical informatics, mathematics or comparable
You have many years of relevant professional experience in the field of cloud services, e.g. Azure, AWS, Google Cloud and have worked in cross-functional teams
We are also happy to welcome applicants who have completed vocational training and further education in connection with additional professional experience in this field
You have very in-depth technical knowledge of database and data warehouse technologies (especially Snowflake or Cloud DWH solutions) as well as data integration, e.g. SAP SLT, Glue, Matillion and other ETL tools, as well as data streaming, e.g. Kafka
You have experience designing, implementing, and supporting scalable data solutions leveraging open lakehouse technologies (storage layer, file formats, compute options), to improve reliability, performance, and interoperability across analytics platforms
You have very in-depth knowledge of corporate data landscapes and architectural building blocks (data sets); Experience with data models from SAP ERP, SAP BI and CRM software is an advantage
You have very good experience in developing solutions with SQL (standard and procedural), scripting languages and data transformation methods (e.g. Python, Airflow, Azure functions)
You have experience in software development with agile working models (DevOps approach)
Your profile and skills:
You are results-oriented and have the ability to take the initiative
They are able to handle multiple parallel tasks and prioritize accordingly to meet deadlines
You will work closely with project managers and development teams to understand technical requirements and develop appropriate solutions
You are a team player, have good communication skills, and can work and build relationships with people from different backgrounds and cultures
You will work and communicate with colleagues in an international environment and have business fluency in English. German knowledge is a nice-to-have asset for communication with German-speaking stakeholder
