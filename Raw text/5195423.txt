Information about the position
Job description, responsibilities and duties
We are seeking a highly skilled and experienced SAS L3 Support Services Specialist to provide advanced technical support, expert guidance, and mentoring in the administration and optimization of SAS environments. This role is critical in ensuring the stability, performance, and security of SAS systems, while also supporting version control through Subversion (SVN) and aligning with client-specific security models.
The ideal candidate will possess deep expertise in SAS server operations, metadata management, system troubleshooting, and performance tuning. You will work closely with client teams to deliver proactive support, resolve complex issues, and ensure seamless integration with third-party systems.
Key Responsibilities
Provide expert-level knowledge transfer, mentoring, and advisory services to clients on SAS system administration
Monitor, start, stop, and manage SAS servers and spawners
Administer and maintain SAS metadata repositories, including regular backups and access control
Use SAS Management Console (SMC) to manage metadata, user permissions, and library configurations
Troubleshoot SAS server issues including connectivity, authentication, and performance bottlenecks
Monitor system resources (CPU, RAM, disk, ports) and optimize server performance
Install and manage SAS software updates, patches, and hotfixes
Support and maintain Subversion (SVN) repositories, including user access, folder structures, and hook routines
Resolve SVN client connectivity issues and assist with operations such as checkout, commit, import/export
Maintain audit and message logs, and perform corrections as needed
Ensure compliance with customer security models and access policies
Support integration of SAS with third-party systems such as databases and web application servers
