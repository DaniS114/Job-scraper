Job description, responsibilities and duties
​We are looking for a Senior Data Engineer (Data Analytics & BI).
This is your role :
Technical analysis and data mapping across systems
Development and optimization of data pipelines (ETL/ELT) in Hadoop, Spark and Scala environments
Design and implementation of distributed data architecture
Working with CI/CD and automated deployment (e.g. UC4), involvement in cloud migrations
Creating test scenarios, executing tests and validating data
Close cooperation with IT and business departments on creating data solutions and reports
Preparing status reports, documentation and outputs for management
