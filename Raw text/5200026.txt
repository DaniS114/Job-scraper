Job description, responsibilities and duties
For our client we are looking for experienced Test Automation Engineer with at least communicative (ideally fluent) German language.
Responsibilities:
- Work either in a team or independently
- Responsibility for all aspects of analysis, test design, and creation of test scenarios for automated tests
- Execution and monitoring of automated test results (script integration and continuous integration support)
- Creation/maintenance/error handling related to automated testing
- Preparation of test data required for automated testing
- Documentation and reporting of automated testing results
- Defining and reporting identified defects
- Close collaboration with the internal development team
- Supporting the client/developers in resolving identified defects
