Job description, responsibilities and duties
We are looking for a skilled software engineer to join a small, agile team delivering solutions for Asset Management. The role involves developing and maintaining Azure-based applications that integrate trading, finance, and risk management systems, as well as building reporting tools to support business processes and regulatory compliance.
Responsibilities are:
- Develop, maintain, and optimize Azure applications and reporting solutions.
- Design scalable, secure, and efficient software components.
- Write and maintain automated tests and documentation.
- Develop Azure Functions using Python and small applications on the Palantir Foundry platform.
- Analyze existing code, troubleshoot issues, and propose improvements.
- Collaborate with business analysts and stakeholders to deliver technical solutions.
- Support a culture of continuous learning and Agile practices.
