Job description, responsibilities and duties
Make a meaningful impact at the intersection of data and sustainability! Join our innovative team where your technical expertise will drive environmental initiatives and help shape a more resilient future.
About the Role
As a Data Engineer in our Product Area "Research & Sustainability," you'll be at the forefront of supporting impactful sustainability initiatives through data-driven solutions. This role offers an excellent opportunity to apply your technical skills to meaningful projects while collaborating with experienced professionals across our global organization.
Key Responsibilities:
- Support and develop data pipelines and products in collaboration with our Lead Engineer
- Write and maintain high-quality, efficient code that meets our technical standards
- Analyze complex datasets to identify patterns and help create valuable insights
- Collaborate effectively with cross-functional teams to ensure product success
- Contribute to innovation in our sustainability-focused technical solutions
About the Team
Our team drives data-driven insights and technological innovation within Group Underwriting. We create tech solutions tailored for sustainability, working in a global environment with colleagues based in Zurich, Bratislava, Bangalore, and beyond. This is an exciting opportunity to learn from experienced professionals and grow in a dynamic, international setting.
