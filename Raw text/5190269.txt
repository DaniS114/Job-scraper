Information about the position
Job description, responsibilities and duties
TECH-ARROW is seeking a dedicated and skilled Service Specialist to join our IT team. The successful candidate will be responsible for providing technical support, troubleshooting, and customer service related to IT services and products. This role requires excellent communication skills, a strong technical background, and a customer focused mindset.
As a Service Specialist, you will play a key role in ensuring the smooth operation of IT systems, responding to service requests, resolving technical issues, and delivering highquality service to both internal and external stakeholders.
Key Responsibilities:
Technical Support: Provide timely and efficient technical assistance to customers and internal teams via phone, email, and other communication channels.
Incident Management: Troubleshoot and resolve hardware, software, and network issues, escalating more complex problems to higher-level, support teams when necessary.
Service Delivery: Assist in the delivery and maintenance of IT services, ensuring that all issues are tracked, documented, and resolved according to Service Level Agreements (SLAs).
Customer Interaction: Act as the first point of contact for customers seeking technical assistance. Provide excellent customer service, ensuring customer satisfaction.
Configuration & Installation: Support the setup, configuration, and installation of IT hardware and software, ensuring that systems are properly integrated and functioning.
System Monitoring: Monitor the health of IT infrastructure, identifying potential issues proactively and working to prevent disruptions.
Training & Support Materials: Create and maintain user guides, FAQs, and other documentation to assist end-users in understanding and troubleshooting basic IT issues.
Collaboration: Work closely with other departments, including IT support, development, and project management teams, to resolve complex problems and deliver improvements to IT services.
