Join our Digital Tribe as a Product Owner and take ownership of end-to-end digital product delivery!
You will work across key platforms:
mobile app
,
webshop
, and
web
. You will be responsible for driving the product vision, prioritizing features, and ensuring seamless delivery that aligns with both local and international business goals. Moreover, this position requires close collaboration with technical HAL and development teams, and cross-country stakeholders to harmonize priorities and deliver high-quality digital experiences.
Key Responsibilities:
Lead the
end-to-end delivery of digital products
(app, webshop, web) by defining, prioritizing, and managing the product backlog in alignment with business strategy and customer needs.
Harmonize delivery priorities
and timelines with Dev/QA/Business teams, ensuring efficient resource allocation and cross-team alignment.
Oversee testing activities, review test results, and
ensure quality standards
are met before product releases.
Collaborate closely with headquarters and teams across multiple countries
to ensure consistency, share best practices, and support regional initiatives.
Act as the primary
point of contact for stakeholders
, representing Slovak Telekom in workshops, forums, and cross-border meetings.
Manage stakeholder expectations
, facilitate transparent communication, and negotiate priorities to balance local and global requirements.
Drive continuous improvement by analyzing
product performance
, gathering feedback, and optimizing user journeys.
Champion
agile methodologies
and foster a collaborative, results-driven culture within the Digital Tribe.
Requirements: Who You Are?
We are looking for a Proven
Product Owner
with experience in agile environments and
digital product delivery
(mobile app, webshop, web), who also has:
Strong
stakeholder management skills
, with the ability to represent local interests in international settings and facilitate cross-country collaboration.
Experience
harmonizing priorities across multiple teams
(HAL, dev, QA) and overseeing testing processes.
Excellent communicator
(in both - English and Slovak language) able to build trust and influence stakeholders at all levels.
Comfortable working in a
fast-paced, multicultural environment
and representing Slovakia in workshops and forums.
Data-driven mindset
with a passion for delivering customer-centric digital solutions.
