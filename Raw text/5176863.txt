Cloud Data Engineer (Mid-Level)
Place of work

POSITION
DESCRIPTION
Join Accenture’s growing Cloud Data Engineering team and build the next generation of cloud-based data and analytics platforms.
As a Cloud Data Engineer, you’ll work in an agile, collaborative environment to design, develop, and deploy modern data solutions that help global clients turn data into real business insights. You’ll be part of a skilled team of engineers, analysts, and testers - learning from experts while contributing to impactful cloud data projects.
WHAT WILL
YOU DO
• Design, code, and test data pipelines and integration components for cloud-based BI and analytics platforms
• Prepare, transform, and optimize data for reporting, machine learning, and advanced analytics use cases
• Build and deploy scalable BI and data solutions using cloud-native services
• Support testing, documentation, and release activities to ensure smooth deployments
• Monitor and tune applications to maintain high performance and reliability
• Collaborate closely with data architects, developers, and business analysts across global teams
WHAT WE
EXPECT
• 2–5 years of hands-on experience as a Data Engineer or Cloud Developer
• Practical experience with at least one major cloud platform — AWS, Azure, or Google Cloud Platform
• Experience with big data systems such as Snowflake, Redshift, Synapse, or BigQuery
• Skilled in one or more ETL tools — e.g. Informatica, Talend, SSIS, Data Factory, Glue
• Working knowledge of SQL and/or Python for data processing
• Strong analytical mindset and good communication skills
• English proficiency (B2 or higher); German language knowledge is a plus
• Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in IT or a related field
Nice to have:
• Experience with reporting or BI tools (Power BI, Qlik, Tableau, Business Objects)
