Position Summary
We are seeking an experienced HRIS Analyst with a strong focus on SAP SuccessFactors to join our HR Technology team. The HRIS Analyst will support, maintain, and optimize our HR systems, ensuring data integrity, process efficiency, and positive user experience. This role requires 5+ years of hands-on experience with SAP SuccessFactors modules, strong problem-solving skills, and the ability to productively collaborate with HR, IT, and business stakeholders to deliver effective HR technology solutions.
Key Responsibilities
Serve as the primary point of contact for SAP SuccessFactors configuration, troubleshooting, and enhancements.
Support and maintain modules such as Employee Central, Recruiting & Recruitment Marketing, Performance & Goals, Compensation, and Learning.
Manage system configurations, permissions, workflows, and role-based access.
Collaborate with HR and business partners to gather requirements and design solutions that improve user experience and process efficiency.
Maintain data integrity through audits, reporting, and analysis.
Develop and maintain dashboards, standard reports, and ad hoc queries to support HR decision-making.
Provide end-user training, system documentation, and support to HR teams and employees.
Partner with IT and external vendors to ensure system updates, patches, and releases are tested and deployed successfully.
Assist in HR system projects, including new module implementations, integrations, and process improvements.
Ensure compliance with data security, privacy, and HR policies.
