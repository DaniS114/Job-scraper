Job description, responsibilities and duties
We are looking for a skilled and experienced M365 Tenant Administrator to join our team in the banking sector. The role involves providing 3rd level support for M365 operations, assisting in onboarding projects, managing the platform, and overseeing tenant administration and license management. Additionally, the position includes handling Reporting Button Requests (Phishing) to ensure the security and compliance of the organization’s M365 environment.
This role requires participation in on-call support 24x7 for one week per month, ensuring system reliability and quick incident resolution.
Key Responsibilities:
Provide 3rd level support for M365 tenant operations.
Assist in onboarding projects and platform management.
Administer and manage M365 tenants, including global configurations and security policies.
Support license management and allocation.
Monitor and manage Reporting Button Requests related to phishing incidents.
Ensure compliance with banking security regulations and best practices.
Collaborate with IT security teams to strengthen M365 security measures.
Participate in on-call support rotation (one week per month).
