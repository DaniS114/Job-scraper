Job description, responsibilities and duties
Purpose and Mission
Your role as Solution Architect for Apriso MES of Dassault is to describe the solution context, transform requirements into specific solution architecture design documents,  design technology solutions and goals for a specific project and/or infrastructural area, in collaboration with Domain Architect and Product/Project Managers,
work with Domain Architect to ensure solution architecture align to program/project design guidelines.
Key Activities and Responsibilities
Hold the MES solution context, collaborate with product/project team closely with solid understanding of MES solution and manufacturing processes, transform the business requirement into solution design, take the lead to deliver together with development team
Take end-to-end ownership to MES product which includes managing the requirement, enterprise architecture, solution design, integration with eco-system, elaborate solutions, validate technology assumptions, evaluate implementation alternatives, to design the high performing & valuable solution to the business with the quality assurance
Follow the technology trends actively, participate or lead the discussion together with cross domain product/project team, enterprise architect, which keeps the MES environment sufficient with dynamic business needs.
Work closely with development team, responsible for the MES development guidelines and quality framework, assess the adoption to ensure the solutions developed to meet operational and service level requirement.

Brief description of the company
Bekaert is the world's largest independent producer and supplier of steel wires and cords, headquartered in Belgium. With over 90 production facilities and an extensive network of sales offices and agencies globally, Bekaert stands as a leader in its industry.
Bekaert in Slovakia
Bekaert has significant operations in Slovakia, with branches located in Sládkovičovo and Hlohovec.
Sládkovičovo Plant. Established in 2000, the Sládkovičovo facility is a greenfield project dedicated primarily to the production of steel cords. The plant employs over 600 workers.
Hlohovec Plant. In 2003, Bekaert acquired the former Drôtovna Hlohovec company to serve as a strategic platform for expansion into Central and Eastern Europe. The Hlohovec plant employs nearly 1,600 individuals.
