Náplň práce a zodpovednosť
Penta Hospitals International is the headquarter of the biggest international chain of hospitals, outpatient clinics and elderly care facilities in the Central and Eastern Europe. The chain comprises 47 hospitals and 57 outpatient clinics, as well as 54 elderly care facilities located in Czechia, Poland and Slovakia. We employ 20 000 employees and continue to grow.
We are seeking a skilled and detail-oriented Data Warehouse (DWH) Analyst with expertise in Apache Spark and Power BI. This role is critical in enabling data-driven clinical and operational decision-making by integrating and analyzing large, complex healthcare datasets from electronic health records (EHRs), enterprise resource planning (ERPs) and billing systems, and other sources.
Key Responsibilities:
• Design and maintain scalable ETL/ELT pipelines using Apache Spark to support data processing.
• Integrate data from multiple clinical and operational systems into a unified Data Warehouse.
• Clean, transform, and standardize structured and unstructured data while ensuring data integrity and quality.
• Collaborate with clinicians, administrators, and other stakeholders to define reporting requirements and develop impactful Power BI dashboards.
• Create data models and visualizations to support patient care analytics, population health, operational efficiency, and regulatory reporting.
• Monitor data flows and perform root-cause analysis on data inconsistencies and failures.
• Document data lineage, definitions, and transformations to support data governance efforts.
• Contribute to the evolution of the organization’s enterprise data strategy and self-service BI initiatives.
