About this position
We are looking for a new addition to our ICT team who will ensure the seamless operation of end-user technology, IT assets, and meeting room systems, including audio-visual and PA systems. The role involves troubleshooting technical issues, supporting meeting room setups, maintaining IT infrastructure, and enhancing user experience through efficient and timely support.
Location
Bratislava, Slovakia
Type of Employment
Full-time
Salary (brutto)
From €2000/month
Reports to
IT Manager
Key Responsibilities:
1. Technical Support and Troubleshooting:
Deliver first and second-level assistance for issues related to hardware, software, and networks.
Address incidents and service requests submitted through ticketing systems, emails.
Record and monitor all reported issues and their resolutions for future reference.
Extract list of EOL devices and replace those devices with new ones.
2. Meeting Room Setup and Maintenance:
Prepare and maintain equipment in meeting rooms, such as projectors, displays, video conferencing tools, and audio systems.
Conduct routine inspections of meeting rooms to verify that all equipment is functioning properly.
Offer immediate support for corporate meetings, video conferences, and events.
Liaise with external vendors for the enhancement, repair, or replacement of meeting room technology.
3. Device Management:
Set up, deploy, and oversee end-user devices (e.g., laptops, desktops, mobile devices).
Manage the lifecycle of IT assets, including acquisition, distribution, and retirement.
Facilitate device upgrades or replacements, ensuring minimal disruption for users.
4. User Onboarding and Offboarding:
Support IT setup for new employee onboarding, including account provisioning, device allocation, and meeting room access.
Oversee the retrieval and reconfiguration of IT and meeting room resources for employees who are leaving.
5. Collaboration and Coordination:
Collaborate with global and regional IT teams for escalations and large-scale initiatives.
Work with vendors to ensure prompt repairs or replacements for IT, PA, and meeting room systems.
Assist cross-functional teams in implementing new IT and meeting room solutions.
Monthly Bill payments and PO creation as per requirement
6. Proactive System Maintenance:
Regularly assess and monitor SCCM, Autopilot, meeting room technology, and PA systems.
Detect recurring problems and suggest improvements to processes or systems.
Ensure that meeting room equipment is updated and compatible with existing IT infrastructure.
7. Training and Documentation:
Provide training or guidance to employees on IT tools, meeting room systems, and PA system usage.
Maintain and update IT and meeting room knowledge base and user documentation.
8. Compliance and Reporting:
Ensure adherence to company IT, PA, and meeting room equipment policies.
Ensure cleanness and organization of IT storage and server room.
Report key metrics related to ticket resolution times, asset status, meeting room usage, and user satisfaction.

Ability to prioritize and deliver service, to agreed service levels, within a diverse and constantly changing technical environment.
