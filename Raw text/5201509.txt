Job description, responsibilities and duties
Business Analyst
Purpose of the Job
The Business Analyst supports the development, delivery, and continuous improvement of data-driven solutions across business units. It bridges data-driven insights with customer-centric strategies, supporting marketing, sales, and digital teams in enhancing customer engagement, retention, and satisfaction. Additionally, this position has strong emphasis on CRM and digital capabilities
Principal Accountabilities
· Conduct data analysis to generate insights for business operations, customer engagement, and digital initiatives.
· Aggregate and analyze customer data from CRM platforms, digital channels, and third-party sources.
· Support the development of acquisition and retention strategies based on data insights.
· Identify gaps in customer experience and propose improvements.
· Assist in segmentation and targeting for marketing campaigns.
· Monitor campaign effectiveness and recommend optimizations.
· Ensure seamless data flow between CRM and other business systems.
· Coordinate with cross-functional teams to align CRM strategies with business goals.
· Present actionable insights to stakeholders and senior leadership.
· Ensure data integrity and accuracy across systems.

Skills and Competencies
