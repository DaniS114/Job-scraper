Information about the position
Job description, responsibilities and duties
As a Software Developer you will play a key role in designing and implementing software that meets business and technical requirements, collaborating closely with internal and external development teams to ensure seamless integration of software and hardware components.
Main Responsibilities
• Work as part of an agile software development team on embedded systems projects.
• Analyze requirements and develop software specifications.
• Design and implement software architecture and applications — primarily in C++ for backend systems and web-based technologies (React preferred) for the frontend.
• Actively contribute to software testing, debugging, and performance optimization.
• Improve system performance and robustness by minimizing technical debt and addressing bugs.
• Participate in software release management, including building and validating release candidates.
• Provide 3rd level support for customers when needed.
• Apply Kistler’s professional software development methodology.
• Support and mentor new team members in the use of technologies, tools, and methods.
• Take ownership of specific modules or components within projects.
• Ensure continuous system integration and deployment processes are maintained.
