Job description, responsibilities and duties
Senior Python Developer – Commercial Insurance IT
Commercial Insurance IT seeks a Senior Python Developer to join our internal Commercial Insurance ‘Group Pricing & Specialist Underwriting Tools’ team.
You will be part of a dynamic team implementing a state-of-the-art open-source Python product as a ‘Global Rating Engine’ where actuarial pricing models and rate tables will be coded in Python enabling multiple underwriter workbenches to integrate with to provide pricing of policies.
You will collaborate in an international team with other developers and architects along with business analysts, testers, and business stakeholders to support the implementation of multiple pricing models for different business and market segments in Commercial Insurance.
Help us build and evolve a scalable, reliable solution that makes a real difference for our customers and colleagues. As a Senior Python Developer, you’ll help to shape the future of our actuarial technology, working together with talented teams and driving innovation.
Your role
As a Senior Python Developer your main responsibilities will involve:
Design, develop, and maintain robust Python ‘rating engines’ for multiple segments of business across Commercial insurance
Propose best practices for Python coding
Collaborate with cross-functional teams to deliver high-quality software solutions
Write clean, efficient, and testable code, with a focus on maintainability and scalability
Mentor junior developers and share knowledge within the team
Drive automation, CI/CD, and DevOps practices in Python projects
Identify commonalities across calculation models and support effective re-use of modules
Troubleshoot and resolve complex technical issues
Stay up-to-date with the latest Python frameworks, tools, and trends
Within the project development team suggest and share ideas, align with other developers, document methods of coding for specific topics to ensure transparency
Participate in ongoing unit testing and subsequent UAT (user acceptance testing) ensuring bugs are identified and correctly fixed within the project framework

Brief description of the company
Zurich is a leading multi-line insurer that serves its customers in global and local markets. With about 60,000 employees it provides a wide range of property and casualty, life insurance products and services in more than 200 countries and territories. Zurich’s customers include individuals, small businesses, and mid-sized and large companies, as well as multinational corporations.
Zurich Slovakia is a Competence Center based in Bratislava and Kosice providing highly specialized services for Zurich's headquarter in Switzerland and all Zurich Business units around the globe. Our services are focusing on all types of actuarial services – Life and Non-life, pricing and reserving, on data science and engineering, risk management, business intelligence, information technology, software asset management as well as insurance network management and technical accounting. Our Competence Center employs around 400+ staff from 20+ different countries.
