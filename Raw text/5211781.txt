Ciklum

Middle JavaScript Engineer

We are a custom product engineering company that supports both multinational organizations and scaling startups to solve their most complex business challenges. About the role:
As a Middle JavaScript Engineer, become a part of a cross-functional development team engineering experiences of tomorrow.
The project focuses on the rollout of a large-scale Manufacturing Digital Platform across multiple business units within an international enterprise. The goal is to standardize digital processes, improve data visibility, and enhance operational efficiency in manufacturing environments. We are looking for experienced and proactive developers to join the nearshore team and contribute to the platform rollout and continuous development.
Responsibilities:
Develop and maintain backend components in TypeScript, supporting platform expansion and new feature delivery
Collaborate with distributed teams across Europe to ensure seamless integration and delivery
Manage and refine user stories and backlog items, ensuring timely and high-quality implementation
Communicate effectively with stakeholders to clarify requirements, report progress, and address issues
Participate in sprint planning, code reviews, and cross-team collaboration sessions
Contribute to system reliability, scalability, and performance improvements
Requirements:
3+ years of experience as a JavaScript/TypeScript Developer
Strong hands-on experience with TypeScript (Back End)
Proficiency with AWS services and cloud-based architectures
Experience working with Kubernetes and containerized deployments
Excellent communication and stakeholder management skills
Proven ability to work effectively in distributed, cross-functional teams
Must be based in Europe for close collaboration with the core team
Desirable:
Experience in large-scale system rollouts or digital transformation projects
Familiarity with CI/CD pipelines, infrastructure as code, or DevOps practices
Experience with monitoring and observability tools (e.g., Grafana, Prometheus)
