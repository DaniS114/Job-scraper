How We Work
We don’t do sprints or ticket factories. We deliver whatever we deem best value/effort, release biweekly (for now), and constantly work to shorten feedback loops. Developers write code, tests, and deploy. Each team has one QA and one DevOps - no handovers, no walls to throw things over.
Everyone is expected to contribute to
