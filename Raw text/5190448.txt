POSITION
DESCRIPTION
Successful candidates will support client engagements Project Management Officer functions through consistent, repeatable processes. Candidates will gain exposure to most of Project Control Services offerings and will help the project manager to administer and operate within different project management areas.
Are you ready for the next step in your career as a Junior Project Reporting Specialist with German Language? Choose Accenture and make delivering innovative work part of your extraordinary career!
WHAT WILL
YOU DO
• Analyze and interpret data and present information to support sound business decisions
• Preparing regular and ad-hoc reports
• Communicating with the project team and project counterpart, usually a Project Manager
• Project workplan management support (time actuals update)
• Tracking project finances and preparing inputs for invoice generation
• Covering project resource management
• Onboarding support
WHAT WE
EXPECT
• You are able to work in an independent, self-directed manner and to perform equally as well as part of a team
• Good MS Office skills (Excel, Word, PowerPoint)
• Excellent oral and written communication
• Focusing on details, logical reasoning
• Interested in self-development
• Knowledge of English AND German language proficiency at Upper intermediate (B2) or advanced (C1) level AND Slovak language at Mastery level (C2)
• Master or Bachelor Degree
