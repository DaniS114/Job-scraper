Job description, responsibilities and duties
Ready to shape the future of legal tech? Freshfields is launching a new IT hub in Bratislava’s Shared Services Centre (SSC)—and we’re looking for talented professionals to join us. Be part of an innovative, international team working on transformative digital solutions. Build your career with Freshfields and make an impact on a global scale!
We’re looking for a skilled and forward-thinking Full Stack Developer to join our Application Platforms team. This is a key role in shaping and supporting our Document Management System, where you’ll collaborate with product specialists, analysts, and stakeholders to deliver secure, scalable, and user-focused solutions aligned with our technology roadmap.
What you’ll do
*Design, develop and maintain high-performing applications and integrations, including iManage Work v10, Intapp, CAM and custom-built solutions
*Work closely with support teams to resolve complex issues and contribute to incident management
*Participate in solution architecture, testing, and deployment to ensure robust and maintainable systems
*Identify opportunities for automation and continuous improvement
*Share knowledge and support team development while learning from technical leads and peers
*Engage in project planning and delivery, ensuring quality and timelines are met
