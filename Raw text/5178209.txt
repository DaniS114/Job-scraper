Job description, responsibilities and duties
We are looking for OpenShift Administrator extend current remote team providing support within OpenShift environment.
You can work in a combination of : Nitra+Homeoffice/Presov+Homeoffice/remote by agreement
Key Responsibilities:
• Installation, configuration, and operation of locally deployed OpenShift environments
• Management and maintenance of the underlying hardware infrastructure
• Development and implementation of automation solutions to optimize operational processes
• Implementation and support of monitoring and logging solutions for clusters and applications
• Setup and maintenance of GitOps processes for continuous delivery and infrastructure management
• Troubleshooting and issue resolution during ongoing operations
• Participation in 24/7 support to ensure smooth platform operation
• Collaboration with application teams to ensure a stable and high-performing platform
Personality requirements and skills
You must have:
• In-depth experience in administering Kubernetes, ideally OpenShift
• Experience in installing and operating container platforms
• Strong knowledge of Linux administration and hardware management
• Experience with automation tools
• Experience with GitOps tools and processes (e.g., ArgoCD)
• Knowledge in monitoring solutions (e.g., Prometheus)
• Willingness to participate in 24/7 on-call support
• Analytical thinking, structured working style, and team spirit
