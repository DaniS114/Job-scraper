Information about the position
Job description, responsibilities and duties
Do you love building attractive, responsive, and purposeful business applications using web technologies? We are looking for a skilled Angular developer who understands how to write clear and maintainable code but also has a solid understanding of architectural best practices, modern frameworks, and the importance of creating intuitive user experiences that make a real impact.
About the Role
As a Frontend Engineer, you'll collaborate with cross-functional teams to create exceptional user experiences that power our insurance platforms from end to end.
Key Responsibilities
-Develop and review code that meets our high standards for quality and maintainability
-Debug TypeScript, CSS, and HTML exceptions and profile performance issues
-Integrate REST APIs in a secure and typesafe way
-Shape architecture by contributing to key decisions and documentation that guide our technical direction
-Stay current with emerging technologies and Generative AI developments to continuously improve our development efficiency
-Collaborate closely with user experience experts, product owners, and requirements engineers
-Build end-to-end user engagement platforms spanning the entire insurance value chain
About the Team
We are a team that believes in engineering excellence and that our leaders should also be engineers themselves. We build applications that are carefully designed, thoughtfully implemented, and surpass the expectations of our users by working hand-in-hand with product owners. Quality and stability are first-class deliverables in everything we do, and we lead by example by embedding high standards into our processes.

Personality requirements and skills
You're a naturally curious and thoughtful professional who thrives in a high-performance engineering environment. Your passion for coding is matched by your commitment to delivering business value.
