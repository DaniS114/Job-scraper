Job description, responsibilities and duties
Our client is a globally focused fintech company based in Bratislava that aims to transform the way people understand and manage their finances.
Role summary of Financial Quant analyst
Research, backtest, and implement financial systematic strategies and risk models. Work with large, noisy datasets to produce production‑ready alphas and tools.
What you’ll do
Source and      clean market/alt data; build research pipelines.
Develop,      backtest, and evaluate strategies; manage overfitting and multiple‑testing.
Build risk and      portfolio construction components (e.g., factor models, optimization).
Partner with      engineering to productionize signals; monitor live performance.
Write clear      research reports and present results.
Employee perks, benefits
Opportunity to be at the very beginning of a new international fintech project
Direct influence on strategic and technical decisions
Collaboration with experienced professionals from the global finance and AI sectors
