Information about the position
Job description, responsibilities and duties
Join our innovative team where you'll collaborate with trusted professionals to drive innovation across public and private cloud environments through automation. In this pivotal role, you'll contribute to building secure, stable platforms with a core focus on self-service capabilities for Swiss Re's business units.
As a Lead Java Application Engineer within our Database as a Service squad, you'll spearhead the development of intelligent self-service solutions for database users while providing technical leadership for future development initiatives. Your expertise will shape how our users interact with database services, making complex database operations accessible and efficient.

Personality requirements and skills
We're seeking a curious, quality-focused professional to join our dynamic cross-functional Database team spanning Bratislava and Zürich. You'll thrive in our collaborative environment if you're passionate about multi-cloud technologies and building innovative tools. Don't worry about meeting 100% of our requirements—we value potential and enthusiasm just as much as experience.
We are looking for candidates who meet these requirements:
- Strong Java expertise including Spring, Spring Boot, and related libraries
- RESTful API development experience and implementation knowledge
- Automated testing skills with unit and integration testing frameworks
- Technical proficiency with Git, Maven, OpenAPI, and Docker
- CI/CD pipeline experience and Java best practices implementation
- Problem-solving abilities with attention to detail and solution-oriented mindset
- Collaborative approach to working across cultures and geographies
- English language proficiency for effective communication
These are additional nice to haves:
- Experience with Postgres and Oracle databases
- Java and/or Microsoft Azure certifications
- Knowledge of Agile and DevOps methodologies
- Interest in machine learning and statistical modeling
- Strong customer orientation with ability to anticipate needs
- Flexible approach to hybrid working arrangements
