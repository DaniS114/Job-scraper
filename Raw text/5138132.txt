About the Role
We are seeking a dedicated and detail-oriented Business Analyst to join our team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for analyzing reporting needs, identifying areas for improvement, and implementing solutions to enhance efficiency and effectiveness. This role requires strong analytical skills, excellent communication abilities, and a collaborative mindset.
The Business Analyst focus is on requirements analysis, design & build of the reports, regulatory disclosures and other analytical tasks. The ideal candidate will leverage their investment expertise, as the position will interact with various finance (like GIR and Asset Management) and gain a deep understanding of the Finance Investment reporting processes. In addition, the candidate will be responsible for on-going reporting, accuracy, quality and ensuring the integrity of the standard reporting product.
You will enable and implement the Investment Finance Reporting vision and ensure best practice standards are met. You will translate requirements for Reporting acting as a conduit between the teams and the implementation service delivery partners.
Accountabilities will include:
•    Ensure that data and attributes used by reporting systems are consistent and reliable delivering accurate information required by stakeholders
•    Perform analysis and documentation of FPSL subledger HANA tables
•    Write technical specifications of required reporting views to support report development •    Effectively communicate requirements to application developer
•    Liaise and coordinate with the team members to make sure all implementations follow guiding principles, aligned with the target architecture and standardization concept.
•    Execute functional testing of reporting views and reports to ensure all requirements are implemented properly
•    Define and ensure consistent reporting capabilities based on SAP S/4HANA Financial Products Subledger and Analysis for Office (AfO), as well as other Reporting Tools.
•    Perform root cause analysis on identified defects and resolve accordingly
•    Ensure best practice methods are adhered to, reports are well documented, training and support documentation are provided.
•    Ensure communication within the team and across the organization.
About the Team
Subledger Reporting team has ownership of investment reporting solution and is responsible for defining business requirements and provide operational support.
The team will be newly centralized in Bratislava. working within the Finance Reporting Solutions product area to deliver performant, scalable, and maintainable solutions that enable our stakeholders to quickly extract knowledge from large and disparate financial datasets. Our solutions support financial reconciliation, steering and auditing based primarily from subledger data on the SAP S/4 HANA and Microsoft Azure Data and Palantir Foundry platforms.
You will act as a business analyst within this team, with end-to-end responsibility, such as development & maintenance as well as operations along the full life cycle, for our team's solutions. This includes the design and implementation of functionalities that enable fast and reliable reporting of data. Specifically, you will need to understand the standard use of SAP S/4 HANA and other platforms for analytical purposes, collaborating with customers as well as contributing to and fostering best practices and standards for your area of expertise.
