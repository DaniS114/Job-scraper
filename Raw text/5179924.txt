Job description, responsibilities and duties
We are looking for an experienced
UiPath Cloud Architect / Engineer
to join our
sister company Millennium
, where you will lead the transition from on-prem Orchestrator to
UiPath Automation Cloud
and help deliver the next generation of
AI-powered automations
.
You will design, implement, and operate a secure, observable, and cost-efficient cloud platform, while coaching developers on best practices for modern, cloud-first automation.
This is a hands-on technical role that combines
architecture, delivery, and enablement
— ideal for someone who enjoys both strategic design and practical implementation.
Your mission:
Lead the migration from on-prem Orchestrator to Automation Cloud – planning, testing, PROD cutover, rollback, and post-go-live audit.
Define the tenant & access model (folders, accounts, RBAC, SSO) with strong security and least-privilege principles.
Build monitoring and observability using UiPath Insights and Orchestrator dashboards (SLOs, alerts, reporting).
Implement CI/CD pipelines for packages and configurations (Git-based).
Deliver AI Agents built in UiPath Agent Builder under AI Trust Layer governance (policies, model evaluation, and traceability).
Integrate systems using UiPath Integration Service and secure connection management.
Coach developers on cloud-native automation patterns (queue-centric design, long-running workflows, human-in-the-loop).
Employee perks, benefits
Participation in high-impact automation and AI projects with enterprise clients.
Opportunity to shape the UiPath Cloud & AI automation landscape within Soitron Group.
Work in a modern environment that combines the stability of a large IT integrator with the agility of a family-oriented culture.
Motivating financial package based on experience and expertise.
