Information about the position
Job description, responsibilities and duties
Are you ready to shape the future of actuarial technology at a global reinsurance leader? Join Swiss Re as an Engineering Director and leverage your technical expertise to drive innovation that makes the world more resilient. This is your opportunity to lead a talented engineering team in developing cutting-edge solutions that transform how actuarial reserving functions across the organization.
About the Role
As Engineering Director in Finance Data and Technology (D&T) Actuarial Reserving, you'll be at the intersection of technology and business, co-leading a vital function that powers Swiss Re's financial backbone. You'll drive architectural excellence, foster innovation, and build high performing engineering teams that deliver mission-critical solutions for actuarial professionals across the globe.
Key Responsibilities
•	Strategic Leadership: Define and execute the technology strategy for your domain, aligned with business goals and Swiss Re's standards
•	Team Development: Lead and mentor Engineering Managers, overseeing people management, performance feedback, and development planning
•	Architectural Excellence: Analyze high-level business requirements, make key architectural decisions, and establish technical standards across teams
•	Quality Assurance: Be accountable for non-functional aspects such as performance, reliability, and security
•	Resource Optimization: Oversee resource planning and cloud cost management while continuously optimizing actuarial reserving system landscapes
•	Cross-functional Collaboration: Work seamlessly with actuaries, Product & Delivery Directors, and operation teams to ensure business needs are met
•	Governance & Compliance: Ensure adherence to development best practices, internal standards, and regulatory requirements
•	Innovation: Drive continuous improvement in engineering practices, tooling, and delivery pipelines
About the Team
You'll be joining the actuarial reserving unit which consists of two major teams (P&C and L&H) with approximately 90 internal and external team members. These teams have full responsibility (design, build, run) for all landscapes in P&C RE and L&H RE reserving. In this role, you will co lead the unit together with the product & delivery director, ensuring seamless collaboration between engineering and product functions.

Personality requirements and skills
You're a technology leader who thrives in complex environments and excels at bridging the gap between business needs and technical solutions. You combine deep engineering expertise with strong leadership skills and have a passion for building high-performing teams that deliver exceptional results.
We are looking for candidates who meet these requirements:
