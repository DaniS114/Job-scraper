Job description, responsibilities and duties
We are now looking for
Medior Frontend Developer
to join the Moxymind team to work on a long term projects for for fleet management platform, serving the construction and heavy machinery industry.
In this role, you will primarily work on HTML/CSS development with a strong focus on email templates, UI components, and cross-platform rendering. You will collaborate with backend and QA teams, implement frontend improvements, and contribute to automated testing using Cucumber. Experience with Java is welcome but not required.
As a Medior Frontend Developer with us you will:
Develop, maintain, and optimize responsive HTML/CSS components and email templates.
Ensure consistent rendering across email clients and platforms using tools such as Litmus.
Collaborate with backend engineers to integrate and test email services (each email service has its own dedicated service).
Participate in writing and executing Cucumber test scenarios.
Contribute to UI improvements, accessibility, and performance optimization within the ecosystem.
Work within agile team and participate in planning, daily stand-ups, and review sessions.

Personality requirements and skills
What we expect from you:
Have 2–4 years of experience in frontend development with strong hands-on skills in HTML5 and CSS3 (must-have).
Understand email development specifics, including cross-client compatibility and rendering constraints (Litmus experience is a strong plus).
Have basic knowledge of Java or willingness to learn it for better collaboration on the project.
Have experience with automated testing frameworks, ideally Cucumber.
Be comfortable working in agile teams and communicating in English in a distributed environment.
