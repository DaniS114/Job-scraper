Job description, responsibilities and duties
For our client we are looking for medior or senior Java Engineer who will be designing new and maintaining existing business apps. This role  combines architecture and design work beside business SW development.
Medior:
Develop and implement business applications and integration solutions based on technical specifications
Participate in code reviews to ensure quality, consistency, and adherence to coding standards
Collaborate with senior engineers to understand technology stacks and improve your technical expertise
Troubleshoot and resolve technical issues by applying your knowledge and working with the team
Learn and apply new technologies and frameworks to enhance our applications
Senior:
Design and develop business and integration applications serving both internal and external users and systems
Review and approve pull requests to ensure code quality, consistency, coding standards, and maintainability
Guide and mentor development teams on involved technology stacks, sharing your expertise to elevate overall team performance
Solve complex problems by leveraging your experience and technical knowledge to optimize software performance
Research and implement emerging technologies to continuously improve our solutions and stay ahead of industry trends
