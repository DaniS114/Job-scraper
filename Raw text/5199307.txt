Job description, responsibilities and duties
We are looking for a Senior Network & Security Infrastructure Engineer to support and enhance our enterprise network environment. In this role, you will manage firewalls (Fortinet/Cisco/Palo Alto), SAN (Cisco MDS), DWDM optical systems, and core network components, ensuring high availability, security, and compliance with SLA parameters.
What You’ll Do:
• Monitor and manage network, security, SAN, and DWDM infrastructure
• Respond to incidents (A+/A/B/C) and lead troubleshooting and recovery
• Administer firewalls, WAF, email security, and IPS/IDS systems
• Support SAN fabrics, FC zoning, and optical transport (ROADM, XPonder, OSNR/BER)
• Perform patching, upgrades, and configuration hardening
• Participate in network architecture, optimization, and capacity planning
• Integrate monitoring tools (Zabbix/PRTG/SNMP/Syslog)
• Maintain documentation (NetBox) and produce SLA reports
• Deliver knowledge transfer to internal IT teams
