The Opportunity
As a System Support Analyst, you will play a vital role in supporting the operation, maintenance, and troubleshooting of business-critical systems and applications used within Takeda’s pharmaceutical and regulated environments (such as software as a medical device, SAMD). You’ll work closely with cross-functional teams, including IT, quality assurance, compliance, and business stakeholders, to ensure reliable and compliant application support for users in a highly regulated industry. This position offers an excellent opportunity to develop your skills and experience in system support, regulated environments, and pharmaceutical IT while growing your career.
Key Responsibilities
Application & System Support
- Provide level 1 and 2 support for pharmaceutical and business systems, including monitoring, troubleshooting, and resolving user-reported issues.
- Assist with onboarding and user account management for validated systems and GxP applications.
- Document, escalate, track incidents and service requests to resolution, ensuring proper handover to specialist teams when required.
Issue Resolution & Incident Management
- Respond promptly to support tickets, logs, and alerts per established SLAs.
- Diagnose and resolve technical problems related to application performance, access, or workflow disruptions.
- Maintain accurate records of support activities and resolutions in the service management system.
Compliance & Documentation
- Operate with a strong awareness of GxP, validation, and data integrity principles required in a pharmaceutical environment.
- Follow documented operating procedures, work instructions, and change control processes.
- Contribute to knowledge base articles, user guides, and training materials.
Continuous Improvement & Collaboration
- Provide feedback to improve user experience, efficiency, and reliability of supported systems.
- Participate in quality assurance, patching, and periodic validation testing activities.
- Collaborate closely with IT, QA, and Compliance teams to maintain a culture of continuous improvement and regulatory compliance.

Personality requirements and skills
Required
- Bachelor’s degree in information technology, computer science, life sciences, or equivalent work experience.
- At least 1 to 2 years of experience in application or system support.
- Familiarity with IT service management (ITSM), ticketing systems (e.g., ServiceNow, Jira), and structured troubleshooting.
- Basic understanding of pharmaceutical, healthcare, or regulated environments, with a willingness to learn more about GxP, compliance, and data integrity.
- Strong oral and written communication skills in English.
- Customer-focused approach with strong problem-solving and interpersonal skills.
- Ability to follow processes, document work, and escalate issues as needed.
Preferred:
- Previous experience supporting validated or GxP applications.
- Understanding of software/application validation and change control.
- Experience working with Windows operating systems and basic networking concepts.
- Familiarity with user account administration (Active Directory or similar).
- Ability to read and interpret technical/user documentation.
- Interest in the pharmaceutical or healthcare sector and eagerness to grow within a regulated industry.
- Experience working with application life cycle management (ALM) systems to support software or system processes.
- Experience working in a fast-paced, multi-site environment with remote support capabilities.
