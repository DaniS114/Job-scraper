About Us
Actusmart
is an innovative technology company based in Slovakia, specializing in the development of intelligent software solutions for electric actuators and automation systems.
We combine embedded software, edge computing, and AI technologies to create smarter, more connected actuator systems.
To support our growing projects and new software platform developments, we are looking for a motivated
Software Engineer – Embedded, Edge & AI Systems
to join our team.
Your Tasks
As a
Software Engineer
, you will contribute to the further development of our actuator software ecosystem — from firmware to intelligent tools and edge platforms.
Develop and maintain cross-platform tools for
parameterization, diagnostics, and troubleshooting
(desktop, web, and mobile).
Contribute to
edge device and gateway solutions
, bridging the OT and IT worlds for connected actuator systems.
Improve and expand
industrial communication and IoT connectivity
, including protocols such as MQTT and IO-Link.
Support ongoing
firmware maintenance and development
for our actuator controllers (e.g., transition from Logik 2 to Logik 3).
Collaborate with hardware, software, and AI teams to integrate smart functions and predictive capabilities.
Participate in design reviews, testing, and continuous improvement of our software platforms.
Your Profile
Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, or a related field.
At least
2 years of experience
in software development for embedded or automation systems.
Strong programming skills in
C
; additional experience in C++, .NET, or mobile/web technologies is a plus.
Familiarity with
industrial communication protocols
,
edge computing
, and
IoT technologies
.
Knowledge or interest in
AI/ML
applications for control and diagnostics.
Solid understanding of software architecture, testing, and version control.
Solution-oriented, reliable, and a good communicator.
Fluent in
English
; Slovak or German is an advantage.
