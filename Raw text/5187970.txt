POSITION
DESCRIPTION
We are looking for an experienced Oracle Database Administrator to join our growing technology team and ensure the performance, stability, and reliability of mission-critical databases within a large-scale financial environment.
You will play a key role in maintaining our Oracle infrastructure, optimizing database performance, and collaborating closely with cross-functional teams to deliver secure and efficient solutions.
WHAT WILL
YOU DO
•	Diagnose and resolve database errors and issues
•	Execute deployment scripts and manage database updates
•	Administer and optimize database storage
•	Perform data exports and imports as needed
•	Monitor database performance and adjust parameters for optimal efficiency
•	Oversee and verify backup jobs
•	Develop and maintain comprehensive database documentation
•	Conduct regular audits and security checks
•	Collaborate closely with Linux and storage teams, database programmers, and web developers
WHAT WE
EXPECT
•	2–5 years of hands-on experience administering Oracle databases    on UNIX/Linux platforms
•	German language proficiency B2 or higher
•	Strong knowledge of SQL and PL/SQL scripting; ability to automate routine DBA tasks
•	Solid experience with backup and recovery (RMAN) and performance tuning
•	Familiarity with database monitoring tools and performance optimization
•	Basic understanding of Unix/Linux shell scripting
•	Strong analytical and problem-solving mindset, with focus on reliability and efficiency
•	Excellent communication and documentation skills; team-oriented approach
•	Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or related field
•	Willingness to participate in on-call rotations (shared schedule within the DBA team)
