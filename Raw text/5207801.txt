Experienced Cloud Software Engineer - Eset Protect
Place of work
Bratislava, Slovakia (Job with occasional home office)

From 3 500 EUR/month
* The final basic wage component can be increased accordingly to individual skills and experience of the selected candidate.
* Performance bonus 2 times per year up to 10% of the basic salary paid for the evaluation period(usually 6 months).
Contract type
full-time
We are looking for an experienced Cloud Software engineer to join our team and help shape the future of ESET Protect, a product trusted by over 100,000 companies worldwide. ESET Protect manages more than 5 million devices and handles tens of thousands of device connections per second. Our ecosystem includes numerous microservices, web applications, endpoint agents, and CI/CD pipelines.
As a experienced software engineer, you will play a key role in designing scalable solutions, improving cloud architecture, and mentoring other developers.
Join us to deliver end-to-end features that support system and network administrators globally.
What does working as a software engineer on ESET Protect entail?
Leading the design and implementation of complex features and services.
Driving architectural discussions and optimizations for our cloud-based ecosystem.
Ensuring best practices in code quality, testing, and deployment.
Collaborating with cross-functional teams to deliver high-quality solutions.
What do we expect?
Proven experience in software development.
Proficiency in C# with .NET Core and experience with C++ for performance-critical components.
Understanding of cloud platforms (Azure preferred) and cloud-native development.
Hands-on experience with Kubernetes, Kafka/EventHub, Terraform, and Helm.
Strong knowledge of databases (MS SQL, PostgreSQL (Citus), Azure Cosmos NoSQL).
Experience with microservices architecture, containerization, and CI/CD pipelines.
Excellent problem-solving skills and ability to make architectural decisions.
Willingness to work in an Agile environment.
Nice to have
Knowledge of front-end frameworks like React.js - to be able to deliver end to end feature.
Experience with distributed systems, concurrency, and performance optimization.
Familiarity with DevOps practices and Infrastructure as Code
