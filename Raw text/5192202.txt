We are looking for an enthusiastic and driven graduate to join our Management Information function team and support the build and rollout of automated reporting using the Power BI (PBI) service. This is an exciting opportunity to engage with function leads, learn how to identify and expose key performance indicators (KPIs), and contribute to driving PBI adoption and delivering valuable insights to end-users. You will work closely with our internal data team to assist in identifying and transforming data, contributing to the development of data models, and helping to create clear and insightful dashboards for various functions. This role offers a great opportunity to work with unique and innovative tools with strong emphasis on one’s ability to learn and shape the role.
