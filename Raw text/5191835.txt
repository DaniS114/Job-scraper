Job description, responsibilities and duties
​We are seeking an experienced
Java developer with fluent German
to join a multinational team.
In this position, you will be creating a web application, designing and implementing back-end requirements, and dealing with a highly complex state-of-the-art solution as part of a top-notch project.
​
Tasks:
You will design and develop modern software components with Java 21, Spring Boot 3, and PostgreSQL in an agile team
Together with our product owners, you will translate technical requirements into robust technical solutions
You will analyze and fix bugs – and develop preventive strategies to avoid errors
You will contribute to the further development of our architecture and code standards
Quality is your top priority: with automated tests, structured code reviews, and clear documentation
You actively contribute your suggestions and ideas in an environment with short decision-making processes
You actively contribute to the team culture – through knowledge transfer, mentoring, and thinking outside the box
