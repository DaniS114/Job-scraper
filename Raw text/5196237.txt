Information about the position
Job description, responsibilities and duties
We are looking for a motivated specialist to support sales processes and reporting. If you enjoy working with data, sales tools and want to grow in a dynamic environment, this role is for you!
Responsibilities and tasks:
Prepare and maintain sales reports, pipeline files and cost calculations
Support quotation preparation and CRM system setup
Manage SAP and B2B platforms, ensuring data accuracy
Collaborate with finance, production and other internal teams
Assist with market analyses and process improvements
Support day-to-day operational tasks and small projects as needed
