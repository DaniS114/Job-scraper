Job description, responsibilities and duties
Your Mission
Be one of the first members of our
brand-new location in Bratislava
and join our dynamic team in revolutionizing the FinTech landscape! We are looking for someone genuinely initiative-taking who wants more than just routine tasks. Play a crucial role in
designing, implementing, and optimizing robust CI/CD pipelines on cloud-native infrastructure
. As a DevOps Engineer, you will automate and oversee build, test, and deployment processes, ensuring seamless integration and continuous delivery for our innovative platforms. Your expertise in Kubernetes, Docker, and automation will drive high-quality, reliable, and scalable deployments across our global FinTech solutions.
Sounds exciting? Keep reading!
What You Will Do
Design and implement robust CI/CD pipelines to automate the build, test, and deployment processes, ensuring seamless integration and delivery.
Manage and orchestrate containerized applications using Kubernetes and Docker
Oversee the release process, warranting high-quality software delivery with no disruption and downtime
Automate deployment processes to facilitate continuous deployment
Collaborate with development and operations teams to foster a culture of collaboration and continuous improvement
Monitor system performance and proactively identify areas for optimization and enhancement
Your Profile
At least 3 years of experience in a comparable role in DevOps
Proven expertise in CI/CD practices and tools, with focus on automated deployment pipeline
Strong experience with Kubernetes and Docker
Proficiency in scription languages for automation and system management
Automation-focused mindset, with the ability to implement continuous deployment strategies that ensure zero downtime during updates
Excellent problem-solving skills, paired with a collaborative and open-minded approach to working within cross-functional teams
​English proficiency at a C1 level - other European languages are a plus​​
Valid work permit for Slovakia and ability to work in the office in Bratislava at least 2 days a week​
