Job description, responsibilities and duties
​We are searching for a Technical Compliance Manager to join our Platform Compliance Team, who is responsible for transferring provided legal requirements into smart technical requirements/concepts. It’s a key role linking together the Legal, Regulatory, IT and Business Teams and to make sure the platforms, products and services meet the mandatory compliance requirements.
Your tasks will be:
Read, understand & clarify the Legal/Compliance Requirements (provided by our Regulatory Compliance Department) from a product point of view
Translate and interpret the Rules & Regulations into smart technical requirements for the development teams understanding
Breakdown high-level requirements into user stories
Prepare the (technical) requirements document and hand it over to development teams that they can work on the detailed technical analysis
Conduct Discovery & Grooming Meetings with the development teams & relevant stakeholders
Plan and manage the delivery of the features/products
Ensure that feature sets, that are built for existing & new markets, are in compliance with the regulation
Assist in the QA Testing
Coordinate the delivery of the requested features/products/work packages between development teams, operational team and product owners
Provide technical expertise for any platform features in regards of platform compliance and sign off implementation to ensure compliance
Support the investigation and technical problem solving for any compliance issues
Assist in collating technical evidence from development teams in support of internal and external audits
Run the regulatory monitoring on a daily and monthly basis
Work with Legal, Developers, Product Owners, IT Compliance and Regulatory Compliance
Ensure stakeholders are always well informed on the progress and updates of any ongoing developments
