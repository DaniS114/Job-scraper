Job description, responsibilities and duties
Senior Pricing Analyst for Global Rating Engine implementation
We are looking for a Senior Pricing Analyst to support the development and maintenance of actuarial pricing tools and processes. This role bridges actuarial, data science, and IT functions, helping to ensure the quality, transparency, and scalability of pricing models and analytics. You will play a key role in enabling technical capabilities, supporting documentation, and fostering collaboration across teams. We are looking for a person who enjoys working on a variety of tasks rather than specializing.
Your role
As a Senior Pricing Analyst, your main responsibilities will involve many of:
Supporting the design and prototyping of modular pricing algorithms
Translating pricing logic into Excel or Python for broader accessibility
Collaborating with data and platform teams to define and validate data inputs
Assisting actuarial teams with version control, code navigation, and documentation
Supporting the development of unit tests and impact testing for pricing models
Contributing to the maintenance of pricing assumptions and rate tables
Delivering analytical support for rate development and portfolio monitoring
Your Skills and Experience
As a Senior Pricing Analyst, your skills and experience will ideally include:
University degree in Actuarial Science, Data Science, Statistics, or a related field
5+ years of experience in pricing, analytics, or technical actuarial roles
Proficiency in Excel and basic Python scripting (other languages like R are an advantage)
Familiarity with version control tools (e.g., Git)
Strong communication and prompt engineering skills
Experience working with cross-functional teams in a technical environment
Autonomous /Independent thinker
Actuarial qualification is a plus

Brief description of the company
Zurich is a leading multi-line insurer that serves its customers in global and local markets. With about 60,000 employees it provides a wide range of property and casualty, life insurance products and services in more than 200 countries and territories. Zurich’s customers include individuals, small businesses, and mid-sized and large companies, as well as multinational corporations.
Zurich Slovakia is a Competence Center based in Bratislava and Kosice providing highly specialized services for Zurich's headquarter in Switzerland and all Zurich Business units around the globe. Our services are focusing on all types of actuarial services – Life and Non-life, pricing and reserving, on data science and engineering, risk management, business intelligence, information technology, software asset management as well as insurance network management and technical accounting. Our Competence Center employs around 400+ staff from 20+ different countries.
