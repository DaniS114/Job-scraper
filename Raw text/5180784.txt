Information about the position
Job description, responsibilities and duties
Are you ready to apply your Java skills to solve real business challenges? Join our dynamic team where you'll build innovative applications that power our digital ecosystem! We're looking for a motivated developer who can turn requirements into reliable, efficient code that makes a difference in our organization.
About the Role
As our Java Application Engineer, you'll develop and maintain business applications using Java and Spring Boot. You'll work alongside experienced team members to implement features, improve existing systems, and contribute to solutions that serve both our internal teams and external users.
Key Responsibilities
•	Develop and implement business applications and integration solutions based on technical specifications
•	Participate in code reviews to ensure quality, consistency, and adherence to coding standards
•	Collaborate with senior engineers to understand technology stacks and improve your technical expertise
•	Troubleshoot and resolve technical issues by applying your knowledge and working with the team
•	Learn and apply new technologies and frameworks to enhance our applications
About the Team
Our team develops, extends and maintains business applications. Both business user group and respective solutions range from very tailored and specialized to large applications for hundreds of users. The content covers the use case for data capture, integration, transformation to BPM solutions. We closely collaborate with other teams around the globe.
