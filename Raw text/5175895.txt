Job description, responsibilities and duties
We are searching for an experienced
Finance Automation Run Lead
(contractor) for our client from the
automotive industry
.
You will take ownership of day-to-day operations of
Finance automations (RPA & AI Agents)
built on UiPath—across
Record to Report (RTR)
,
Procure to Pay (PTP)
, and
Order to Cash (OTC)
processes.
Your mission: ensure stable, efficient, and measurable platform operations. You’ll coordinate incidents, service requests, and vendor activities, maintain dashboards and reports, and continuously drive optimization and improvement.
This is a unique opportunity to work with
cutting-edge automation and AI technologies
, contributing directly to the next generation of enterprise process automation in a global company.
You will be reporting to the RPA Product Manager.
Key Responsibilities
Manage day-to-day automation schedules, queues, and system capacity.
Coordinate resolution of incidents, analyze root causes, and propose improvements.
Define and monitor SLAs (e.g., success rate, AHT, backlog), and publish operational dashboards.
Support change requests, deployments, and hypercare with clear exit criteria.
Maintain runbooks, SOPs, and CMDB records; manage vendor SLAs and issue tracking.
Collaborate with finance process owners and SMEs to prioritize and deliver automation use cases.
Lead UAT cycles, manage test data, and maintain regression test packs.
Act as daily liaison between finance users, IT operations, leadership, and vendors.
Drive continuous improvement initiatives to reduce manual effort and improve efficiency.
Coach users on AI prompt engineering and context creation for finance use cases.
