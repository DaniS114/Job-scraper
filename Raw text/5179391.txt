At
CulturePulse
we’re building AI that understands people. Our platform leverages advanced data analytics and cultural AI models to help organizations connect with their audiences on a deeper, more human level.
To support our growth, we’re looking for a
Data Engineer
who will own our data pipelines, shape the future of our ETL/ELT architecture. This is a unique opportunity to join an ambitious, international team at the forefront of cultural AI.
Your responsibilities
Pipeline Ownership
Design, build, and maintain scraping pipelines for multiple sources through APIs and BigQuery
Ensure raw data is consistently stored and organized in a centralized data layer (single source of truth for downstream processing)
Architecture & Future-Proofing
Identify architectural gaps and implement modern data architecture (e.g., medallion architecture with bronze/silver/gold layers)
Define and introduce standards and best practices for ETL/ELT jobs
Monitoring & Reliability
Improve monitoring and alerting systems for better reliability
Develop proactive issue detection and automated recovery strategies
Refine data quality checks and pipeline SLAs
Database & Storage
Manage and optimize MongoDB (Atlas): indexing, schema optimization, archiving, performance tuning
Explore and lead transition toward scalable modern platforms (Snowflake, Redshift, or similar)
Collaboration
Partner with Data Science to deliver reliable, high-quality datasets for training and production
Work with Backend and Product teams to ensure data availability meets product requirements
Act as a hands-on contributor
Align with the Head of Engineering on long-term backend strategy and standards
