Company profile
Descartes is the global leader in providing on-demand, software-as-a-service solutions focused on improving the productivity, performance and security of logistics-intensive businesses.
More than 26 000 our customers use our modular, software-as-a-service solutions to
- route, schedule, track and measure delivery resources;
- plan, allocate and execute shipments;
- rate, audit and pay transportation invoices;
- access global trade data;
- file customs and security documents for imports and exports;
- and complete numerous other logistics processes by participating in the world's largest, collaborative multimodal logistics community.
Our headquarters are in Waterloo, Ontario, Canada and we have offices and partners around the world.
Learn more at www.descartes.com
Your responsibilities
We’re looking for someone who enjoys solving big technical challenges, loves cloud tech, and wants to help shape how our systems run across Azure, public cloud, and on-prem environments. If that sounds like you, keep reading.
What you’ll be doing
You’ll be part of our global engineering team—sometimes working from home, sometimes meeting with colleagues in the office. Your main mission: keep our Windows and cloud environments running smoothly while helping us grow and modernize them.
Here’s a taste of what your days might look like:
•	Running and improving our  hybrid/cloud environments and  Windows domains.
•	Leading or jumping into projects involving Azure, Office 365, and cloud modernization.
•	Driving automation using PowerShell, Azure CLI, and Infrastructure as Code.
•	Helping steer our cloud strategy—what we should adopt, improve, or rethink.
•	Troubleshooting tricky issues and handling production changes.
•	Sharing your knowledge with teammates around the world.
•	Making sure our server environments are secure, scalable, and reliable.
•	Taking part in an on-call rotation
The final job offer depends on your knowledge and experience.
Your profile
