Information about the position
Job description, responsibilities and duties
Join a team of cybersecurity professionals and help Swiss Re to fulfil its mission in making the world more resilient. As an Operations Specialist, you'll be tasked with managing strategic people-planning activities, supporting various reporting needs and analysing financial and operational performance across our divisions.
About the team
The Security Team is the focal point for all security activities across Swiss Re. We are responsible for all aspects of cybersecurity engineering and operations, governance, risk, and compliance. We define and advance the company's security strategy.
As part of the Security Team, the Strategy & Operations Centre of Excellence drives the Security Team's overall strategy and acts as a hub for its continuous improvement with a strong focus on culture, ambition and mandate. We're looking for an experienced individual who will play a pivotal role in shaping the future of our Security Team.
In your role, you will…
•	Oversee and execute the strategic workforce planning process
•	Support Divisional Operations Officer with coordinating recruitment activities across the division (in collaboration with the Management and the Recruitment teams)
•	Keep track of divisional financial performance and collaborate closely with key stakeholders
•	Create and compile management presentations, reports and analyses of workforce-related simple data
•	Ensure adherence to the governance framework
•	Oversee Objectives & Key Results and manage quarterly review processes
•	Prepare, coordinate and lead leadership meetings and divisional events
•	Act as a trusted partner and go-to person for operational questions for the leadership team and the entire division
