Senior IT project manager in Bratislava
As a member of the international transformation team of DTSE, you will act as part of transformation projects for DTSE and its customers.You will lead project activities within your area of responsibility in the large sized (sub)projects.
Contract type
full-time
Place of work

Wage (gross)

About
the position
Within your personal scope of duties, you will be responsible for the following issues:
· Set-Up and planning of projects (structures, organizations, phases, resources, budget, risk-mgmt. & reporting) according to PMI- & Agile-related methodologies
· Supporting or leading tasks & activities in Agile environment with certification as a Scrum Master or/and Product Owner
· Setting up project management office and steer all project related activities
· Plan and coordinate SAP data migrations and cutover for selected rollout companies
· Support driving the strategy to migrate data from original system into the central ERP environment
· Responsible for steering and controlling of migration activities and results according to prepared plan
· Align and communicate all activities between central and local migration teams and establish strong working relationship
· Define and manage migration scope
· Consolidate migration concept and coordinate review
· Define and steer migration deliverables for ERP projects
· Monitor and steer input delivery before migration runs
· Plan and coordinate migration validation and learnings implementation
· Responsible for preparation of test cases for migrated data and support execution and defect solving
· Monitor validation cases documentation progress during auditor quality process
· Monitor and steer progress & report the milestones for the rollout company
· Preparation of management presentations, status reports and documentations
· Manage cross-functional topics and escalation issues
· Change and risk management
· Communication to project team and relevant stakeholders
· Resource management
· Document management
· Further development of DTSE project standards and tools
· Analysis of processes and identification of optimization opportunities regarding efficiency and quality
· Initiating the assessment of Proof Of Concept (POC). Works on evaluation and realisation of POC to ensure cost reduction and increase of efficiency in migration projects
The published salary is the minimum possible offer.
The starting salary may be higher depending on the extent of fulfillment of the employee's requirements (education, language skills, required practice, personality assumptions and skills).
