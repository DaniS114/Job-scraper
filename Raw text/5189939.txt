Information about the position
Job description, responsibilities and duties
️ CME Group is seeking an experienced and visionary Head of Data and AI Architecture to lead the design, development, and governance of our group-wide data architecture.
️ This role plays a critical part in modernizing CME’s data platforms, ensuring scalability, security, and regulatory compliance while enabling advanced analytics, business intelligence, and AI-driven innovation.
️ Ready to build the next generation of CME’s data ecosystem? Apply now and join our team in Prague.
Who we are? https://www.cme.net/
What You Will Be Responsible For?
Strategic Data Architecture
• Define and implement the group-wide data architecture vision aligned with business and regulatory requirements.
• Lead the migration from legacy and on-premise systems to modern, cloud-based data platforms (Azure, AWS, or GCP).
• Harmonize data models, reporting structures, and semantic layers across all CME markets.
Data Platform Design & Management
• Architect and oversee scalable data platforms, including data lakes, warehouses, and semantic layers.
• Ensure efficient and secure ETL/ELT processes and seamless integration of internal and external data sources.
• Maintain and optimize data pipelines for reliability, performance, and security.
Data Governance & Compliance
• Establish and maintain data governance frameworks covering data quality, classification, retention, and access control.
• Ensure full compliance with GDPR, the EU AI Act, and other relevant regulations.
• Collaborate closely with compliance, IT, and information security teams at both group and local levels.
Collaboration & Enablement
• Partner with Data Engineers, Analysts, Scientists, and business stakeholders to ensure high-quality, accessible data for analytics and decision-making.
• Support business intelligence, non-financial reporting, and digital transformation initiatives.
• Act as a key partner in vendor and technology discussions related to data tooling and infrastructure.
Continuous Improvement
• Monitor global trends in data architecture, analytics, and AI integration.
• Promote the adoption of modern technologies and best practices across all markets.
• Lead or contribute to cross-country projects aimed at enhancing data capabilities and reporting standards.
