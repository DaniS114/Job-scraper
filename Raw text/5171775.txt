Job description, responsibilities and duties
We’re looking for a forward-thinking IT Service Manager to lead the delivery and performance of our operational IT services. This is a key role focused on ensuring services are reliable, scalable, and aligned with business needs—while fostering a culture of continuous improvement and customer excellence.
You’ll manage the full service lifecycle, build strong relationships across technical teams and stakeholders, and ensure outsourced partners meet performance expectations. If you're passionate about service quality, collaboration, and driving meaningful change, we’d love to hear from you.
What You’ll Be Doing
Ensure consistent, high-quality delivery of IT services across all regions
Develop and maintain the global IT service model and Service Catalogue
Drive a service culture through process improvement and governance
Manage SLAs, OLAs, XLAs and risk acceptance frameworks
Lead service reviews with vendors and manage inventory
Support major incident response and out-of-hours IT MI rota
Oversee smooth service transitions from projects into live environments
Promote IT adoption through training, communications, and self-help resources
Monitor service costs and identify opportunities for efficiency
Provide clear reporting and insights to stakeholders
What We’re Looking For
Strong ITIL knowledge (Foundation level minimum)
Experience in service management, vendor oversight, and incident response
Analytical thinking and the ability to translate data into actionable insights
Excellent communication and stakeholder engagement skills
A collaborative, customer-focused mindset
Familiarity with ITSM tools (ideally ServiceNow)
Experience in regulated or audit-sensitive environments
