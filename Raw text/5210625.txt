Job description, responsibilities and duties
Our client is a globally focused fintech company based in Bratislava that aims to transform the way people understand and manage their finances.
Role summary
Own the technology strategy and architecture end‑to‑end. You’ll set the technical vision, lead the engineering org, and remain hands‑on where it matters—scaling systems, shipping product, and building a high‑performing culture.
What you’ll do
Technology      strategy:
Define 12–24-36-month tech roadmap aligned to business goals; make      build/buy/partner decisions.
Architecture:
Design and      evolve a secure, scalable, observable platform (APIs, data, cloud, AI/ML).
Execution:
Establish      SDLC, CI/CD, testing, and incident management; drive delivery      predictability.
Team:
Hire, mentor,      and structure engineering (frontend, backend, data/ML, DevOps/SRE).
Security &      compliance:
Implement best practices (e.g., least privilege, encryption, key management);      prepare for SOC 2/ISO 27001 as needed.
Data & AI:
Set up the      data platform and MLOps; govern AI usage (privacy, safety, cost).
Cost &      reliability:
Optimize cloud spend; set SLOs and capacity plans.
Stakeholder management:
Partner with      Product, GTM, Finance on prioritization and trade‑offs.
