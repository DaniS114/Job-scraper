O365 Collaboration Engineer
Your contribution:
As a Microsoft 365 Collaboration L3 Engineer, you will be at the forefront of transforming how teams work together. You will leverage the power of Microsoft 365 productivity suite, including the cutting-edge Microsoft 365 Copilot and PowerPlatform and other collaborative technologies such as AppSpace, to drive adoption and foster a culture of innovation and harnesses the full potential of AI to enhance productivity and streamline workflows. Join us in our mission to revolutionize the way we work and achieve excellence together!
Key Responsibilties:
Lead the deployment, configuration, and maintenance of Microsoft 365 collaborative workloads such as Exchange Online, SharePoint Online, Teams, Power Platform, and Microsoft 365 Copilot, ensuring they meet organizational needs and compliance standards.
Provide Level 3 technical support for all collaboration technologies, resolving complex incidents, problems, and service requests in a timely and efficient manner.
Administer, support, and optimize Digital Signage platforms, including AppSpace and BrightSign, integrating them with Microsoft 365 services where appropriate.
Drive adoption and effective use of Microsoft 365 Copilot and other emerging Microsoft collaborative tools by developing and sharing best practices, documentation, and end-user training materials and maintaining related Viva Engage communities.
Act as a subject-matter expert and advocate for Microsoft 365 technologies, collaborating with internal stakeholders to understand business requirements and translate them into technical solutions.
Partner with security and compliance teams to ensure that collaboration solutions adhere to organizational policies and regulatory obligations.
Monitor system health, performance, and usage metrics for all collaboration services, proactively addressing potential issues and implementing improvements to ensure high availability and user satisfaction.
Participate in strategic planning and roadmap development for the evolution of collaborative technologies within Clarios, including evaluation and implementation of new features and third-party integrations.
Maintain clear and thorough documentation of architectures, configurations, procedures, and troubleshooting steps for knowledge sharing and continuity purposes.
Knowledge, Skills and Abilities:
Bachelor’s degree or related experience in Computer Science, Engineering or related discipline.
5+ years of hands-on experience implementing and supporting Microsoft 365 services, including Exchange Online, SharePoint Online, Teams, Power Platform.
Fluency in English
1+year hands-on experience with Copilot family services, include Copilot Studio.
Experience with Digital Signage solutions such as AppSpace and BrightSign, including deployment, configuration, and troubleshooting.
Strong planning and organizational skills, with a proven ability to manage priorities and deliver results in a fast-paced environment.
Exceptional interpersonal and communication skills, enabling effective collaboration with stakeholders at all organizational levels.
Demonstrated ability to translate business needs and requirements into technical solutions, with a consultative approach to problem solving.
Experience providing Level 3 support for complex enterprise collaboration environments.
Solid understanding of security, compliance, and governance concepts as they relate to Microsoft 365 and digital collaboration platforms.
Familiarity with identity and access management in Microsoft 365, including Entra ID, conditional access, MFA and role-based access controls.
Experience with scripting and automation (e.g., PowerShell) for managing and deploying Microsoft 365 resources.
Ability to work independently, exercise good judgment, and prioritize tasks to meet business objectives.
Relevant Microsoft certifications are highly desirable.
