What will you do
We are seeking an experienced Power BI Developer to join our Analytics and Reporting team at Lenovo. The Power BI Developer role will be responsible for designing, developing, and implementing interactive dashboards and reports in Power BI for various business units.
This role will be part of a dynamic and fast-paced team and will have the opportunity to work on high-impact projects that drive significant business value. If you have a passion for data visualization and are excited about working with a global, world-class team, we want to hear from you!
Key Responsibilities:
- Design, develop, and implement interactive dashboards and reports in Power BI.
- Work with business stakeholders to gather requirements and understand their data visualization needs.
- Create data models and develop queries to connect to various data sources.
- Utilize DAX formulas, Power Query, and Power Pivot to enhance data analysis capabilities.
- Enhance existing dashboards and reports to meet changing business needs.
- Test and debug dashboards and reports to ensure they function as expected.
- Collaborate with other developers and analysts on cross-functional projects.
Please note: this role is a maternity cover.
