What can you expect?
Adient (NYSE: ADNT) is a global leader in automotive seating. With 70,000+ employees in 29 countries, Adient operates more than 200 manufacturing/assembly plants worldwide. We produce and deliver automotive seating for all major OEMs. From complete seating systems to individual foam, trim and metal components, our expertise spans every step of the automotive seat-making process. Our integrated, in-house skills allow us to take our products from research and design to engineering and manufacturing — and into millions of vehicles every year. For more information, visit www.adient.com.
At Adient, we are proud to offer hybrid working to our employees, which involves being able to work remotely for up to 2 days per week based on the agreement within specific department.
What will your responsibilities be?
•	Stakeholder Management: Build and maintain strong relationships with Engineering, PMO, Purchasing, Quality teams to understand their needs and priorities.
•	Strategic Planning: Collaborate with business leaders to develop IT strategies that support business goals and drive innovation.
•	Project Management: Oversee the delivery of IT projects, ensuring they meet business requirements, are delivered on time, and within budget.
•	Service Management: Ensure IT services are aligned with business needs and are delivered with high quality and reliability.
•	Change Management: Lead change management initiatives to ensure smooth adoption of new technologies and processes.
•	Risk Management: Identify and mitigate risks associated with IT projects and services.
•	Training: Training the business teams about the new tools and technologies
•	Alignment to IT Goals: Promote the use of emerging technologies to drive business innovation and efficiency. Align with Adient IT Goals
Which qualifications are we looking for in potential employees?
•	A Bachelor’s degree in Information Technology, Mechanical / Manufacturing Engineering or a related field.
•	6+ Years of experience in a similar role.
•	Working experience of Teamcenter RAC, AWC and other modules of Teamcenter such as Structure Manager, Query builder, Change Management
•	Good understanding of Engineering Bill of Materials, precise & imprecise BOMs, TC data model  (e.g., relationships between the objects).
•	Good understanding of interfaces and middleware such as eQube, Seeburger including data Mapping/transformation.
•	Experience supporting integrations between Teamcenter and SAP, QAD, Mendix and custom applications using API’s, file transfer, TC Webservices.
•	Proficient in working with data formats such as XML and JSON
•	Should have experience in working on Azure DevOps or similar ALM tools for requirement / Bugs creation and tracking
•	Analytical Skills: Strong analytical and problem-solving skills, capable of interpreting complex datasets and visualizing information effectively.
•	Technical Proficiency: Azure DevOps user level, Proficiency in Excel, MS Project, MS PPT, Agile Methodology
•	Excellent Communication: Strong verbal and written skills to interact effectively with stakeholders at all levels and present findings in a clear and concise manner.
•	Collaboration and Leadership: Ability to work in a team environment, facilitating communication and ensuring project goals are met collaboratively.

Brief description of the company
Adient is the leading automotive seating supplier.
With around 3000 employees and five locations in Slovakia, the company is one of the key employers in Slovakia’s automotive industry. We are committed to Sustainability and are proud ambassador of Diversity Charter in Slovakia since 2017.
Adient’s only business center worldwide is in Bratislava. Approximately 800 employees in Bratislava support the company’s business around the globe in finance, information technology, purchasing, sales, human resources, transport and logistics and project management.
In Trencin, Adient operates the biggest automotive technology center across Slovakia. More than 400 employees there develop a wide range of highly innovative products for the cars of tomorrow.
In Zilina, the company produces complete seat systems and automotive fabrics, in Lucenec foam components for seats as well as metal components for head restraints.
Adient is a global leader in automotive seating. With approximately 70 000 employees in 30 countries, Adient operates 200 manufacturing/assembly plants worldwide, we produce and deliver automotive seating for all vehicle classes and all major OEMs. From complete seating systems to individual components, our expertise spans every step of the automotive seat-making process. Our integrated, in-house skills allow us to take our products from research and design all the way to engineering and manufacturing – and into more than 20 million cars every year.
