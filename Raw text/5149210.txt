POSITION
DESCRIPTION
Join Accenture as an Azure Infrastructure and Security Consultant and play a pivotal role in enhancing the reliability and recoverability of our clients' cloud environments. You will leverage your deep expertise in Microsoft Azure and disaster recovery to deliver actionable recommendations that fortify our clients' systems against disruptions.
This is a unique opportunity for senior professionals with experience in Infrastructure to upskill in the Security, latest technologies and earn industry-leading certifications. At Accenture, you'll work on diverse projects, ensuring continuous learning and growth. Be part of a dynamic team that values innovation and excellence.
WHAT WILL
YOU DO
• Conduct in-depth technical analysis of cloud infrastructure, application dependencies, observability configurations, and failover capabilities
• Evaluate and strengthen configurations for Azure AD (Entra ID), Privileged Identity Management (PIM), Azure security services, including Microsoft Defender for Cloud and others
• Review and enhance MIRPs, including escalation protocols, incident communication plans, recovery sequencing, and impact containment strategies
• Assess the resiliency and security posture of customer environments from both technical and operational perspectives
• Identify risks, vulnerabilities, and single points of failure across workloads and operational processes
• Deliver professional, customer-facing documentation summarizing technical findings and process maturity recommendations
WHAT WE
EXPECT
• up to 5 years of hands-on experience with cloud infrastructure architecture, specifically within Microsoft Azure environments
• Experience with Azure AD (Entra ID), PIM, Azure Policy, Azure Monitor
• Understand the concept of High Availability (HA) and Disaster Recovery (DR) design in Microsoft Azure
• Proven ability to assess and design effective Major Incident Response Plans (MIRPs) that align with operational SLAs and business risk tolerances
• Excellent communication and documentation skills for technical and executive stakeholders
• Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in IT or a related field
• English and German language proficiency at Upper intermediate (B2) or Advanced (C1)
Nice to have:
• Microsoft certifications such as: AZ-305, AZ-400, SC-100, AZ-500, SC-200, SC-300, AZ-104
•  Experience with Azure security and governance services, including Microsoft Defender for Cloud, Sentinel, Key Vault, and Log Analytics
