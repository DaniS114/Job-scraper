Are you a passionate Java developer looking for an exciting career opportunity? At Accenture Advanced Technology Center, we offer a dynamic and challenging IT career with a strong focus on application development, work-life balance, and continuous professional growth.
Join us and be part of cutting-edge projects that leverage cloud-first solutions, Agile methodologies, and DevOps principles. Our tech stack is modern, our approach is forward-thinking, and our environment is built for innovation.
WHAT WILL
YOU DO
•	Work on impactful projects using Java (Spring) and related frameworks
•	Develop and maintain cloud-native applications (AWS, custom Java frameworks)
•	Gain hands-on experience with Gen AI and other emerging technologies
•	Be a key player in a fast-moving Agile team with strong DevOps culture
•	Contribute to iterative prototyping to optimize user experience
•	Take the opportunity to grow into an architect role and lead teams
•	Mentor junior and mid-level developers, shaping the future of our tech community
WHAT WE
EXPECT
•	Experienced in Java development (5+ years of experience)
•	Proficient with Spring framework
•	You have solid knowledge of SQL/NoSQL databases
•	Experienced in technical design and solution architecture
•	University educated (Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in IT or a related field)
•	You communicate well in English (at least B2 level)
•	Fluent in Slovak (C2 level)
Nice to have:
•	Experience with frontend development (JavaScript, React, Angular, or similar)
•	Hands-on experience with CI/CD pipelines and DevOps practices
•	Knowledge of Docker, Kubernetes, and containerization
•	Experience in team and project management
