Information about the position
Job description, responsibilities and duties
Are you passionate about creating intuitive, responsive user interfaces that transform complex business needs into elegant digital experiences? Join our innovative team where your Angular expertise will help shape the future of our digital landscape! We are looking for a skilled Angular developer who understands how to write clear and maintainable code but also has a solid understanding of architectural best practices, modern frameworks, and the importance of creating intuitive user experiences that make a real impact.
About the Role
As our Senior Front End Developer, you'll design and implement modern web applications using Angular for our Contract Information Hub - the central platform for P&C Treaty and Facultative contract administration. You'll deliver intuitive user interfaces that enable efficient contract information management while collaborating with teams across the organization.
Key Responsibilities
•	Design and develop responsive, user-friendly interfaces for the Contract Information Hub, serving internal users across the organization
•	Review and approve pull requests to ensure code quality, consistency, coding standards, and maintainability of front-end code
•	Guide and mentor development teams on Angular best practices, sharing your expertise to elevate overall team performance
•	Solve complex UI/UX problems by leveraging your experience and technical knowledge to optimize application performance and user experience
•	Research and implement emerging front-end technologies to continuously improve our solutions and stay ahead of industry trends
About the Team
Our team develops, extends and maintains business applications with a primary focus on the Contract Information Hub - Swiss Re's central platform for P&C Treaty and Facultative contract administration. This system serves as one of the key building blocks in our P&C's Hub Architecture. We closely collaborate with other teams around the globe to ensure seamless integration within our ecosystem.
