Information about the position
Job description, responsibilities and duties
We are seeking a hands-on Senior IT Security Engineer who will directly implement and manage cybersecurity improvements across the organization. This role is critical to aligning the company with ISO 27001 standards and ensuring a secure environment for operations. Unlike managerial roles, this position is focused on execution — ideal for someone who thrives in technical challenges and independent work.
​This position is suitable for candidates residing in Western Slovakia.​
Key Accountabilities
• Develop and maintain security policies and incident response plans.
• Implement and manage Identity and Access Management (IAM) systems.
• Manage Fortinet NGFW and Cisco network device configurations.
• Act as the primary contact during cyber incidents.
• Utilize WithSecure MDR, SIEM, SOAR, and EDR tools for threat detection and response.
• Oversee application security lifecycle including vulnerability scanning and penetration testing.
• Define and enforce Microsoft 365 cloud security policies and MFA.
• Coordinate with backup providers to ensure secure data recovery solutions.
• Deliver security awareness training across the organization.
• Conduct audits and ensure compliance with ISO 27001.

The company's technology enables the production of high-quality products with efficient design, the tightest tolerances and shorter delivery times. At the same time, the company focuses on providing the right advice and global support for clients' critical needs to meet their requirements. With more than 1700 employees in more than 7 locations in Europe, the Americas and the Pacific, they are committed to deliver an impactful product to exceed the customer expectations.
