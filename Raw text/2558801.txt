What we do
Come create the technology that helps the world act together.

What are your job duties
As a C/C++ Software Developer, you will work on the development and evolution of software for IP routers. You will have a chance to participate in the development of leading IP products. You will have the opportunity to use your creativity and engineering experience to improve the development and test environment, procedures and the products themselves.
As part of our team, you will:
•	Develop features that will evolve the IP router products
•	Collaborate with the test team to deliver your features with exceptional quality and to maintain it during their lifetime
•	Be in close contact with the other engineers and teams
•	Understand the challenges of SW design for high availability systems
•	Be able to cope with the responsibility of delivering a feature you committed to
What we are looking for
We are looking for junior and senior developers as well as fresh graduates with a programming background. The key is a passion for software development and finding joy in programming.
We count on you have:
•	A good understanding and experience with programming and debugging in C, C++
•	Fluent English knowledge – verbal and written as well
•	University degree in Engineering and Technology / Information Technology
Nice to have:
•	Experience with Linux (command line) including basic scripting
•	Experience with some versioning systems (preferably GIT)
•	Relevant experience with IP protocol family and routing
Only CVs in English will be considered for the selection process.
