Senior Digital Analyst
Place of work
Bratislava, Slovakia (Job with occasional home office)
Wage (gross)
From 2 700 EUR/month
*Performance bonus 2 times per year up to 10% of the basic salary paid for the evaluation period (usually 6 months).
Contract type
full-time
As a Senior Digital Analyst, you will be a key member of the Global Digital Analytics team, analyzing digital data to identify opportunities and recommend improvements to digital business performance.
The role involves creating reports and dashboards on key metrics, evaluating online campaign performance, and providing actionable insights, as well as conducting ad-hoc analyses to uncover trends.
Managing web analytics tools to ensure accurate tracking and seamless integration with marketing platforms is a key responsibility. The Senior Digital Analyst also defines technical specifications for analytics platforms, collaborates with development teams, educates internal teams on best practices, and mentors colleagues on data best practices.
A core responsibility is integrating business and web analytics data to generate insights that inform strategic decisions, evaluate online initiatives, identify trends, and provide actionable recommendations for improvements.
In this role you will:
Analyze web analytics and other quantitative and qualitative data to identify opportunities and recommend improvements to website performance
Gather and document reporting requirements, then design and create reports and dashboards focusing on traffic, conversion, sales, and other online customer behavior metrics
Identify automation needs, design and automate reports to reduce repetitive manual workload and enhance efficiency
Drive evaluation of online activities and campaigns and provide insights and actionable recommendations for improvement
Conduct ad-hoc analyses on key trends and provide recommendations based on findings. Collaborate with the digital marketing and sales teams to gather requirements for reporting
Oversee the setup and maintenance of web analytics tools and marketing platforms, ensuring seamless connections. Manage user access and account structures as needed
Design and maintain scripts and dashboards to monitor website performance, detect anomalies, and alert on critical changes or issues to enable timely intervention
Identify and gather business requirements and translate them into actionable tracking requirements
Manage tracking tags in a tag management tool, ensuring all relevant marketing and advertising platforms capture accurate data
Define technical specifications for web analytics and tag management platforms, communicate requirements to development teams, and verify implementation accuracy before launch
Lead educational initiatives for internal teams and regions on web analytics, data interpretation, and tracking best practices to support informed decision-making
Generate and utilize quantitative and qualitative research data, translating into actionable recommendations on website optimization or A/B testing ideas
Process large datasets, create custom data structures, metrics and data blends to extract actionable insights using advanced data processing tools and techniques
Integrate diverse data sources to enable a comprehensive understanding and optimal utilization of available data in steering the business direction. Be responsible for the accurate utilization and comprehension of sales data integrated with web analytics data
Analyze business intelligence data, interpret findings, and deliver actionable recommendations to support business growth
Provide strategic recommendations based on a holistic understanding of integrated business and web analytics insights
Plan and coordinate team tasks per managerial directive, be responsible for the performance of the tasks entrusted to it in accordance with established procedures and processes and prioritize tasks of colleagues in order to meet established objectives and achieve required results
Continuously develop knowledge and skills and keep up-to-date with new trends and information in the field of expertise
Required experience and skills:
At least 7 years of experience in a similar role
Proficiency in English at B2 level or higher
A proactive, "can-do" attitude, with a curious and analytical mindset
Expert quantitative and qualitative analytical skills OR strong Google Tag Manager experience with technical implementation experience

Good knowledge of SQL or BigQuery
Good knowledge of Google Platform products and data connection between them
Great ability to translate business requirements into KPIs and metrics
Solid analytical skills, capable of finding insights in diverse datasets and a solid grasp of statistics
Strong communication skills and ability to influence stakeholders through data-driven business recommendations
Great understanding of online marketing principles, web design, user experience, e-commerce, SEO, SEM, PPC and CRO best practices
Technical acumen and experience with web technologies such as HTML, JavaScript
Strong personal preference on data-driven decision making
Proficiency in Microsoft Excel
Ability to work independently, prioritize tasks, and adapt to changing priorities
Service-orientated team player with expert communication skills
Preferred Qualifications (a plus):
Google Analytics Individual Qualification certification or similar web analytics certification
Certification in Google Tag Manager or other tag management platforms
Experience with Google BigQuery or SQL certification
Experience with BI reporting tools
