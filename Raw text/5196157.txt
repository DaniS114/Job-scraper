Job description, responsibilities and duties
At Siemens Healthineers we pioneer breakthroughs in healthcare. In our Advanced Therapies business, we deliver state-of-the-art, image-guided therapy systems that enable minimally invasive vascular procedures worldwide.
Our project is focused on accelerating the release process while maintaining the highest quality standards. To achieve this, we are integrating our legacy test automation tool with a new approach - SITA-based test automation tool. This involves development of communication between these different systems to support needs of old and new world and export data to other reporting systems. Therefore, we are looking for a candidate with experience in architectural design and software patterns, who can help us successfully bridge these systems and drive innovation within our team.
Your responsibility
Design, develop and maintain SITA plug-ins in C#
Implement and incorporate API functions to allow communication between old and new world in automation
Invent and design a new way to allow communication between former and new automation tool and allow data exchange
Implement communication to systems for test specification and Azure DevOps
Write clean, testable, well-documented code and participate in peer reviews
Collaborate with architecture, quality, cybersecurity and field-service teams
Contribute to incremental process improvements
