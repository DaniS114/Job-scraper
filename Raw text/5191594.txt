Job description, responsibilities and duties
Insignia Group of Companies is one of the most bespoke high-end lifestyle management and payment service groups in the world. We are looking for a
DevOps Engineer
to join our dynamic Bratislava team.
We are seeking an experienced
DevOps Engineer
to join our team and help us modernize, automate, and optimise our infrastructure. Our primary environments are workloads hosted in our on-premises datacenter,
Microsoft Azure
and
Microsoft 365
. The ideal candidate will be responsible for designing and implementing
CI/CD pipelines from scratch, planning and managing the gradual migration of critical services from on-premises to cloud
and introducing modern DevOps best practices across the organization.
What You Will Be Doing
CI/CD Implementation & Automation
Design, build, and maintain CI/CD pipelines tailored to our applications and services.
Automate builds, testing, deployments, and monitoring.
Implement Infrastructure as Code (IaC) using tools like Terraform, ARM templates, or Bicep.
Cloud Migration
Drive the process of
strategic migration of critical services
from on-premises datacenter to cloud.
Design migration roadmaps that minimize downtime and risk while maximizing efficiency and scalability.
Ensure hybrid cloud solutions operate seamlessly during the transition phase.
Infrastructure Management
Manage and optimize resources across cloud and on-premises servers.
Monitor, secure, and maintain infrastructure availability and performance.
Ensure services are scalable and resilient during and after migration.
Collaboration & Process Improvement
Partner with development and IT teams to coordinate cloud adoption and DevOps processes.
Introduce modern practices such as GitOps, containerization, and automated testing.
Drive cultural adoption of DevOps principles within the organization.
Security & Compliance
Ensure infrastructure and pipelines adhere to security, compliance, and governance standards.
Implement monitoring, alerting, and logging solutions to improve system reliability and visibility.
What You Bring
Strong experience with
Azure (IaaS, PaaS, networking, security)
and
Microsoft 365
environments. AWS considered a plus.
Strong experience with Unix
Proven experience building CI/CD pipelines using
GitLab, Azure DevOps, GitHub Actions or similar tools.
Proficiency in Infrastructure as Code (
Ansible, Terraform, Bicep, ARM
).
Knowledge of scripting and automation (
PowerShell, Python, Bash
).
Experience with containerization (
Docker, Kubernetes
) and modern deployment practices.
Experience with
Databases & Data Management
: MS SQL, MySQL, MongoDB, Redis.
Experience with message brokers: RabbitMQ, Apache Kafka.
Proven experience
planning and executing cloud migrations
from on-premises environments.
Strong problem-solving skills and ability to work independently.
Excellent communication and collaboration skills with cross-functional teams.
Proactive mindset with eagerness to introduce and drive new technologies.
