About the role
Our team is searching for a
Senior Test Manager
, a position which plays a pivotal role in driving quality and excellence across multiple projects and daily business.
As a Senior Test Manager, you will have the autonomy to manage and support your team, coordinating efforts, assigning tasks, and ensuring that we adhere to the highest industry standards. Your expertise in quality assurance will be crucial as you plan and manage resources on daily basis, while tackling challenges head-on to ensure smooth testing with high quality results.
You will have the exciting opportunity to create and implement comprehensive testing plans and strategies, suggest and implement improvements in testing processes and tools.
If you are passionate about quality, thrive in a collaborative environment, and are ready to take on a new challenge, we want to hear from you!
Working Tasks and Responsibilities:
Manages the testing team
Participates in creating and implementing the team's test plan and strategy
Oversees the testing process during all its phases
Manages the correct definition and implementation of the scope of tests, based on actual requirements and strategy
Ensures adherence to testing standards
Prepares effort estimations and plans testing resources according to priorities
Oversees all testing-related activities throughout the testing process (e.g., methodology, test data)
Regularly evaluates the progress of tests and the effectiveness of test execution, proposing changes/improvements
Prepares regular reports/dashboards (e.g., testing status, defects, risks, etc.)
Applies appropriate measurement methods and metrics for measuring the performance of testers
Manages incident management across all testing phases
Communicates and collaborates with all stakeholders
Oversees the maintenance and development of test automation
Drives the preparation and implementation of test improvements on regular basis
Evaluates the work of individual testing team members
Provides consultations, training, and advice to team members, supporting their knowledge level and professional growth
Oversees the creation of test documentation and manuals.

Who we are
Established in 2000 as a division of Tatra banka, RPC is a shared service centre of Raiffeisen Bank International (RBI).
In 2008, we evolved into a separate company, solidifying our position as a strategic hub for centralizing the group's card payment operations with specialization in card issuing, acquiring, fraud risk monitoring, and digital payments.
We bring fresh, innovative payment technologies across the European region, which guarantees our employees to be part of many exciting projects in multiple CEE countries.
Besides Tatra banka in Slovakia and UBB in Bulgaria, we are proud to have among our clients Raiffeisen banks from Austria, Albania, Czech Republic, Croatia, Romania, Hungary, Serbia, Ukraine, and Kosovo, which makes us one of the largest processing companies in the region.
Come be a part of RPC as we shape the future of payment technology! If you're passionate about innovation and want to join a collaborative and international team, we'd love to have you with us.
