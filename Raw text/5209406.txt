Admin and support IT Service Operational activities under IT Operations, primarily covering Incident, Problem and Change Management tasks End-user support.
Responsibilities:

-	Support and pro-active management of End Client Equipment’s (Laptops, Desktops, Tablets, Mobiles, Printers, Projectors, Engineering Devices etc.

-	Support & Enable Technical Tower teams to meet SLA/KPIs and compliance to processes
-	Co-ordination with cross functional teams
-	Vendor coordination for End User equipment’s
