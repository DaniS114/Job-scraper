Job description, responsibilities and duties
We are looking for experienced experienced Linux System Administrator with hands-on experience in managing, maintaining, and optimizing Linux-based server environments. Skilled in supporting production systems, ensuring stability, performance, and availability across on-premise and hybrid infrastructures.
If you are comfortable working with common Linux distributions and performing day-to-day administration tasks, troubleshooting, and automation and familiar with security best practices and compliance-oriented environments, you are the right one for us.
We are a part of international agile team which supports our customers within Raiffeisen group.
We have our offices in Presov and Nitra, but you can work from home/remotely as well.
Core Responsibilities & Skills:
• Administration of Linux servers (RHEL 7, 8, 9 installation, configuration, maintenance)
• System monitoring, performance tuning, and capacity management (Zabbix, Wazuh, Splunk)
• Patch management and system updates (RHEL sat)
• Troubleshooting OS, service, and application issues
• User, group, and permission management
• Backup and recovery operations (TSM)
• Basic to intermediate scripting and automation (Bash, Shell) / (Python, Salt, terraform)
• Documentation of system configurations and operational procedures
Personality requirements and skills
Security & Compliance (Nice to Have):
• Understanding of Linux security hardening and best practices (CIS)
• Familiarity with PCI DSS requirements and compliance principles
• Support of vulnerability remediation and audit activities
• Access control and secure system configuration
Professional Profile:
• Mid-level (mediator) experience with strong Linux fundamentals
• Proactive, reliable, and detail-oriented
• Ability to work independently and within a team
• Continuous learner with interest in security and automation
