Job description, responsibilities and duties
Join a forward-thinking team at the heart of digital transformation in the legal tech space. We’re looking for a skilled Public Cloud Engineer with a strong background in Google Cloud Platform (GCP) to help shape and deliver scalable, secure, and innovative cloud solutions.
As part of our Public Cloud & Identity Platform team, you’ll collaborate across disciplines to build and support enterprise-grade platforms using GCP and Azure. This is a high-impact role where your expertise will directly influence how we deliver services to internal teams and clients globally.
What You’ll Do
Design, build and maintain cloud infrastructure with a focus on automation, scalability and resilience.
Contribute to our ‘Cloud First’ strategy through lifecycle management and service planning.
Define and uphold platform standards, ensuring secure and cost-effective solutions.
Collaborate with developers, architects and stakeholders to deliver high-performing cloud services.
Support incident and problem management, and ensure systems are documented and evergreen.
