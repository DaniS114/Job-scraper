Job description, responsibilities and duties
We’re looking for an experienced Network Security Engineer who’s ready to take ownership of complex security infrastructures in a global automotive environment. In this role, you’ll manage and develop cutting-edge firewall systems (Fortinet, CheckPoint), design and implement security solutions, and play a key part in protecting the digital backbone of an international manufacturing leader.
You’ll collaborate with IT teams and external partners, lead security projects from planning to operation, and ensure the company’s systems remain robust, compliant, and future-ready. If you’re passionate about cybersecurity, thrive on responsibility, and want to make a tangible impact in a high-tech industry, this is your next step forward.
Administration and further development of firewall systems (Check Point, Fortinet)
Ensuring ongoing operations, conducting error analyses, and resolving incidents
Planning, implementation, and operation of security solutions (e.g., Trellix, Crowdstrike, Proxy (Squid, Netskope, Cisco WSA))
Creating and maintaining operational concepts, documentation, and monitoring of responsible systems
Executing projects in the area of network security and lifecycle management
Advising internal projects on network architecture and security requirements
Training and supporting IT colleagues and the service desk in handling security solutions
Coordinating external service providers and preparing for audits
Participating in on-call duty
