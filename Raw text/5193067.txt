Senior Backend Developer - CRM Transformation (f/m)
full-time
Bajkalská 28, Ružinov
4 500 - 4 501 EUR/month
the final salary offered will be agreed together on the basis of your skills and experience
We are the
leading telco company in Slovakia
, currently driving one of the most significant
IT transformation programs
in recent years. We're looking for a
Senior Backend Developer
to lead the technical execution of our
CRM domain transformation
– one of the most complex and critical initiatives in our modernization journey.
You'll be hands-on in
decomposing and replacing our legacy Siebel CRM
with modern
microservices architecture
, building the backbone systems that power our customer operations, sales processes, and service delivery.
This is a
high-impact individual contributor role
for someone who excels at
complex system refactoring
,
data migration
, and building
scalable backend services
in enterprise environments.
What you'll be doing
Leading hands-on development in the
CRM domain transformation
– decomposing monolithic Siebel into
domain-driven microservices
Building core backend services for:
Customer data management
and customer 360° view
Order management
and fulfillment orchestration
Product catalog
and configuration services
Billing integration
and account management
Sales and service process automation
Developing robust
data migration strategies
for transitioning from legacy systems
Implementing
event-driven architecture
using
Kafka
for real-time synchronization between CRM domains and downstream systems
Building
high-performance APIs
that handle millions of daily transactions across multiple consumer systems
Ensuring
data consistency and integrity
across distributed services during the transition period where legacy and new systems coexist
Collaborating with
architects
,
business analysts
, and
domain experts
to understand complex telco business processes and translate them into technical solutions
Optimizing system performance, managing
database schemas
, and ensuring services can scale to handle peak loads
What we're looking for
Expert-level
Java
and
Spring Boot
development skills with proven track record in
enterprise backend systems
Deep experience with
CRM systems
transformation, ideally with knowledge of
Siebel
,
Salesforce
, or similar platforms
Strong expertise in
microservices architecture
,
domain-driven design
, and
distributed systems patterns
Solid experience with
relational databases
(Oracle, PostgreSQL),
data modeling
, and
query optimization
Hands-on experience with
Kafka
or similar event streaming platforms for system integration
Proven track record in
legacy system modernization
and
large-scale data migrations
Experience with
containerization
(Docker, Kubernetes) and
CI/CD pipelines
Understanding of
telco domain
(customer lifecycle, order-to-activation, billing) is a significant advantage
Strong
problem-solving skills
and ability to work with complex, ambiguous requirements
Excellent communication skills to work effectively with both technical teams and business stakeholders
Self-driven mindset with ability to take ownership of large technical initiatives
Nice to have
Experience with
NoSQL databases
for specific use cases
Familiarity with
observability and monitoring tools
(Prometheus, Grafana, ELK stack)
Experience with
performance testing
and optimization tools
Knowledge of
SAFe
,
Scrum
, or other agile methodologies in large-scale environments
Experience with
API gateway patterns
and API management platforms
Background in
DevOps practices
and infrastructure as code
Previous experience
mentoring junior developers
or leading technical initiatives
Experience with
caching strategies

Knowledge of
