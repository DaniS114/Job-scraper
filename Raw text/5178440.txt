About the role:
As a Senior Manual QA Engineer, become a part of a cross-functional development team engineering experiences of tomorrow.
The project focuses on maintaining and extending mission-critical trading systems ensuring stability, scalability, and real-time data integrity. You will work in a cross-functional agile team, closely collaborating with developers, analysts, and DevOps to validate trading workflows, automate test coverage, and ensure consistent quality across high-availability environments.
This role requires strong analytical and technical skills, experience with manual and automated testing of backend and UI components, and a proactive approach to process improvement and test design in complex financial systems.
Responsibilities:
Design, plan, and execute manual and automated test cases for backend services and trading modules
Ensure end-to-end system validation across multiple components and data flows
Review and verify RESTful APIs using tools such as Postman, Swagger, or custom scripts
Build and maintain automated regression test suites (e.g., with XUnit, NUnit, or similar frameworks)
Collaborate with developers to ensure proper test coverage and defect resolution
Participate in sprint planning, backlog grooming, and release validation activities in Jira
Maintain and improve test documentation and procedures in Confluence / AsciiDoc
Perform functional, regression, smoke, and integration testing in complex environments (multiple configurations and components)
Validate network communication (TCP/IP) and ensure correct message flow between trading modules
Support continuous improvement by identifying gaps in processes, documentation, and tooling
Work closely with the QA Lead and product teams to ensure stable, compliant, and high-performing releases
Requirements:
5+ years of experience in QA / Testing, preferably in financial or trading systems
Strong knowledge of software testing principles, including regression, integration, and system testing
Experience with API testing (Postman, Swagger, REST clients)
Solid understanding of SQL and ability to validate data consistency in MSSQL databases
Familiarity with TCP/IP, socket communication, and message-based architectures
Proficiency in Jira (issue tracking, reporting) and Confluence for documentation
Strong analytical mindset and attention to detail
Excellent communication skills and ability to collaborate in a distributed team
Desirable:
Experience with CI/CD integration for testing pipelines (GitLab, Jenkins)
Familiarity with Docker and containerized test environments
Knowledge of performance and load testing tools (JMeter, k6, etc.)
Understanding of message brokers (Solace, Kafka, RabbitMQ)
Hands-on experience in test automation (C#, Python, or Java-based frameworks)
Experience in financial/trading domain testing and regulatory compliance validation
Basic knowledge of monitoring tools for QA in microservice environments
