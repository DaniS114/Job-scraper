Job description, responsibilities and duties
Ready to shape the future of legal tech? Freshfields is launching a new IT hub in Bratislava’s Shared Services Centre (SSC)—and we’re looking for talented professionals to join us. Be part of an innovative, international team working on transformative digital solutions. Build your career with Freshfields and make an impact on a global scale!
Join a global technology team that’s committed to keeping systems secure, data protected, and people empowered. As a Security Vulnerability Analyst, you’ll play a pivotal role in safeguarding our infrastructure, collaborating across teams, and driving continuous improvement in our security operations.
We’re looking for someone who thrives in a dynamic environment, brings deep expertise in vulnerability management, and is passionate about making a meaningful impact. Whether you’re reviewing scan reports, guiding remediation plans, or supporting incident investigations, your work will help shape a resilient and secure future for our firm.
What You’ll Do
Lead day-to-day vulnerability management activities
Maintain and evolve our vulnerability dashboard and reporting
Analyse scan reports, identify risks, and support remediation planning
Collaborate with Information Security Group on emerging threats
Support incident investigations and process improvements
Act as a bridge between security, infrastructure, and project teams
On-call/out of hours rota will be expected as part of the position
