Job description, responsibilities and duties
Pricing Analyst
As integral part of the Commercial Insurance pricing team, the candidate will work closely with the Zurich central actuarial pricing team and support the execution of the pricing work and related pricing projects and business initiatives.
Your role
As a Pricing Analyst, your main responsibilities will involve many of:
work closely with pricing actuaries, data & analytics team, IT teams and partner countries to establish and maintain pricing datasets leveraging central data lake capabilities
supporting and overseeing the data quality work
support the Commercial Insurance pricing team to maintain and annual update of the central pricing platforms, including the pricing models calibration, implementation, and subsequent release & operational management
ensure maintenance of technical documentation and support development of guidance and training material for the underwriting community
completion of pricing performance and trends analyses and deliver those insights back to Product and Market facing teams
support completion of portfolio profitability reviews and portfolios scenario analyses to help the formulation of UW strategies & tactical plans
be part of agile projects for scaling pricing analysis by supporting development of data pipelines and applications

Brief description of the company
Zurich is a leading multi-line insurer that serves its customers in global and local markets. With about 60,000 employees it provides a wide range of property and casualty, life insurance products and services in more than 200 countries and territories. Zurich’s customers include individuals, small businesses, and mid-sized and large companies, as well as multinational corporations.
Zurich Slovakia is a Competence Center based in Bratislava and Kosice providing highly specialized services for Zurich's headquarter in Switzerland and all Zurich Business units around the globe. Our services are focusing on all types of actuarial services – Life and Non-life, pricing and reserving, on data science and engineering, risk management, business intelligence, information technology, software asset management as well as insurance network management and technical accounting. Our Competence Center employs around 400+ staff from 20+ different countries.
