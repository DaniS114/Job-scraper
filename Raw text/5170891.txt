Job description, responsibilities and duties
Ready to take your first step into the world of IT product management? We are looking for a
Junior/Trainee Product Manager
to join our team.
This part-time role is perfect for someone curious, proactive, and eager to learn how products are built, improved, and launched. You’ll work closely with experienced product managers, designers, and developers, gaining hands-on experience while contributing to real product success stories.
What you'll do:
• Collaborate with product teams, designers, developers, and management to bring ideas to life.
​• Help define and refine product requirements, user stories, and acceptance criteria.
​• Prepare meeting agendas, take notes, and follow up on action items.
​• Assist in planning product roadmaps, tracking progress, and reporting results.
​• Support product launches, feature releases, and ongoing product improvements.
​• Maintain and organize product-related documentation.
​• Learn modern product management tools and methods while working alongside talented colleagues.
​
What you’ll learn:
​• How to turn ideas into real products that customers love.
​• How to prioritize features based on business goals and user needs.
​• How to work effectively with cross-functional teams (design, development, marketing).
​• How to use industry-standard product management tools and techniques.
​• How to gather feedback, analyze data, and make informed product decisions.
​• How to manage a product’s lifecycle from concept to launch and beyond.
​
