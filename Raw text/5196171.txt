Senior Automation Engineer
To our Smart Automation Team of digital experts we are searching for a colleague who will join us on our digitalization journey and will support us in delivering smart automation solutions for our customers. If you enjoy working with the latest technologies, want to learn from real experts and like to work in a friendly atmosphere with super cool people, we are the right choice for you. And if you are hardworking, curious, eager to learn and grow, self-driven, responsible and reliable person, you would be the right choice for us.

About
the position
Analysis of customer requirements and design of tailor-made automation solution
Autonomous End2End delivery and development of advanced automation solutions
Apply our automation solution portfolio (e.g. Blue Prism / ABBYY / ChatGPT)
Own, establish and govern the development and operation standards
Initiate and drive R&D for automation solutions like ML/AI, RPA, Smart Data Extraction
Evaluation and realisation of process improvements to ensure cost reduction and increase of efficiency and/or quality.
Deliver consulting and trainings in the area of expertise (Automation/ Digitalization/ Innovation)
Creating and maintaining positive customer relationships to ensure future sales
Presenting automation solutions to customers and management
Leading complex projects
We work in a HYBRID STYLE of working with the possibility of home office, but twice a week we meet in the office for meetings.
The published salary is the minimum possible offer. The starting salary may be higher depending on the extent of fulfillment of the employee's requirements (education, language skills, required practice, personality assumptions and skills).
