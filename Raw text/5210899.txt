Job description, responsibilities and duties
We are looking for experienced Windows System Administrator with solid knowledge of managing and supporting Windows-based enterprise environments. Strong background in server administration, user management, and operational support for business-critical systems.
If you are capable of handling daily administrative tasks, troubleshooting, and system maintenance in structured IT environments and familiar with security standards and compliance-driven operations, you are the right one for us.
We are a part of international agile team which supports our customers within Raiffeisen group.
We have our offices in Presov and Nitra, but you can work from home/remotely as well.
Core Responsibilities & Skills:
• Administration of Windows Server environments (Windows 2k16, 2k22, 2k25)
• Active Directory user, group, and policy management
• Server installation, configuration, and patching (WDS, Terraform)
• Monitoring system health, availability, and performance (Zabbix)
• Troubleshooting OS, service, and application issues
• Backup, restore, and disaster recovery support (TSM)
• Basic to intermediate automation and scripting (PowerShell)
• Creation and maintenance of technical documentation
Personality requirements and skills
Security & Compliance (Nice to Have):
• Knowledge of Windows security best practices and hardening (CIS)
• Awareness of PCI DSS requirements and compliance processes
• Participation in security audits and remediation activities
• Implementation of access controls and security policies
Professional Profile:
• Mid-level (mediator) experience in Windows administration
• Structured and analytical approach to problem-solving
• Strong communication and teamwork skills
• Willingness to expand expertise in security and infrastructure
