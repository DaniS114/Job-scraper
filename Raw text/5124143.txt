POSITION
DESCRIPTION
Are you a passionate Java developer looking for an exciting career opportunity? At Accenture Advanced Technology Center, we offer a dynamic and challenging IT career with a strong focus on application development, work-life balance, and continuous professional growth.
Join us and be part of cutting-edge projects that leverage cloud-first solutions, Agile methodologies, and DevOps principles. Our tech stack is modern, our approach is forward-thinking, and our environment is built for innovation.
WHAT WILL
YOU DO
•	Maintain and enhance a complex legacy Java solution while leading its transition to a modern, scalable architecture using microservices and Spring Boot
•	Design and implement cloud-native solutions (AWS), and contribute to system modularization and performance improvements
•	Stay actively involved in hands-on coding, peer reviews, and problem-solving
•	Drive architectural decisions related to APIs, data flow, scalability, and system integration
•	Champion clean code, testability, and engineering best practices in an Agile/DevOps environment
•	Collaborate with product, QA, and infrastructure teams to ensure technical alignment and value delivery
•	Mentor developers and contribute to the evolution of the tech strategy and engineering culture
WHAT WE
EXPECT
•	An experienced Senior Java Developer (5+ years of experience) with solid backend development skills and a strong grasp of modern engineering principles
•	You already have architecture experience (solution or system-level), or you’ve been partially involved in architecture and are eager to step up and grow into a more strategic role
•	Proficient with Spring Boot, RESTful APIs, and microservice design patterns
•	Experienced with SQL and NoSQL databases, and comfortable working in large-scale distributed environments
•	Skilled in cloud-native development (AWS), containerization, and integration of backend systems
•	A strong communicator and natural mentor who enjoys knowledge sharing and team development
•	University educated (Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in IT or a related field)
•	Advanced in English (B2 level and above)
•	Fluent in Slovak (C2 level)
Nice to have:
•	Experience with frontend technologies (React, Angular, or similar)
•	Familiarity with CI/CD, Docker, Kubernetes, and DevOps practices
•	Exposure to Gen AI or AI-assisted tools in development workflows
•	Previous experience in team or technical leadership roles
