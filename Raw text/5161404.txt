Information about the position
Job description, responsibilities and duties
We are looking for a motivated AI Solutions Developer to join our product development team.
You will help us design and deliver business-ready AI applications that improve productivity and user experience across HR, sales, helpdesk, and other business functions.
Your main focus will be developing chatbots, copilots, and productivity tools in Microsoft Copilot Studio, supported by Azure AI Foundry to orchestrate, manage, and scale AI services. This role is about applying Microsoft AI technologies in practical, value-driven ways, not building custom LLMs.
Primary Responsibilities
Build and deploy AI solutions using Microsoft Copilot Studio.
Use Azure AI Foundry to manage models, prompts, and AI orchestration.
Ensure solutions are reliable, accurate, and intuitive for business users.
Collaborate with stakeholders to translate needs into practical copilots and assistants.
(Optionally) integrate solutions with Power Platform and Microsoft Teams.
