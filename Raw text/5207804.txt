We are looking for a talented developer to join our team and work on ESET Protect, a product trusted by over 100,000 companies worldwide. ESET Protect manages more than 5 million devices, handling tens of thousands of device connections per second. Our ecosystem includes numerous microservices, web applications, endpoint agents and CI/CD pipelines.
Join us to deliver end-to-end features that support system and network administrators globally.
What does working as a full-stack developer on ESET Protect entail?
Collaborating with the team to design, implement, and maintain new features and services.
Discussing potential changes and optimizations to the project's cloud architecture, utilizing cutting-edge cloud concepts and components.
Ensuring code quality by working closely with Senior Developers and our QA Test Engineer.
What do we expect?
Experience with high-level OOP languages and frameworks (we use primarily C# with .NET Core).
Knowledge of version control systems (we use GIT).
Familiarity with cloud concepts and technologies: Azure, Kubernetes, Kafka, Terraform, and Helm.
Experience with databases (we use MS SQL, PostgreSQL (Citus), and Azure Cosmos NoSQL).
Willingness to learn new concepts and technologies.
Willingness to work in Agile environment.
Nice To Have (but not really needed)
Some knowledge of front end frameworks like React.js
