Information about the position
Job description, responsibilities and duties
As a member of Team, you will develop scalable and secure applications that power dynamic financial services. You will design and develop robust solutions, architect integrations and drive CI/CD pipelines with modern DevOps practices. This team is responsible for enabling/disabling Base24 (payment engine) acquiring and for Terminal Management & Configurations ensuring efficient and reliable operations that empower clients and shape the future of digital transactions.
What you will do:
• Enhance our continuous integration, delivery, and deployment pipelines through automation and best DevOps practices.
• Take responsibility for deployments and validation.
• Make continuous improvements to observability, monitoring and alerting.
• Contribute to infrastructure migration and modernization.
• Participate in Agile ceremonies, code reviews and technical discussions.
