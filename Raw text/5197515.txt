Job description, responsibilities and duties
Ready to shape the future of legal tech? Freshfields is launching a new IT hub in Bratislava’s Shared Services Centre (SSC)—and we’re looking for talented professionals to join us. Be part of an innovative, international team working on transformative digital solutions. Build your career with Freshfields and make an impact on a global scale!
Join a forward-thinking technology team where quality, innovation and collaboration drive everything we do. As a Test Lead, you'll play a pivotal role in shaping and enhancing testing practices across our internal delivery teams and third-party partners—ensuring robust, scalable solutions for business workflow automation using tools like UiPath and Power Automate.
This is a hands-on leadership role, ideal for someone who thrives in dynamic environments and is passionate about embedding quality from the ground up. You’ll guide teams, influence strategy, and champion continuous improvement across testing operations.
What You’ll Do:
Lead and uplift testing capabilities across internal teams and suppliers
Define and implement test strategies, frameworks and governance
Support automation adoption and process maturity
Report on quality metrics, risks and progress to senior stakeholders
Provide leadership continuity in the absence of the Test & Environments Manager
