Information about the position
Job description, responsibilities and duties
Are you passionate about building robust, scalable applications that power enterprise data solutions? Join our team as an MDM API/Azure Engineer and leverage your Java expertise to create innovative solutions that drive business value through effective master data management. This role offers the perfect blend of technical challenge and professional growth in a dynamic, collaborative environment.
About the Role
As our MDM API/Azure Engineer, you'll be at the intersection of cloud technology and enterprise data management. You'll architect, build, and maintain API solutions that ensure data consistency, accessibility, and security across our organization.
Key Responsibilities:
- Design and develop Java-based APIs for master data distribution
Implement and optimize RESTful APIs using Spring Boot to facilitate seamless data integration
- Utilize Hibernate for ORM and manage complex database interactions with high performance
- Deploy and manage applications on Azure Cloud, leveraging Azure DevOps for CI/CD pipelines
- Automate infrastructure provisioning and management using Terraform for consistent environments
- Collaborate effectively with cross-functional teams to deliver high-quality software solutions
- Troubleshoot and resolve complex technical issues across the application stack
- Ensure adherence to best practices for security, performance, and scalability
About the Team
We are a dynamic, globally distributed team of analysts and engineers driving the MDM function at Swiss Re. Our expertise spans the entire software development lifecycle, from business analysis and solution design to platform management for nearly 750 master data and reference data sets. As the central hub for all MDM topics, we serve as the primary point of contact for customers, business partners, and enterprise architects, ensuring data consistency and integrity across the organization.
