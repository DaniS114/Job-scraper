Job description, responsibilities and duties
We are looking for a proactive and detail-oriented IT Business Analyst to support two strategic development projects as part of our transition to group-standard solutions under Porsche Bank Austria. The projects include:
A new dealer frontend system for car dealers to offer leasing, financing, and insurance products (target go-live: mid-2026).
Replacement of core systems for product and contract administration (target go-live: 2027).
The consultant will act as a key link between business stakeholders and the development team in Austria, ensuring requirements are clearly defined, documented, and implemented. A critical part of the role will be writing and managing Jira user stories and Confluence documentation.
Key Responsibilities:
Gather, analyze, and document business requirements from stakeholders.
Translate business needs into clear Jira epics, user stories, and acceptance criteria.
Maintain structured and up-to-date Confluence documentation for all project phases.
Facilitate workshops, interviews, and meetings to elicit and validate requirements.
Collaborate closely with developers, testers, and project managers throughout the Software Development Life Cycle.
Support backlog grooming, sprint planning, and UAT activities.
Create process models, user journeys, and use cases.
Ensure traceability of requirements and alignment with business goals.
Identify risks, dependencies, and opportunities for improvement.
Mentor internal project members and transfer knowledge from previous software development projects.
Work primarily on-site in Bratislava, with up to one visit per quarter to HQ in Salzburg.
