Job description, responsibilities and duties
Ready to shape the future of legal tech? Freshfields is launching a new IT hub in Bratislava’s Shared Services Centre (SSC)—and we’re looking for talented professionals to join us. Be part of an innovative, international team working on transformative digital solutions. Build your career with Freshfields and make an impact on a global scale!
Join our Workplace Technology Group, where innovation meets reliability. We’re looking for a Unified Communications Engineer to help shape the future of our communications platforms—supporting telephony, unified messaging, and meeting room systems that are central to our firm’s growth.
What you’ll do:
*Lead and support incident, problem, and change activities across telephony and unified communications technologies.
*Contribute to the design and implementation of meeting room solutions and communication platforms.
*Provide expert guidance and training to technical teams and business stakeholders.
*Ensure systems are secure, reliable, and meet performance targets.
*Collaborate with project and service transition teams to deliver high-quality solutions.
*Maintain accurate documentation and support disaster recovery planning and testing.
