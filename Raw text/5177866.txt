German-Speaking IT Experts for Specialized Data & Project Roles
Job description, responsibilities and duties
Are you a skilled professional fluent in
German
, ready to tackle
complex IT challenges
? We're seeking individuals with strong technical expertise, particularly in areas like data governance, software development, quality management, and IT project management. Join our international team to explore diverse business environments and gain hands-on experience crafting innovative
IT solutions for our German clients
. Your fluency in German and technical skills are not just appreciated—they’re essential to driving success and transformation within our projects.
Why Join Us?
Multifaceted IT Roles:
Engage in projects that require a blend of technical expertise, analytical skills, and strategic consulting.
Collaborative Environment:
Work within teams that prioritize communication and stakeholder engagement.
Global Impact:
Use your German fluency in projects that enhance technological advancement in international markets.
Position Overview:
Step into the heart of IT-driven strategic transformation and innovation. You'll play a crucial role in designing and implementing solutions across major IT projects, helping our clients optimize operations and drive growth. We offer a variety of roles depending on your experience and interests:
Data Governance & Quality:
Data & AI Organization Specialist:
Focuses on developing data governance frameworks and promoting data literacy through tools like Collibra and Informatica.
Data Quality Specialist:
Designs and implements robust data quality frameworks, utilizing AI processes for improved governance. Integrate data quality functionalities in platforms like Databricks, Snowflake, and BigQuery.
Software Testing:
Test Manager:
Oversees the management of testing strategies and ensures quality assurance, utilizing automation tools like Selenium and Azure DevOps.
Test Analysis Manager:
Leads comprehensive test strategies and execution, focusing on enhancing team coordination and managing risks. Using tools such as SOAPUI and Jira for efficient test management.
Analysis and Project Management:
Business / Tech Analyst:
Utilizes knowledge in IT sectors within banking and insurance to perform process analysis and technical specifications. Experience with platforms like AWS and Microsoft Azure is advantageous.
Project Manager:
Manages scope, budget, and risks in IT projects, with strong organizational skills and leveraging tools such as Jira for effective stakeholder engagement.
Architecture:
Architect:
Designs enterprise solutions with a focus on robust architectural planning, using technologies like AWS and Azure. Experience in designing through UML and TOGAF certification is essential.

apply now

Employee perks, benefits
Benefits you can enjoy while working at PwC:
Financial Rewards:
Benefit from regular salary reviews, performance bonuses and other special bonuses. Enjoy meal contributions and a cafeteria benefit system offering personalized choices.
Flexibility & Work-Life Balance:
Enjoy our hybrid working policy, flexible working hours, 2 extra days off during Christmas along with Sick-days. Every minute of your extra work is valued and compensated.
Career Development:
Engage in tailored internal and external training programs, benefit from a structured career development plan, and as an employee take advantage of discounts of up to 30% on internationally recognized certifications and qualifications from leading organizations through our PwC Academy.
Health & Well-being:
Access premium benefits such as physiotherapy consultations, Multisport card and medical center membership. Take advantage of our Well-being and Work-Life Coaching program.
Supportive Culture:
Participate in teambuilding activities and social events and join our employee referral program. Experience a culture with regular feedback and a supportive environment.
Global Opportunities:
Seize the opportunity for international assignments and work on diverse projects with international clients. Explore the world while working with the possibility of working from abroad.
Extra perks:
Use various discounts at partner companies such as cars, health care, beauty, culture or others. Transfer up to 4 telephone numbers at discounted rates.
