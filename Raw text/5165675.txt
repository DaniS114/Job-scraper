POSITION
DESCRIPTION
Are you ready to kickstart your IT career and grow into a future specialist in low-code and digital process automation?
With us, you’ll work on long-term projects for top clients in industries like banking and financial services, gaining hands-on exposure to real enterprise environments.
From day one, you’ll enter a structured Pega career path — starting with paid certification, guided mentoring, and practical project experience. Pega is a global leader in model-driven, low-code platforms that enable full lifecycle development — from design, through build, to deployment and operations. You’ll learn how to deliver end-to-end enterprise solutions while building highly transferable skills in integration, case management, and digital automation.
Whether you want to grow into a Pega System Architect, Low-Code Solution Designer, or Technology Consultant, we’ll help you shape a career that fits your ambitions.
WHAT WILL
YOU DO
•	Learn directly from experienced Pega developers and architects, actively contributing to software projects from requirements analysis to technical design.
•	Build enterprise applications using the Pega low-code platform across all phases — development, deployment, operation, and monitoring.
•	Write clean, maintainable code when required, supporting integrations, extensions, and testing.
•	Contribute to the maintenance and improvement of existing solutions by analyzing and resolving issues.
•	Work in an agile team environment, sharing ideas, experimenting, and delivering value iteratively.
WHAT WE
EXPECT
•	Basic knowledge of at least one object-oriented language (e.g., Java, C#, .NET, C++, JavaScript, Python)
•       Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in IT or a related field
•	Understanding of SQL and database concepts
•	Familiarity with software testing principles
•	Analytical and creative problem-solving skills, with curiosity to learn new tools and approaches
•	Team spirit and willingness to contribute ideas
•	English language proficiency at Upper intermediate (B2) or advanced (C1) level and Slovak language at (C2) Proficiency
