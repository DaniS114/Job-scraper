About the Job
As an Application Maintenance Specialist at Allianz Technology, you will be instrumental in supporting and enhancing our suite of products tailored for the Central and Eastern Europe (CEE) region. Your role will focus on ensuring the stability, performance, and continuous improvement of these applications, directly impacting the efficiency and satisfaction of our customers in the CEE market.
What you do
Act as the primary point of contact for application maintenance inquiries related to Allianz Technology products in the CEE region, ensuring effective communication with stakeholders.
Monitor the performance and availability of applications specific to the CEE region, proactively identifying and resolving issues to minimize downtime and disruption.
Collaborate with cross-functional teams to implement changes, updates, and enhancements to applications, ensuring they align with the unique needs of the CEE market.
Manage incident resolution processes, including troubleshooting, root cause analysis, and implementing corrective actions for CEE-specific applications.
Maintain detailed documentation of application configurations, changes, and maintenance activities relevant to the CEE region.
Support the integration of new applications into the existing landscape, focusing on the seamless transition and minimal impact on users in the CEE region.
Participate in regular reviews of application performance metrics and suggest improvements to optimize functionality and user experience for CEE customers.
