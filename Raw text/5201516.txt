POSITION
DESCRIPTION
Are you interested in AI and ready to take the next step in your career? We’re looking for motivated professionals who have experience with AI and want to deepen their expertise. As an AI Adoption Consultant, you’ll have the opportunity to learn from industry leaders, work on diverse projects, and develop your skills in a supportive environment.
WHAT WILL
YOU DO
• Participate in AI adoption workshops and learn how to deliver practical guidance to clients
• Help assess organizational readiness and identify high-impact use cases for AI
• Support the creation of actionable adoption plans and learn to manage change initiatives
• Collaborate with experienced colleagues to build networks of champions and managers
• Track adoption metrics, analyze progress, and learn to report results
• Gain experience in addressing resistance and promoting responsible, safe use of AI
WHAT WE
EXPECT
• up to 5 years of experience in consulting, transformation or change enablement
• Exposure to Microsoft 365 or Copilot adoption is a plus
• Interest in stakeholder engagement and communications
• Willingness to learn about AI transformation and skilling
• Openness to working with Prosci or similar frameworks
• Excellent communication and documentation skills for technical and executive stakeholders
• Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in IT or a related field
• English and German language proficiency at Upper intermediate (B2) or Advanced (C1)
Nice to have:
• Prosci Certified Change Practitioner certification
• Microsoft Copilot credentials
