What can you expect?
Adient (NYSE: ADNT) is a global leader in automotive seating. With more than 65,000 employees in 29 countries, Adient operates ~200 manufacturing/assembly plants worldwide. We produce and deliver automotive seating for all major OEMs. From complete seating systems to individual components, our expertise spans every step of the automotive seat-making process. We take our products from research and design to engineering and manufacturing — and into millions of vehicles every year. For more information, please visit www.adient.com
We are seeking a Data Scientist to drive advanced analytics and unlock insights from our IT Manufacturing Data Warehouses.
At Adient, we are proud to offer hybrid working to our employees, which involves being able to work remotely for up to 2 days per week based on the agreement within specific department.
What will your responsibilities be?
•	Advanced Analytics & Modeling: Apply statistical methods and machine learning algorithms to identify trends, predict outcomes, and optimize processes.
•	Data Visualization & Storytelling: Build impactful dashboards and reports using Power BI and Grafana, enabling clear communication of insights.
•	Data Pipeline Development: Design and maintain robust ETL processes to ensure timely and accurate data availability.
•	Data Quality & Governance: Validate and monitor data integrity across systems, implementing best practices for reliability.
•	Innovation with AI/ML: Explore and implement AI/ML use cases to drive continuous improvement in manufacturing and business operations.
•	Collaboration & Stakeholder Engagement: Work with cross-functional teams to understand business needs and deliver data-driven solutions.
Which qualifications are we looking for in potential employees?
•	Master’s degree in Computer Science, Data Science, Statistics, or a related field.
•	Proven experience in data analytics, data engineering, or business intelligence, with exposure to machine learning and predictive modeling.
•	Programming for Analytics: Skilled in Python for data analysis, machine learning, and automation; familiarity with .NET (C#) for enterprise integration.
•	Advanced SQL Expertise: Strong knowledge of Transact-SQL (T-SQL) for complex queries and performance optimization.
•	Data Integration & Modeling: Experience with SSIS and SSAS for ETL and multidimensional modeling.
•	Cloud & DevOps Practices: Hands-on experience with Azure DevOps Agile processes (Boards, Backlogs, Sprints) and CI/CD pipelines for data solutions.
•	Modern Architectures: Knowledge of microservices and containerization using Docker & Kubernetes for scalable data applications.
Preferred Mindset
•	Data Science Mindset: Ability to transform raw data into insights, leveraging statistical methods, predictive modeling, and advanced analytics.
•	Strong understanding of business processes and ability to connect data insights to operational goals.
•	Clear communicator who can translate technical findings into business language.
•	Innovative and forward-thinking, eager to explore emerging technologies.

Brief description of the company
Adient is the leading automotive seating supplier.
With around 3000 employees and five locations in Slovakia, the company is one of the key employers in Slovakia’s automotive industry. We are committed to Sustainability and are proud ambassador of Diversity Charter in Slovakia since 2017.
Adient’s only business center worldwide is in Bratislava. Approximately 800 employees in Bratislava support the company’s business around the globe in finance, information technology, purchasing, sales, human resources, transport and logistics and project management.
In Trencin, Adient operates the biggest automotive technology center across Slovakia. More than 400 employees there develop a wide range of highly innovative products for the cars of tomorrow.
In Zilina, the company produces complete seat systems and automotive fabrics, in Lucenec foam components for seats as well as metal components for head restraints.
Adient is a global leader in automotive seating. With approximately 70 000 employees in 30 countries, Adient operates 200 manufacturing/assembly plants worldwide, we produce and deliver automotive seating for all vehicle classes and all major OEMs. From complete seating systems to individual components, our expertise spans every step of the automotive seat-making process. Our integrated, in-house skills allow us to take our products from research and design all the way to engineering and manufacturing – and into more than 20 million cars every year.
