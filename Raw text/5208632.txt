Job description, responsibilities and duties
We are looking for a
Junior Data Analyst
to join our International Education Research team. This role is a great fit for a
graduate or someone early in their career
who enjoys working with data and wants to learn how research supports real decisions in education and student mobility.
You will work with data on study destinations, student markets, and global education trends. Most projects are international, and you will learn by working closely with more experienced analysts. You do not need years of experience, curiosity, attention to detail, and a willingness to learn matter most.
What you will be doing
Working      with data related to international education and student mobility
Supporting      research projects using both desk research and datasets
Finding      patterns and trends in the data and helping turn them into insights
Creating      charts, tables, and short written summaries
Helping      with reports and presentations for clients
Collaborating      with colleagues across the team
Occasionally      joining client meetings, events, or presentations

BONARD is a leading consultancy with a strong reputation in the international student sector and student accommodation real estate.
