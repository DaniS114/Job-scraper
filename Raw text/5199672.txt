We are seeking an experienced
Senior DevOps Engineer
to participate in the design, development and operation of a modern
Kubernetes-based Platform-as-a-Service
for a new Open Sovereign Cloud product. The focus is on high-sovereignty cloud environments for regulated sectors such as healthcare, security, and public administration.
You will take part in the implementation of sovereignty controls—encryption, identity management, platform security—and contribute to shaping the architecture and automation of a cloud platform fully operated within the EU.

CI/CD pipelines
