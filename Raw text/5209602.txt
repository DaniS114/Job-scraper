Job description, responsibilities and duties
Would you like to be part of a well-established, fast-growing management consulting company based in Bratislava? Take the opportunity to work on diverse international projects, develop your expertise in project management, and strengthen your IT skills. Join a team that values innovation, collaboration, and a hands-on approach — without the feel of a large corporate environment.
YOUR TASKS:
Master operational excellence of our clients, drive digital transformation efforts and ensure smooth implementation of new banking systems and solutions for the biggest industry players.
One of the key responsibilities is the parameterization and development of core functionalities within the Avaloq banking system.
Work on the frontline and assist with all phases of the project life cycle i.e. analysis, requirements, planning, development, testing and deployment to ensure project deliverables meet defined scope
Apply strategic and critical thinking to develop recommendations that address client challenges
Conduct workshops with the client to collect requirements
Help client in defining user cases
Respond to, and handle complex issues in testing and production
Gather requirements relating to the integration of new systems
Be in close contact and interact directly with our customers
Willingness to travel internationally to engage with clients and stakeholders at various levels.

Synpulse is an internationally established management consulting company with 29+ years of experience and with offices in the United Kingdom, Switzerland, Germany, Austria, Slovakia, USA, Singapore, Australia, the Philippines and more.
