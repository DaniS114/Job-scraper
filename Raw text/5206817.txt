POSITION
DESCRIPTION
Accenture Technology Solutions offers the opportunity to work on international integration projects, build hands-on expertise with modern low-code platforms, and collaborate with skilled professionals across global teams. As a Senior Integration Developer, you will take responsibility for delivering integration components, improving platform stability, and contributing to continuous enhancements.
WHAT WILL
YOU DO
• Lead the design and deployment of integration solutions using low-code platforms
• Evaluate and refine existing system architecture
• Drive code quality and standards, overseeing development practices and reviews
• Enhance existing integrations and improve development standards
• Collaborate closely with product owners, architects, and cross-functional teams to align business and technical goals
• Mentor junior developers, promote best practices, and support knowledge transfer across teams
• Actively contribute to agile processes and continuous improvement initiatives
WHAT WE
EXPECT
• 5+ years of experience with integration development or low-code platforms (e.g., MuleSoft, Pega, WSO2)
• Proficient in REST/SOAP APIs and SQL
• Exposure to XML technologies (XSD, XSLT, XPath)
• Experience in Agile teams and DevOps practices
• A technically driven professional who takes ownership, mentors others, and ensures delivery quality at scale
• Excellent communication and problem-solving skills
• Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in IT or a related field
• English language proficiency at Upper intermediate (B2) or advanced (C1) level
