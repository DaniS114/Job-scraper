Information about the position
Job description, responsibilities and duties
️  CME Group is looking for a skilled and detail-oriented Data Engineer / Analyst to join our data and analytics team. If you're passionate about data and have a knack for innovative ideas, keep reading!
️  In this role, you will help design, build, and maintain the data pipelines and systems that power insights across our media and business operations. Creativity is one of our core values, and in the realm of learning, it’s even more crucial.
️  You will combine strong technical expertise with analytical thinking to deliver reliable, high-quality data that supports decision-making across all CME companies.
Who we are? https://www.cme.net/
What You Will Be Responsible For?
Data Engineering
• Build, maintain, and optimize data pipelines and workflows for efficient data processing.
• Integrate and manage data from multiple internal and external sources.
• Ensure data quality, consistency, and security across platforms.
• Automate data processes and monitor performance of data infrastructure.
• Collaborate with IT teams to manage access, storage, and security.
Data Analysis
• Analyze structured and unstructured data to generate actionable business and audience insights.
• Prepare and visualize data for internal and external reporting.
• Contribute to improving data models, systems, and reporting tools.
Collaboration
• Work closely with other data engineers, analysts, and business teams to ensure accurate and available data.
• Support reporting and analytical initiatives across departments.
