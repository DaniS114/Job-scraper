Job description, responsibilities and duties
Do you have all the skills needed for navigating a project in IT development? If you can set clear goals and steps to achieve them and you are interested in leading a newly created team to a success, this position is exactly for you!
As a Senior Business Analyst, you will strongly contribute to vision and the development of a quality IT product, coordinate the project, create processes and communicate with developers and managers.
What will you do?
• Actively manage the project in an agile environment and ensure its successful completion.
• Manage and distribute the work capacities of team members.
• Design and monitor compliance with team and project goals.
• Create clear dashboards to track project activities and reports for managers.
• Assist in removing obstacles identified by developers and testers.
• Participate in planning and leading daily meetings and other work meetings.
• Actively participate in solving technical tasks and problems.
• Maintain up-to-date information on the progress of the project and record individual phases in MS Azure DevOps.
• Communicate with internal customers to ensure the correct direction of the project, development, testing and completeness of the product based on requirements.
• Oversee compliance with established plans, processes and deadlines.
