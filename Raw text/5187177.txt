Job description, responsibilities and duties
We are looking for a
Product Owner
who will play a key role in driving the success of our
B2B
product offering by ensuring that development efforts are tightly aligned with customer needs and strategic business goals. You will manage the product backlog, translating complex business requirements into clear, actionable items for the development team.
You will act as
the voice of the customer
, representing end users such as fleet managers, dispatchers, financial controllers, or operational leads, and ensure the product consistently delivers value in
real-world use cases
.
The EW Navigation App is already used by over 240k drivers each month
, and the B2B segment represents the highest revenue driver within the navigation domain. It will be your main responsibility to ensure it continues to serve business users efficiently while supporting the company’s broader commercial goals and also to
shape the long-term product vision
, enabling scalable growth, customer retention, and differentiation in a competitive B2B landscape.
Job requirements:
Proven experience as a Product Owner or preferably in a B2B product role
Proven ability to manage a product backlog and write clear, actionable user stories
Experience working in agile environments, including Scrum or SAFe frameworks
Familiarity with product management and agile tools (JIRA Product Discovery, JIRA, Confluence, Figma, Google Analytics, Looker Studio)
Understanding of UX principles and customer journey mapping
Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
Excellent verbal and written communication in English
Ability to work with cross-functional teams and influence decision-making without direct authority
Technical knowledge on device APIs in Android ecosystems
​
Job responsibilities:
​
Lead the continuous Product Discovery process
Define and prioritize the product backlog in alignment with business goals and customer needs
Translate product strategy and customer insights into clear user stories and acceptance criteria
Collaborate with development teams in EW Navigation App and Navigation Engine to ensure shared understanding of product requirements
Make decisions on product scope and priorities within each sprint cycle
Ensure features delivered meet the agreed acceptance criteria and deliver business value
Communicate product vision and goals to marketing, commercial and development teams
Gather and analyze feedback from users and stakeholders to drive continuous product improvement
Work with UX/UI teams to ensure user-centric design and optimal customer experience
Define and watch product metrics through creation of dashboards to track user behavior, product performance, engagement and fulfillment of the objectives
Participate in sprint planning, stand-ups, and retrospectives to support agile development processes

Brief description of the company
We are Sygic and we have been changing the way people travel since 2004.
We are known for the Sygic GPS Navigation mobile application, the first navigation app on the Appstore and the second on Google Play, but today we are much more! After all these years, we extended, but the focus and start-up mentality remain.
Our users are considered part of our development family. We listen to their suggestions and try to ensure that our product still brings them added value.
As part of the Eurowag Group, which comprises a dynamic portfolio of innovative companies, including us – Sygic but also fireTMS, Inelo, WebEye, Princip, KomTeS, and CVS, we collectively play pivotal roles in creating sustainable financial and technological solutions. Together we work to simplify the life of fleet companies worldwide.
At Eurowag Group, we are committed to creating a diverse and inclusive workplace. We encourage women, men, and individuals of all backgrounds to apply and join our team.
