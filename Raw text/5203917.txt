Job description, responsibilities and duties
Job description, responsibilities and duties
We are looking for test automation professional for product portfolio aiming at optimizing efficiency and sustainability of commercial buildings – offices, hospitals, hotel chains, etc. We are part of international team implementing scalable, secure solution with attractive look & feel which is now starting to be used across the world by our customers – building owners, building operators and facility managers.
Check our web site here:
https://xcelerator.siemens.com/global/en/products/buildings/building-x.html
In this role you will be responsible for:
Definition of test cases based on acceptance criteria for features and user stories
Implementation of automated test cases for functional and non-functional requirements
Creation and maintenance of automated test suite
Reporting of test execution results within the development team and towards test manager
Issue tracking
We expect:
Development background or experience with implementation of automated tests
Mandatory skills:
Practical experience with test automation frameworks (e.g. Cypress, Selenium)
Working experience with GIT and issue tracking system (JIRA, JAZZ, etc.)
Nice to have skills:
ISTQB certification
Working experience with SCM (e.g. GIT)
Experience with working in agile environment
Personality requirements and skills:
Good analytical and problem-solving skills
Good written and verbal communication skills
