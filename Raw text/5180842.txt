Job description, responsibilities and duties
Monthly Gross Salary Starting From €2,400
Join a forward-thinking team where your expertise in network security will help shape and safeguard the digital infrastructure of a global firm. We’re looking for a skilled Network Security Engineer to design, implement and manage cutting-edge security solutions across cloud and on-premise environments.
What you’ll do:
Lead the design and deployment of secure network solutions using Cisco ISE, Catalyst Center, and Panorama.
Configure and maintain Palo Alto and Zscaler platforms to ensure robust protection.
Develop cloud network security strategies across AWS, Azure or Google Cloud.
Monitor and respond to security incidents, conduct audits, and implement improvements.
Collaborate across teams to embed security best practices and policies.
What you’ll bring:
Proven experience in network security engineering, with strong knowledge of Cisco SDA, Palo Alto Firewalls, and Zscaler.
Familiarity with cloud security tools and protocols.
Relevant qualifications (e.g. CCNP Security, PCNSE, CISSP) are advantageous.
A proactive, analytical mindset with excellent communication and collaboration skills.
What we offer:
A people-first culture that values empathy, integrity, and collaboration.
Opportunities for professional development, certification support, and career progression.
A flexible and inclusive working environment where diverse perspectives are welcomed.
The chance to work with leading technologies and contribute to meaningful change.
We welcome applications from all backgrounds and communities. If you’re passionate about network security and thrive in a dynamic, team-oriented setting, we’d love to hear from you.
