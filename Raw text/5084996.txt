Job description, responsibilities and duties
As a
Senior Cloud Infrastructure Engineer
, you will play a key role in designing, deploying, and enhancing a hybrid IT infrastructure that bridges on-premises systems with cloud-based services. You will oversee enterprise-level architecture, manage virtualization platforms, cloud environments, identity solutions, and automation tools essential to the company’s daily operations. This position demands deep expertise in Microsoft technologies, Azure services, and modern infrastructure automation practices.
Key Duties and Responsibilities:
• Design and oversee hybrid cloud architectures that integrate on-prem infrastructure with Azure cloud solutions
• Build and deploy virtualization systems using Hyper-V, VMware, or other enterprise platforms
• Create and maintain automation tools leveraging PowerShell, Azure Automation, and Infrastructure as Code (IaC) principles
• Manage and enhance Active Directory and Entra ID (Azure AD) environments
• Configure and administer Microsoft 365 services such as Exchange Online, SharePoint, and Teams
• Enforce and maintain security best practices throughout all layers of hybrid infrastructure
• Maintain and optimize Microsoft SQL Server platforms and related database systems
• Develop disaster recovery and business continuity strategies that cover both cloud and on-prem environments
• Lead and coordinate complex infrastructure initiatives, offering technical leadership to junior staff
• Establish and document infrastructure standards, architectural patterns, and technical guidelines
• Monitor infrastructure performance and support capacity planning activities
• Provide advanced troubleshooting and resolution for critical infrastructure challenges
