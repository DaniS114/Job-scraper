Information about the position
Job description, responsibilities and duties
Join our product team and be the bridge between business and technology in this exciting role! Are you passionate about transforming business requirements into impactful digital solutions? Join our dynamic team in Bratislava where you'll be at the heart of developing and maintaining our sophisticated in-house commercial insurance products, driving real business impact through innovative functionality!
About the Role
In this pivotal role, you'll bridge the gap between business needs and technical implementation, ensuring our insurance products deliver exceptional value. You'll collaborate with cross-functional teams to translate complex requirements into clear specifications that guide our development process.
Key Responsibilities
•	Analyze and clarify business requirements and translate them into functional and technical specifications including business architecture, process maps, wireframes and data models
•	Ensure comprehensive specification of User Stories with all required data and background analysis available to the entire team
•	Design and define related test cases and support the Product Owner in creating effective acceptance criteria
•	Collaborate cross-functionally with Platform Product Owners, Technology Leads, Business Process Managers, Business Architecture and the Data Office to maintain end-to-end consistency of system, process and information architecture
•	Anticipate and analyze customer needs to develop effective problem-solving solutions
•	Organize and prioritize the Product Backlog based on Product Owner guidance
•	Own production support for one or more business applications, addressing user requests directly or triaging more complex cases to the development team
•	Create and maintain comprehensive project documentation on the corporate Wiki
About the Team
Our team is responsible for building products and solutions for the Corporate Solutions business unit. We work in a collaborative, innovative environment where your ideas will have real impact. We partner closely with colleagues around the globe, fostering a diverse and inclusive atmosphere where different perspectives help shape exceptional insurance products. As part of our team, you'll contribute to meaningful projects while developing your skills in a supportive, growth-oriented culture.
