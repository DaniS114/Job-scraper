Job description, responsibilities and duties
Your Role
As a ServiceNow / HALO Developer, you will design, build, and enhance digital workflows that make IT and business processes smarter and more efficient.
You’ll collaborate with consultants and project managers to turn requirements into working solutions — using low-code, scripting, and creative problem solving.
This is a hands-on role with plenty of space to grow, learn, and make visible impact in an international environment.
Key Responsibilities
Development & Implementation
Develop      and configure applications, modules, and integrations within ServiceNow or      HALO
Implement      automation and workflows using low-code/no-code tools or JavaScript      scripting
Test,      document, and deploy high-quality solutions according to design      specifications
Collaboration & Support
Work      closely with process consultants to understand business needs and      translate them into technical deliverables
Troubleshoot      and resolve application issues; support release and upgrade activities
Contribute      to continuous improvement of development standards and internal know-how
Innovation & Learning
Stay      updated with new features and capabilities in ServiceNow and HALO
Explore      ways to enhance platform usability and efficiency through creative      technical approaches
