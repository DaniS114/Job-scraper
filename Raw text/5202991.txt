Information about the position
Job description, responsibilities and duties
We are looking for a .NET Developer to join our Weigh In Motion (WIM) team developing the KiTraffic Digital product, the world’s most advanced digital WIM platform powered by cutting-edge sensors and precision engineering to deliver accurate real-time weight measurements of vehicles moving at any speed.
Based on various product lifecycle phases following responsibilities will arise:
Determining the technical environments
Working with the business roles to formulate and communicate solution options
Working with the business roles and other developers to evaluate technical options and, at a high level, decide the best way to fulfil the business needs
Provide estimations for effort and identify risks detailed enough for release planning
Creation of software architecture and software design and align it with system architecture
Identifying and owning architectural and other technically based risk
Advising on the achievability of non-functional requirements
Provide effort estimation of backlog items and make sure these estimations are up-to date and reliable
Programming and testing backlog items according to Definition of Done
Bug fixing of an existing functionality
Ensuring continuous systems integration
Works collaboratively with other technical roles on development infrastructure and automation
Participates in quality assurance work to ensure products are fit for purpose
Ensuring that technical debts are visible and prioritized appropriately in Product Backlog in collaboration with Product Owner
Promoting appropriate standards of technical best practices
Approving the solution is technically fit for purpose prior to deployment
Managing technical aspects of the transition of the solution into live use
Join us and be part of the team driving innovation with the
KiTraffic Digital product
- a solution that is ideal for many applications beyond WIM:
direct weight and tire enforcement
pre-selection of overloaded vehicles
infrastructure design and maintenance of roads and bridges
