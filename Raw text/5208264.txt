Job description, responsibilities and duties
We are looking for an
experienced B2B Account Manager with a  telecom background
to join our client’s growing international team in Bratislava. This is a
highly customer-facing, dynamic role
where you will manage global corporate clients and actively drive new business opportunities across advanced connectivity and IT solutions.
If you understand how networks really work — from
Dedicated Internet, Broadband,
VPNs and MPLS to 4G/5G, IoT, jitter, and latency
— and enjoy building long-term client partnerships, this role is for you.
Working hours: 3:00 pm-11:30 pm
Working model: 3x per week in the office (Bratislava)
Start date: as soon as possible
Interview process: 2 rounds of online interview
Your responsibilities
Acquire new business opportunities and participate in bidding processes (RFPs)
Build and maintain strong, long-term client relationships
Manage assigned corporate clients worldwide (across multiple time zones)
Handle daily communication with customers in a B2B environment
Prepare pricing offers and review existing pricing models
Manage MACD pricing models and their updates
Collect and analyze data, monitor client account activities
Review contracts and service documentation
Coordinate internal teams to resolve escalations in a timely and efficient manner
Ensure effective use of internal resources
Prepare reports and support ad-hoc business requirements
