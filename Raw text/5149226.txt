What can you expect?
Adient (NYSE: ADNT) is a global leader in automotive seating. With 70,000+ employees in 29 countries, Adient operates more than 200 manufacturing/assembly plants worldwide. What will your responsibilities be?
•	Leads API development activities and ensures quality through:
o	Functional requirement reviews and signoff
o	Impact analysis documentation
o	Solution architecture options and cost analysis preparation
o	Technical design reviews and signoff
o	Unit test plan reviews and signoff
•	Acts as SME for API platforms and interfaces with external applications, contributing to:
o	API development design and deliverables
o	Production support analysis for critical and complex issues
•	Collaborates with application teams to provide insights on functional and technical design activities. Drives high-quality specifications and requirements.
•	Leads proof of concept (POC) initiatives and technical roadmap activities, ensuring new developments align with platform strategy.
•	Aligns API development efforts with Enterprise Architecture (EA) direction through close collaboration.
•	Prepares and presents API solution proposals with multiple implementation options to IT Governance and Strategy teams when requirements involve:
o	Change in System Architecture
o	Change in API based integrations to external/internal and cloud-based applications
•	Keeps current with API management, gateway technologies, APIGEE, and integration best practices.
•	Executes POCs, documents outcomes, and drives implementation and standardization within API integration.
•	Looks for cost saving / quality improvement opportunities
•	Provides multiple solutions to business problems, analyzes each option, highlights pros and cons, and recommends the best approach.
•	Presents the potential solutions / enhancements to both technical and nontechnical audiences

Brief description of the company
Adient is the leading automotive seating supplier.
With around 3000 employees and five locations in Slovakia, the company is one of the key employers in Slovakia’s automotive industry. We are committed to Sustainability and are proud ambassador of Diversity Charter in Slovakia since 2017.
Adient’s only business center worldwide is in Bratislava. Approximately 800 employees in Bratislava support the company’s business around the globe in finance, information technology, purchasing, sales, human resources, transport and logistics and project management.
In Trencin, Adient operates the biggest automotive technology center across Slovakia. More than 400 employees there develop a wide range of highly innovative products for the cars of tomorrow.
In Zilina, the company produces complete seat systems and automotive fabrics, in Lucenec foam components for seats as well as metal components for head restraints.
Adient is a global leader in automotive seating. With approximately 70 000 employees in 30 countries, Adient operates 200 manufacturing/assembly plants worldwide, we produce and deliver automotive seating for all vehicle classes and all major OEMs. From complete seating systems to individual components, our expertise spans every step of the automotive seat-making process. Our integrated, in-house skills allow us to take our products from research and design all the way to engineering and manufacturing – and into more than 20 million cars every year.
