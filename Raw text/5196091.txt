Digital Analyst - medior
Place of work
Bratislava, Slovakia (Job with occasional home office)
Wage (gross)
From 2 300 EUR/month
*Performance bonus 2 times per year up to 10% of the basic salary paid for the evaluation period (usually 6 months).
Contract type
full-time
As a Digital Analyst, you will be a key member of the Global Digital Analytics team, analyzing digital data to identify opportunities and recommend improvements for digital business performance. This role involves creating reports and dashboards on key metrics, evaluating online campaign performance, and providing actionable insights, along with conducting ad-hoc analyses to identify trends.
Managing web analytics tools to ensure accurate tracking and seamless integration with marketing platforms is a key responsibility. The Digital Analyst defines technical specifications for analytics platforms, collaborates with development teams, and educates internal teams on best practices.
A core responsibility is generating insights that inform strategic decisions, evaluating online initiatives, identifying trends, and providing actionable recommendations for improvements.
In this role you will:
Analyze web analytics, along with quantitative and qualitative data, to identify opportunities and recommend improvements to website performance
Gather requirements for reporting needs then design and create reports and dashboards on traffic, conversion, sales, and other online customer behavior metrics
Design and automate reports to reduce repetitive manual tasks and enhance efficiency
Evaluate the performance of online campaigns, provide insights and actionable recommendations for improvement
Conduct ad-hoc analyses on key trends and provide recommendations based on the findings
Oversee the setup and maintenance of web analytics tools and marketing platforms, ensuring seamless connections. Manage user access and account structures as needed
Monitor key metrics, review automated alerts for anomalies, analyze outputs to identify potential issues, and contribute to their resolution
Manage tracking tags in a tag management tool, ensuring all marketing and advertising platforms capture accurate data
Define technical specifications for web analytics and tag management platforms, communicate requirements to development teams, and verify implementation accuracy before launch
Educate internal teams and regions on web analytics, data interpretation, and tracking best practices to support informed decision-making
Generate and utilize quantitative and qualitative research data, translating into actionable recommendations on website optimization or A/B testing ideas
Process large datasets, extract actionable insights using advanced data processing tools and techniques
Integrate diverse data sources to enable a comprehensive understanding and optimal utilization of available data in steering the business direction. Be responsible for the accurate utilization and comprehension of sales data integrated with web analytics data
Analyze business intelligence data, interpret findings, and deliver actionable recommendations to support business growth.
Continuously develop knowledge and skills and keep up-to-date with new trends and information in the field of expertise
Required experience and skills:
At least 4 years of experience in a similar role
Proficiency in English at B2 level or higher
A proactive, "can-do" attitude, with a curious and analytical mindset
Strong quantitative and qualitative analytical skills or strong Google Tag Manager experience with technical implementation experience
Good knowledge of Google Analytics or similar web analytics tool
Good knowledge of Google Tag Manager or other tagging platform
Experience with Google Platform products and data connection between these tools
Ability to translate business requirements into KPIS and metrics
Solid analytical skills, capable of finding insights in diverse data sets and a solid grasp of statistics
Experience using data and metrics to draw business insights
Good understanding of online marketing principles, web design and user experience, e-commerce, SEO, SEM, PPC and CRO best practices
Technical acumen and experience with web technologies such as HTML, JavaScript
Strong personal preference on data-driven decision making
Proficiency in Microsoft Excel
Preferred Qualifications (a plus):
Google Analytics Individual Qualification certification or similar web analytics certification
Certification in Google Tag Manager or other tag management platforms
Experience with Google BigQuery or SQL certification
Experience with BI reporting tools
