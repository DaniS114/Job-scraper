Job description, responsibilities and duties
Are you passionate about building strong information security environments and driving risk management excellence?
As an Information Security and Risk Advisor Expert, you will help design, assess, and improve our local Information Security and Risk Management framework while contributing to global initiatives. Your mission: ensure security, continuity, and compliance while staying ahead of emerging risks and challenges.
Your key responsibilities will be:
Develop and implement a Yearly Information Security Risk Program aligned with corporate and local requirements, including defined actions and timelines.
Conduct Local Information Risk Assessments to uncover threats, propose mitigation strategies, and implement enhanced security measures—especially during major changes (e.g., new technologies, products, or regulations).
Design and adapt local security processes and procedures where needed to strengthen information security.
Provide expert advice on mitigation strategies when security requirements cannot be met fully.
Lead security awareness and training programs throughout the year to foster a culture of security among teams.
Ensure compliance with local security regulations, external obligations, and best practices.
Support local Business Continuity plans and contribute to achieving security certifications or attestations, as required.
