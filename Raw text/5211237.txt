Job description, responsibilities and duties
We are looking for a
ServiceNow Developer
who wants
to help build modern, scalable, and AI-enhanced solutions.
You will work with the latest capabilities of the ServiceNow platform — from advanced automation to generative AI — and develop backend logic that elevates enterprise services to the next level.
You will be part of an international, agile team and contribute to the design and delivery of high-quality enterprise solutions across multiple ServiceNow modules.
Your Responsibilities
Develop backend functionalities on the ServiceNow platform using JavaScript
(Business Rules, Script Includes, Flow Designer actions, Scheduled Jobs, Scripted REST APIs).
Work with AI capabilities in ServiceNow, such as Now Assist, AI Search, intelligent routing, and predictive models.
Contribute to the development and enhancement of the Service Portal and Service Operations Workspace, integrating data, logic, and AI to deliver a modern and intuitive user experience.
Design data structures, workflows, and automation across various ServiceNow modules (e.g. ITSM, GRC, SIR, ITAM – depending on the project).
Test, document, and deliver solutions with a strong focus on quality, including active participation in code reviews.
Collaborate closely with architects, analysts, and other team members in an agile environment.
Continuously explore new ServiceNow platform capabilities and proactively suggest improvements to existing solutions.
