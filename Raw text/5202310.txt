Information about the position
Job description, responsibilities and duties
Role Purpose
An IT Project Manager
supporting manufacturing operations
leads the planning and execution of technology projects spanning maintenance management systems, testing platforms, MES enhancements, labeling solutions, and plant-floor infrastructure and network changes. This role partners closely with operations, engineering, quality, and IT teams to understand production needs, translate them into clear requirements, and deliver robust, compliant solutions with minimal disruption to manufacturing throughput. The Project Manager oversees vendor coordination, risk and change control, validation where required, and ensures all deployments align with regulatory, safety, and performance standards while driving efficiency, reliability, and continuous improvement across the manufacturing environment.
​
Key Responsabilities:
​
Manage Projects in a global environment.
Manage budgets, reporting actuals, planned and forecasted costs to the sponsors, budget line holder on a monthly basis.
Manage the people resource strategy for the Projects, mindful of Convatec’s ambition to increase inhouse knowledge, expertise and to manage and staff projects with internal resources.
Facilitate the budgeting process.  Providing a clear rationale for expenditure and the expected benefits/value that will be realised in Projects.
Create and track Project planning and the value to be delivered for the business.
Monitor and report on progress, utilize industry best practices, techniques, and standards throughout project execution.
Manage the Project status reporting structure and cadence.
Monitor and measure Projects ongoing impact/effectiveness against the declared value.
Establish, and continuously review, ways of working between workstreams that enhances and motivate the teams to deliver the Project´s objectives.
Manage and Harmonize Communication plans to wider stakeholder groups.  Ensuring the communications reflect the needs of the different stakeholder groups.
Carry out mid-year reviews of Projects to identify improved ways of working and whether the objectives of the Programmes and Projects remain relevant or require a Management review.
Act as an escalation point for the Project Teams to unblock issues.
Maintain Up to date information for Projects in the Project Management Tools.
Maintaining project documentation and reporting to senior leadership (RAID logs, plans, trackers, presentations etc.)
Documentation and adherence to processes and procedures.
Management of project financials in collaboration with the PMO/IT Financial Controller.
Coordinate and Lead project meetings.
