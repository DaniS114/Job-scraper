Starting Monthly Gross Salary: €2,500
Freshfields is a global law firm with over 280 years of experience in shaping the future of legal services. We’re proud to be a people-first organisation that values collaboration, innovation and inclusion.
Our team of 100+ professionals is growing, and we’re looking for a skilled Salesforce Developer to help us deliver impactful, scalable solutions across the business.
What you’ll do
As our Salesforce Developer, you’ll play a key role in designing and implementing tailored solutions that support our strategic goals. You’ll work closely with cross-functional teams to translate business needs into technical outcomes, and lead the development of Salesforce projects from concept to deployment.
Key responsibilities include:
Acting as the technical lead for Salesforce solutions, advising stakeholders on best practices.
Designing scalable, maintainable solutions aligned with business requirements.
Leading configuration, customisation and integration efforts.
Collaborating with Solution Architects and Product Owners to deliver end-to-end solutions.
Supporting BAU enhancements and resolving complex issues.
Maintaining deployment pipelines and ensuring code quality.
Mentoring junior developers and contributing to team knowledge sharing.
What you’ll bring
We’re looking for someone with:
3+ years of hands-on Salesforce development experience.
Strong skills in Flows, Apex, SOQL/SOSL, and Lightning Web Components.
Experience with REST/SOAP APIs and third-party integrations.
A service-oriented mindset and excellent communication skills.
Familiarity with Agile/Scrum methodologies and tools like Copado and Azure DevOps.
Salesforce Platform Developer I certification (preferred).
