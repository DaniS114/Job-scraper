Key Responsibilities:
Customer Interaction Management:
Manage customer interactions through various channels including phone, email, and online collaboration platforms.
Focus on enterprise client lifecycle operations, contract retention, and revenue growth.
Spend substantial time on calls with customers, building and maintaining strong relationships.
Account Ownership and Collaboration:
Take full ownership of assigned enterprise accounts or co-manage them with a Strategic Account Lead.
Collaborate on new business development initiatives, RFP management, and strategic deal closures.
Relationship Building:
Build and maintain relationships with key decision-makers and stakeholders within customer organizations.
Ensure customers feel valued and their needs are met promptly.
Sales and Order Management:
Handle sales tools, initiate, and track sales orders from initial receipt through to billing closure.
Understand the client’s business objectives, challenges, and industry trends to position relevant solutions.
Internal Collaboration:
Collaborate with internal teams such as product, marketing, and customer support to ensure client needs are met.
Conduct regular business reviews with clients to gather feedback, communicate progress, and identify new opportunities.
Client Satisfaction and Feedback:
Ensure high levels of client satisfaction by addressing client concerns promptly and effectively.
Provide insights and recommendations to senior management based on client feedback and market analysis.
Continuous Improvement:
Continuously expand and improve your technical and product knowledge by attending educational workshops and reviewing professional publications.
