DESCRIPTION
Are you interested in AI security and looking to deepen your expertise? Join our team as a Secure AI Consultant, where you’ll have the opportunity to learn from leading experts, develop new skills, and advance your career. We’re seeking motivated professionals who have experience with AI and cloud security, and who are eager to expand their knowledge in a supportive, growth-focused environment.
WHAT WILL
YOU DO
• Participate in high-impact AI security workshops for enterprise clients and internal teams
• Learn to assess organizational AI readiness and identify key control gaps
• Help define and implement robust AI governance and risk management frameworks
• Receive guidance on secure and compliant use of Microsoft Copilot and Azure AI tools
• Develop practical controls for data protection, labeling, and prompt safety
• Present findings and next steps to IT management, with support from experienced mentors
• Contribute to incident response and learn how to recommend improvements for AI misuse or data leakage
WHAT WE
EXPECT
• up to 5 years of experience in cloud security, compliance or architecture
• Exposure to secure M365 & AI workload adoption is a plus
• Interest in learning Microsoft Purview, Fabric, and Entra
• Motivation to develop skills in responsible AI, privacy by design, and compliance (GDPR, ISO, NIST)
• Strong communication skills and willingness to learn how to simplify complex topics
• Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in IT or a related field
• English and German language proficiency at Upper intermediate (B2) or Advanced (C1)
Nice to have:
• Familiarity with AI governance frameworks and risk management
• Microsoft certifications such as: SC-100, SC-401, AZ-500, AI-102
