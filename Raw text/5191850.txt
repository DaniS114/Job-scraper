Job description, responsibilities and duties
This is us
At Avenga, we believe that human creativity empowers technology that matters. Operating globally, our 6000+ specialists provide a full spectrum of services, including business and tech advisory, enterprise solutions, CX, UX and Ul design, managed services, product development, and software development.
This is the job
As a Solution Architect, you will take on a key role in shaping and delivering innovative technical solutions that bridge the gap between business needs and technological capabilities. You will be the driving force behind aligning system design with strategic goals, ensuring seamless collaboration across teams, and delivering value-driven outcomes for clients. This role offers a unique opportunity to combine your technical expertise, strategic thinking, and leadership skills to make a meaningful impact on complex projects.
This is you
Required skills:
Leverage your expertise in designing and implementing technological solutions as a Solution Architect with 3-5 years of experience
Demonstrate expertise in UML, Enterprise Architect, and Archimate/Togaf
Showcase a strong understanding of microservices architecture, cloud technologies, integration patterns, and containerization
Proficiently manage SQL, with a solid grasp of data structures and databases
Bring valuable experience from a technical role, ideally in development, as an asset
Understanding of the telecom, banking, or automotive sectors would be a distinct advantage
Effectively communicate technical solutions to both technical and non-technical stakeholders
Demonstrate experience in team leadership and facilitating conferences
Proficiency in Slovak/Czech and English at a professional level is required
This is your role
Analyze and understand client requirements, design tailored technical solutions, and deliver compelling presentations
Serve as the guarantor for solution design during the presales phase and provide input on budget estimations
Facilitate knowledge transfer in the early stages of projects and ensure alignment with business objectives
Proactively identify and mitigate risks associated with the proposed solution
Maintain close collaboration with the project manager, business stakeholders, and IT teams throughout the delivery phase
Take ownership of creating and maintaining solution architecture documentation
Optionally, participate in developing the proposed solution and offer technical leadership to the team

Pleasant working environment and stable background in one of the largest software development companies

You have the opportunity to influence your direction in the company
High quality hardware and software, access to the latest technologies and specific projects
Opportunities to improve your language skills, we are a major global player
At Avenga, you'll get quality support in your development thanks to a wide range of internal and external training courses, so you can push the boundaries of your competences
We offer you benefits such as life insurance and pension contribution, EN/DE/ES lessons, MultiSport card, sick days, meal allowance, lots of company events, offices in Bratislava with quality amenities for lovers of sport, food and coffee, hybrid remote work after mutual agreement, and more

Brief description of the company
Sme globálna technologicko-poradenská spoločnosť, ktorá pomáha organizáciám riešiť zložité IT výzvy prostredníctvom inovatívnych a na mieru šitých digitálnych riešení.
S viac ako 6 000 odborníkmi po celom svete ponúkame komplexné portfólio služieb – či už ide o vývoj softvéru, analýzu, architektúru, projektový manažment, testovanie, oblasť kybernetickej bezpečnosti, návrh UX/UI, DevOps alebo iné pokročilé technologické poradenstvo.
