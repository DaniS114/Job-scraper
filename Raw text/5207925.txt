Junior AI Specialist - Gen AI, LLM & RAG (m/f)
Job description, responsibilities and duties
Join us as a
Junior AI Specialist
at PwC Slovakia, where you'll have the opportunity to grow your skills and contribute to innovative AI initiatives for leading global clients. If you have a solid foundation in Generative AI, Large Language Models (LLM), or Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), and an eagerness to learn and develop, this is your chance to start impactful projects across various domains, including data science.
Projects you could be involved in:
Natural Language Data Exploration:
Develop dashboards powered by GenAI for intuitive business data analysis.
Document Interaction:
Design tools enabling seamless interaction with unstructured and structured text documents.
Knowledge Assistant:
Implement ERP transformation with GenAI tools to resolve employee inquiries efficiently.
Agricultural Monitoring:
Utilize Computer Vision for analyzing satellite imagery on a large scale.
Automated Reconciliation:
Create systems to identify anomalous transactions in accounting ledgers.
Key Responsibilities:
Project Participation:
Assist in AI projects from conception to deployment, contributing to solution design and ensuring timely task completion.
Solution Development:
Support the development of AI solutions using technologies like LLM and RAG, learning from experienced colleagues.
Learning & Development:
Embrace new learning opportunities by staying updated on AI trends and enhancing processes.
Who We're Looking For:
Programming Proficiency:
Proven programming skills in Python (pandas, plotly, numpy); proficiency in other programming languages is a plus.
AI/ML Experience:
Some exposure or academic experience in projects involving Generative AI, NLP, Computer Vision, or ML model development and deployment.
Database Skills:
Familiarity with SQL databases; NoSQL experience is a plus.
Language Skills:
Fluent in English (B2 level or higher).
Analytical Ability:
Strong analytical mindset with a creative approach to challenges.
Team Player:
Ability to collaborate effectively in a team environment.
Problem Solving:
Creative approach to tackling complex challenges.
Independence:
Proven ability to deliver results independently.
