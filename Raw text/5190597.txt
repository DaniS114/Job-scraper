About the Role
We are seeking a skilled and adaptable IT System Engineer to join our Electronic Tolling Operations and Maintenance team in Bratislava. This role supports tolling infrastructure across Europe and requires a hands-on professional who thrives in a dynamic environment and is ready to respond to critical system needs — anytime, anywhere.
Key Responsibilities
Deploy, configure, and manage applications and system changes across tolling platforms.
Maintain system integrity, performance, and security across production and test environments.
Support European end customers via ticketing systems and direct communication with partners.
Monitor and troubleshoot system issues, ensuring timely resolution and minimal downtime.
Participate in 24/7 on-call emergency support rotation.
Collaborate with cross-functional teams to implement updates and enhancements.
Document system configurations, operational procedures, and incident reports.
Daily Tasks
Handle application deployments and configuration updates.
Respond to and resolve customer tickets and incidents.
Coordinate with sub-suppliers and customers to ensure seamless system operations.
Perform system health checks and apply patches or upgrades.
Participate in change management and release planning.
Maintain logs and reports for various purposes.
