System Engineer (Automations / Firewall)
Job description, responsibilities and duties
Are you passionate about
network security
and
network automation
? Do you thrive in environments where you can leverage cutting-edge technology to make a real impact? Join our dynamic team and explore exciting career opportunities tailored to your skills and aspirations.
We're seeking talented professionals for two distinct roles within our security engineering division, each offering unique challenges and opportunities for growth.
1. Security Engineer - Automations
Become a trailblazer in automation! As a
Security Engineer focused on automation
, you will lead efforts to transform traditional network security practices into efficient, automated frameworks. Join us to spearhead global projects that enhance network security and streamline operations.
Key Responsibilities:
Revolutionize Infrastructure: Design and implement automation frameworks to elevate network security to unprecedented levels.
Master Scripting: Utilize your skills in Python, Terraform, and Ansible to automate and improve processes.
Lead and Collaborate: Integrate security solutions into existing workflows and guide teams across regions toward seamless automation.
What We’re Looking For:
Proficiency in Automation tools and scripting languages: Ansible, Terraform, and Python.
A strong background in network/infrastructure security.
3-5 years of hands-on automation experience.
Ability to collaborate with diverse teams globally.
2. Security Engineer - Palo Alto
Are you a Palo Alto enthusiast? This role offers the opportunity to specialize in firewall management and automation, ensuring our global network remains secure and efficient.
Lead initiatives that redefine security operations and deliver innovative solutions tailored to Palo Alto technologies.
Key Responsibilities:
Optimize Security: Manage and configure Palo Alto firewalls, transforming manual processes into automated operations.
Strategize and Implement: Use Panorama for firewall management and integrate advanced routing protocols for optimized security.
Automate with Precision: Implement scripting and automation using Python, Terraform, and Ansible to enhance security measures.
What We’re Looking For:
3-5 years of experience with Palo Alto firewalls.
Proficient in automation tools and scripting languages such as Ansible, Terraform, and Python.
Expertise in network routing, including OSPF, Static Routes, and BGP.
Palo Alto ACE Certification or willingness to obtain after joining.
