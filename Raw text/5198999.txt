AI Services - Business Process Analyst–AI & Automation Solutions /KE/
We are seeking an experienced Business Process Analyst to join our dynamic team as a digitalization expert. In this role, you will bridge the gap between business needs and technical implementation, working closely with internal customers across our organization to transform their data landscape and optimize business processes. As part of our agile, multi-national team, you'll play a key role in shaping how our company leverages modern data platforms and automation to drive business value.

About
the position
Which challenges are expecting you:
Analyze and document customer business processes, identifying opportunities for digitalization and automation
Design end-to-end IT solutions that address business requirements while considering technical feasibility, scalability, and integration with existing systems
Drive the architecture and implementation of data integration solutions across multiple platforms and systems
Provide strategic consulting on process optimization, automation strategies, and data platform selection and integration
Coordinate and steer technical teams through solution development, ensuring alignment between business goals and technical execution
Facilitate requirements workshops with stakeholders and translate business needs into technical specifications
Act as a trusted advisor to internal customers, guiding them through digital transformation initiatives
We work in a HYBRID STYLE of working with the possibility of working from home , but twice a week we meet in the office in Košice or Bratislava for meetings.
The published salary is the minimum possible offer.
The starting salary may be higher depending on the extent of fulfillment of the employee's requirements (education, language skills, required practice, personality assumptions and skills).
