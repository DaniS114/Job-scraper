Job description, responsibilities and duties
Would you like to work for a well-established Swiss Management Consulting company with 29+ years of experience in a new, exciting, and fast-growing Bratislava office? Send us your CV and work on various exciting projects within the banking or insurance industry and learn innovative project management techniques combined with IT skills!
We are looking for self-driven enthusiasts with an entrepreneurship spirit backed by a banking and team leader background. If you have project management skills or previous experience as a business consultant, even better. Join us and be part of our successful financial business expansion!
Your tasks:
•	Lead the Banking business consulting team and be responsible for projects which create target operating models, system migrations, process improvements, change management, creating business strategies, IT architecture, software implementation and more
•	Motivate your team and ensure professional development of each if its members by utilizing Synpulse’s wide range of development opportunities
•	Excel on the execution of projects by active client facing consulting to understand their vision and needs
•	Understand and translate customer needs into implementable business solutions to enable long-term customer success, transform business requirements into solutions and understand strategic business needs and plans to sustain growth
•	End-to-end project delivery and quality responsibility including transition to post-go-live phases
•	Review and analyze different aspects of our clients’ business to provide suggestions for improvement
•	Develop and lead the preparation, management, and implementation of business projects
