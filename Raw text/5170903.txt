Informácie o pracovnom mieste
Náplň práce, právomoci a zodpovednosti
•	Active participation on software development to ensure testability of requirements, usability of user interface design, more accurate effort estimations, etc.
•	Ensure the effective implementation of test strategy in collaboration with Product Owner and other technical roles (e.g. test developers, software developers, product managers etc.)
•	Design test cases based on requirements and use cases
•	Implement automated tests and perform manual tests
•	Establish and maintain the test environment
•	Maintain traceability of tests to requirements
•	Report defects and retest the bug fixes
•	Help to ensure that developed product adheres to non-functional requirements by executing tests targeted to these business values (stability, performance, error handling, etc.)
•	Create test documentation (e.g. Test Plan, Test Report, etc.)
•	Report to the stakeholders
•	Provide feedback on quality and consistency of requirements and specifications (testability of requirements, usability of user interface, more accurate effort estimations, etc.)
•	Assists Product Owner to solve business issues related to testing
