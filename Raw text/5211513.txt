Information about the position
Job description, responsibilities and duties
At
Visma Idella
, we are the backbone of the financial and pension sectors in the Netherlands. Our
SIVI Team
is at the heart of this mission, focusing on the seamless integration and standardization of financial data exchange.
The
SIVI team
works on the "Experience" layer of our architecture, ensuring that complex pension and financial data are not just processed, but transformed into intuitive, high-performance services. By joining SIVI, you aren't just writing code, you are building the modern bridges that connect financial institutions with the people they serve, all within a high-security, high-availability Azure microservices ecosystem.
What You’ll Work On
Cloud-Native Development:
Build robust, scalable, and maintainable .NET microservices using the latest versions of the .NET framework
.
Azure Ecosystem:
Utilize a modern stack including Container Apps, Key Vault, Service Bus, and Event Grid.
Distributed Systems:
Implement highly scalable and fault-tolerant systems designed for the Azure cloud.
Architecture & Security:
Support security-driven architecture principles and help address technical debt through smart refactoring and system design improvements.
Your Role
As a Medior Developer
you’ll:
Develop & Implement:
Create features for .NET-based microservices and Azure cloud infrastructure.
Collaborate:
Work with senior team members to translate technical designs into high-quality code and participate in meaningful code reviews.
Problem Solve:
Identify and resolve technical challenges while ensuring adherence to coding standards.
Partner with Product:
Work closely with the Product team to understand business requirements and contribute to technical solutions during sprint grooming and estimation.
Support the Lifecycle:
Provide technical support through development, testing, and deployment phases.
What We’re Looking For
3+ years of experience in software development, with a strong focus on C# and the .NET framework.
Proven experience in building microservices-based applications, preferably in Azure.
Functional knowledge of Azure services (Container Apps/App Services, Service Bus, or Event Grid).
Proficient in distributed systems and a solid understanding of software architecture patterns and design principles.
Good problem-solving skills and a proactive, critical-thinking mindset.
Excellent English communication skills to effectively collaborate with cross-functional teams.
Preferred Qualifications
Experience with Agile methodologies (Scrum or Kanban).
Knowledge of containerization (Docker) and orchestration (Kubernetes).
Familiarity with DevOps (CI/CD pipelines and Infrastructure as Code).
Azure or .NET certifications (e.g., Azure Developer Associate).
