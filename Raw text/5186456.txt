Agentic AI Developer
Job description, responsibilities and duties
Are you an adaptable
full-stack developer
ready to make your mark in the realm of
Agentic AI
? We are looking for a dynamic individual to join our AI Engineering team, focusing on building
Agentic Workflows using Co-Pilot Studio
. If you have experience with similar technologies, a strong understanding of the Microsoft ecosystem, and the agility to quickly master Co-Pilot Studio, this role is tailored for you.
Why This Role?
Be part of a vibrant team that innovates using AI to streamline workflows and enhance operational efficiency. Engage with Co-Pilot Studio to develop agent-driven processes that propel us toward a future of intelligent automation.
Key Responsibilities:
Develop and implement Agentic Workflows using Co-Pilot Studio, enhancing existing processes and driving efficiency.
Engage in the complete development lifecycle, capitalizing on your skills in both front-end and back-end development within a Microsoft-centric environment.
Work closely with your team to seamlessly incorporate AI solutions into existing frameworks and optimize functionalities.
What We’re Looking For:
Full-stack development experience with a knack for AI-driven solutions.
Familiarity with the Microsoft ecosystem, including MS Teams integrations.
Prior knowledge of Co-Pilot Studio is an advantage, but relatable experience with other tech is essential.
Proactive approach, eager to learn and innovate with new technologies.
Why You'll Love It Here:
Collaborative Team Environment:
Work with like-minded individuals passionate about AI and innovation.
Meaningful Impact:
Contribute to projects that reshape how we approach workflow automation and gave a global impact.
Continuous Learning:
Enjoy opportunities for professional development and skill enhancement.
Ready to Apply?
Step into the future of Agentic AI with us!
If you are a developer ready to take on the challenge of creating cutting-edge agents in Co-Pilot Studio, we want to hear from you!
