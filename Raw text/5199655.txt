Job description, responsibilities and duties
As a Frontend Developer, you will be responsible forshaping high-performance interfaces with a strong emphasis on datavisualization and user experience. You will lead initiatives to research andevaluate visualization technologies while maintaining the frontend architecture.
Key Responsibilities:
Architecture     & Development:
Design and maintain the frontend     architecture to meet performance, scalability, and maintainability     requirements. Implement new features, debug issues, and ensure code is     production-ready.
Data     Visualization:
Lead initiatives by researching and     evaluating libraries (e.g., React, Canvas/SVG, D3.js) to create     maintainable and performant visualizations.
Quality     & CI/CD:
Maintain build quality and continuous     integration within the frontend ecosystem. Contribute to QA by ensuring UI     meets design and usability expectations.
Planning:
Estimate effort for backlog items and assess the feasibility of     non-functional requirements.
Collaboration:
Work with backend developers, product owners, and QA to improve     infrastructure and automation. Identify and track technical debt.
