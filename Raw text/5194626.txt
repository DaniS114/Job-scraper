Job description, responsibilities and duties
Ready to shape the future of legal tech? Freshfields is launching a new IT hub in Bratislava’s Shared Services Centre (SSC)—and we’re looking for talented professionals to join us. Be part of an innovative, international team working on transformative digital solutions. Build your career with Freshfields and make an impact on a global scale!
Are you a strategic thinker with a passion for delivering impactful technology projects? We’re looking for an experienced IT Project Manager to join our dynamic Technology Delivery team. This is a fantastic opportunity to lead high-impact projects across global teams, driving meaningful change and innovation.
As an IT Project Manager, you’ll lead cross-functional teams to deliver a variety of technology and business-led projects. You’ll be responsible for end-to-end project delivery—ensuring alignment with business goals, managing stakeholders, and embedding sustainable solutions that deliver real value.
You’ll work across multiple regions and collaborate with diverse teams, applying best-in-class methodologies (Agile, Waterfall, hybrid) to ensure successful outcomes. Your leadership will be key in fostering collaboration, driving performance, and ensuring projects are delivered on time and within budget.
