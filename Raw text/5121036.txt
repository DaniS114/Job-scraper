Job description, responsibilities and duties
START YOUR CAREER WITH CONFIDENCE, SUPPORT AND SPACE TO GROW.
We’re an international software company focused on data processing and intelligent solutions. Our Global Technology division is looking for a new colleague — someone curious, analytical, and ready to learn. This is not a high-pressure environment. You’ll be given time to understand our systems, learn modern best practices, and grow at your own pace. Experienced senior developers will patiently guide you, answer your questions, and help you build confidence in your skills.
What You’ll Work On
You’ll help develop software that processes data from documents and other unstructured sources. You’ll also contribute to designing cloud-based tools and solutions. Technologies you’ll encounter include:
•	Web Crawling, Elasticsearch, REST Services
•	Cloud environments: AWS, Azure
•	Relational & non-relational databases
•	Microservices architectures
