Internship Opportunity: Full Stack Developer (JavaScript/Python)
Job description, responsibilities and duties
Are you ready to dive into an exciting year-long internship that will propel your career in technology? Join PwC Slovakia as a
Full Stack Developer Intern
, where your work will have a
global impact
, touching users in Australia, Germany, and the USA from our delivery center. Immerse yourself in
innovative projects
, from crafting sleek front-end interfaces to building robust back-end systems. Engage with industry leaders in a dynamic and collaborative atmosphere that encourages growth and discovery.
Role and Responsibilities:
Develop and Innovate:
Contribute to the development and maintenance of cutting-edge web applications using
JavaScript
and
Python
, helping transform global business solutions and try out different technologies.
Hands-On Involvement:
Engage in
all aspects of the application development cycle
, from design and development to deployment, infrastructure, and DevOps.
Collaborate and Create:
Work with cross-functional teams to design and implement effective project solutions, pushing the boundaries of what's possible.
Refine and Enhance:
Participate in code reviews and collaborate to elevate our software development practices, ensuring top-notch quality and performance.
Problem-Solve and Support:
Assist in identifying and troubleshooting software issues, ensuring seamless project execution.
Learn and Apply:
Dive into best practices, including agile methodologies, and bring your fresh ideas to team meetings to enhance project outcomes.
Qualifications:
Currently in your
4th or 5th year of study
in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or related fields.
Good understanding of
JavaScript, Python, HTML, and CSS.
Familiarity with version control systems like Git adds to your collaborative capabilities.
The most crucial aspect is your motivation and enthusiasm for developing technologies and integrating AI into daily work.
Join Us!
If you're passionate about technology and eager to leave a mark on the digital world,
this internship is your gateway to a promising future.
Blend your academic knowledge with real-world experience in a supportive environment rich with learning opportunities.
