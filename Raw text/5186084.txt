Job description, responsibilities and duties
We are now looking for
Senior Backend QA Engineer
to join the Moxymind team to work on a long term projects for one of our clients operating in the heavy machinery industry. You will be part of cross-functional team, working on software testing solutions that ensure quality, reliability, and scalability. Depending on your experience and skill set, you’ll be assigned to a project where you can make the greatest impact.
As a Backend QA Engineer with us you will:
Ensure the quality of backend services and APIs through both manual and automated testing.
Design, implement, and execute functional, integration, regression, and performance tests.
Analyze business and technical requirements to create and maintain effective test cases.
Collaborate closely with developers, product owners, and DevOps teams to resolve issues and improve test coverage.
Build and maintain test plans, automation scripts, and testing frameworks using Karate.
Document and report defects, testing progress, and results in a clear and structured way.
Work in an Agile environment with CI/CD best practices and modern DevOps tools.
