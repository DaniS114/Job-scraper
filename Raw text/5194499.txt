What we expect
Work independently and with commitment in the area of SAP HCM to understand and implement the requirements of the HR department with a focus on Payroll and Time Management

Who we are looking for
Successfully completed studies in (business) informatics, economics or a comparable qualification

You have in-depth knowledge and practical experience in the areas of personnel administration (PA), German payroll (PY) including schemes and rules, time management (PT), certification, and organizational management (OM)
Ideally, you have good ABAP programming skills
Knowledge of Employee/Manager Self-Services (ESS/MSS) and experience with SuccessFactors is desirable
You have excellent communication skills, a strong customer and solution focus, and good presentation skills
You have excellent English skills and enjoy working in an international environment, in addition German language skills is an advantage
