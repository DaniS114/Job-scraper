Pega Developer (Mid-Level)
Place of work
Remote work
Contract type
full-time
POSITION
DESCRIPTION
Join Accenture’s Pega Engineering team and help shape the next generation of digital banking applications.
As a Pega Developer, you’ll work in an agile environment on a flagship in-house application used to manage the counterparty lifecycle within the client’s Lending Value Chain. This solution is a key part of the client’s Digital Transformation Strategy, streamlining onboarding and due diligence processes through automation and smart workflow design.
WHAT WILL
YOU DO
•	Develop and configure Pega-based components to deliver high-quality features and workflows.
•	Analyze system design and documentation, identify gaps, and propose improvements.
•	Collaborate with team members to validate requirements, clarify technical logic, and ensure seamless implementation.
•	Maintain clean code and follow development standards, version control, and agile delivery practices.
•	Participate in agile ceremonies (daily stand-ups, sprint planning, reviews, retrospectives).
•	Support integrations with APIs, data models, and external systems.
•	Share knowledge and insights within the team to enhance delivery quality and speed.
WHAT WE
EXPECT
•	2-5 years of hands-on experience with Pega platform development.
•	Pega Certified System Architect (CSA) is required; Certified Senior System Architect (CSSA) is an advantage.
•	Familiarity with SOAP, REST, and PL/SQL for integration and backend work.
•	Good understanding of data modeling, XML (XSD, XSLT, XQuery, XPath), and UML.
•	Basic knowledge of BPM principles and process automation.
•	Experience working in Agile (Scrum) environments and understanding of DevOps practices.
•	A proactive team player eager to learn, collaborate, and grow within the Pega ecosystem.
•	Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in IT, business, or a related field.
•	Strong communication skills in English (B2 or higher); German language is a plus.
