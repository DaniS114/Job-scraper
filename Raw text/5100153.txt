Information about the position
Job description, responsibilities and duties
About the role
As a data/application engineer supporting the Alternative Capital Partners IT unit (ACP), you will cooperate closely with other engineers on running and enhancing an IT application. The application is embedded into an environment serving business and integrates with other applications and platforms. The application is productive and operational. The role requires maintenance, new feature development and operations support. It requires communication with an international user community as well as a wide range of IT peers across the globe.
About the team
We are a small, dynamic, and international team of experienced specialists that design, build and operate the ACP IT landscape in collaboration with our business partners. We follow agile methodologies (daily stand-up, bi-weekly sprints, product increments) and utilize DevOps recommendations. We are actively utilizing Microsoft Azure cloud and Palantir Foundry platform for our application landscape.
Key Responsibilities
-Create new solultions, implement change requests, bug fixing and adequate test cases for system/application
-Optimize application performance and ensure secure, scalable deployments in palantir foundry & azure environment.
-Maintain code quality and automated testing using CI/CD pipelines.
-Provide operational support and troubleshooting of the production issues
-Ensure processing of reporting deliverables, data feeds in due time according to closing cycles
-Operational support, monitoring and issue handling within the team
-Produce (and maintain) system & application documentation
