Job description, responsibilities and duties
Our Bratislava-based company FREQUENTIS Solutions & Services s. r. o. belongs to the Austrian group FREQUENTIS AG (www.frequentis.com). We provide communication and information systems for control centers with safety-critical tasks. Control Centre Solutions’ are developed and distributed by FREQUENTIS in the business segments Air Traffic Management (civil and military air traffic control, aeronautical information management, air defence) and Public Safety & Transport (police, fire and rescue services, emergency medical services, vessel traffic and railways).​
Join our team and help us make our solutions even more reliable. Work for an international technological leader and enjoy the benefits of flexible working arrangements and our focus on a good work-life balance.
Who We Are Looking For?
We are looking for System Engineers who will take a key role in the technical implementation and service lifecycle of our systems, ensuring both delivery excellence and high-quality service support for our customers in the Defense domain.
Main tasks
​Act as a technical representative of FREQUENTIS towards customers for all service-related aspects
Collaborate with Service Management and Sales teams to support the sale and renewal of maintenance and service contracts, providing technical expertise during discussions
Assist customers with technical questions related to system operation, configuration, and service
Coordinate and conduct root cause analyses, define corrective actions, and ensure quality service delivery
Support the transition of delivery projects into the service phase, ensuring smooth knowledge transfer and operational readiness
Contribute to product improvement by feeding back service experience to Product Management
Create and maintain detailed technical documentation (system descriptions, interfaces, configuration steps)
Handle service tickets and participate in on-call duties to ensure timely resolution of operational issues
Respond to technical enquiries (incidents, problems, configuration changes) within agreed response times
Participate in delivery projects and other activities as required by business needs.
Design, plan, and execute system changes or extensions to new or existing systems (change requests)
Integrate new systems or components into existing environments
Perform system audits, participate in site acceptances, and ensure system readiness

Brief description of the company
Our Bratislava-based company FREQUENTIS Solutions & Services is part of the Austrian FREQUENTIS group (www.frequentis.com). We are a leading global supplier of communication and information solutions for safety-critical applications, such as for air traffic control towers, public safety control rooms (112) or air defense forces. Our mission-critical solutions cover diverse areas, such as telephony and radio communication, Geographical Information Systems (GIS), incident and resource management, video surveillance and automatic video analytics.
FREQUENTIS Solutions & Services is specialized in software development and project delivery. Together with our parent company we are developing FREQUENTIS’ next generation control room solutions, using state-of-the-art technologies and processes. Our delivery teams then deploy those solutions at our customers around the globe, with responsibility for commercial and technical project management, engineering and quality assurance.
To grow our team, we are constantly looking for talents in all these fields. Our company offers an interesting and challenging technical environment, a people-focused culture with emphasis on a good work-life-balance and, if desired, a long-term development and career opportunities.
For our customers, lives may be at stake and seconds count. Our solutions thus need to be developed to the highest standards when it comes to usability and reliability. Safety is what guides us from the first designs till the final tests of any of our solutions. If those are the kinds of challenges that excite you, then you have come to the right place!
