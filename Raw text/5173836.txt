Information about the position
Job description, responsibilities and duties
As Head of Data & AI, you'll join our Technology Leadership Team and drive the strategic evolution of our data engineering and artificial intelligence capabilities. This is a senior leadership role where you'll shape how we leverage data to deliver exceptional value to our clients in the funds industry.
You'll lead a dedicated team in Bratislava, architecting scalable data infrastructure using cutting-edge technologies, including DataBricks, Azure. Your work will directly enable enhanced insights, operational efficiencies, and position us for sustainable growth.
What You'll Drive
Team Leadership & Strategy
Lead and mentor our Data & AI team, fostering technical excellence and alignment with business objectives
Drive innovation through machine learning models, advanced analytics, and AI integration (including OpenAI/GPT technologies)
Partner with Technology Leadership to define and execute our data and AI roadmap
Technical Excellence
Design, build, and maintain robust data pipelines and architectures using DataBricks and Azure
Oversee data governance, quality assurance, and compliance across all data initiatives
Support seamless service transitions during migrations and technical upgrades
Business Impact
Collaborate with stakeholders across investor relations, fund management, and automation to identify high-value opportunities
Translate complex data challenges into strategic advantages
Primary focus: funds business applications, with potential support for other business lines
