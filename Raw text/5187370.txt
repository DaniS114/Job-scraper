SAP BW Consultant (Mid-Level)
Place of work
Remote work
Contract type
full-time
POSITION
DESCRIPTION
We are seeking a skilled and motivated SAP BW Consultant to join our team and support our data warehousing and reporting initiatives. You will play a key role in designing, developing, and maintaining SAP BW solutions that enable data-driven decision-making across the organization.
WHAT WILL
YOU DO
Design and implement SAP BW data models.
Develop and optimize ETL processes using SAP BW tools and SAP Data Services.
Collaborate with business stakeholders to gather requirements and translate them into technical solutions.
Perform data extraction from SAP and non-SAP sources using standard and custom extractors.
Support BW on HANA or BW/4HANA migration and optimization projects.
Conduct unit testing, integration testing, and support user acceptance testing (UAT).
Provide ongoing support and troubleshooting for BW applications and reports.
Document technical specifications, data flows, and system configurations.
WHAT WE
EXPECT
Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in IT or a related field
At least 3 to 5 years of hands-on experience with SAP BW (preferably BW on HANA or BW/4HANA).
Strong understanding of data warehousing concepts.
English language proficiency at upper intermediate (B2) or advanced (C1) level.
Experience in programming or SQL is an advantage.
Familiarity with SAP Analytics tools (e.g., SAP BO, SAC) is an advantage.
