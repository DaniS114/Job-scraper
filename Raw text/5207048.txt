Information about the position
Job description, responsibilities and duties
We are looking for an experienced Business Partner to act as a key link between company leadership and internal teams.
Your mission will be to drive sustainable business growth through strategic analysis, financial planning, and active stakeholder engagement.
You will play a critical role in shaping volume and pricing strategies, monitoring performance, and delivering accurate financial forecasts.
Key Responsibilities
Strategic Stakeholder Engagement & Market Analysis.
Act as the primary contact for key business stakeholders.
Analyze market trends and develop assumptions to support volume and pricing strategies.
Ensure alignment with overall strategic objectives.
Financial Analysis & Decision Support.
Build robust financial models and scenario analyses to inform strategic decisions.
Deliver timely ad-hoc analyses to identify opportunities and mitigate risks.
Partner with category leads to support business planning and performance optimization.
Performance Monitoring & Continuous Improvement
Track execution of volume and pricing plans, KPIs, and market dynamics.
Identify performance gaps and recommend actionable improvements.
Business Partnering
Build strong relationships across internal teams, acting as a trusted advisor.
Provide insights and guidance to enable informed decision-making.

Brief description of the company
Philip Morris International is the world’s leading international tobacco company, with a diverse workforce of around 80,000 people. Six of the world's top international 15 brands, spanning more than 180 markets, are ours, including Marlboro, the world's number one. We operate 48 production facilities in 32 different countries.
Over the last decade we’re dedicated to doing something very dramatic – replacing cigarettes with the smoke-free products that we’re developing and selling.
That’s why we have a total of over 400 dedicated scientists, engineers, and technicians developing less harmful alternatives to cigarettes. It’s the biggest shift in our history. And it’s the right one for our consumers, our company, our shareholders, and society.
