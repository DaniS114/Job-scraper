What will you do
We are looking for an experienced ADI Reporting Developer to support our data-driven decision-making process by ensuring accurate, timely, and automated reporting solutions. The ideal candidate will be responsible for pulling and analyzing data from multiple sources, developing automated reports, and providing insights that drive efficiency and compliance with partners requirements.
Key Responsibilities:
Retrieve and consolidate data from various sources, including CX, business warehouse, LSUMP, and call center systems.
Calculate, evaluate, and report on Service Level Agreements (SLAs) across business areas such as call centers, complaint management, AWSP partners, and depots.
Develop and maintain automated reporting solutions to meet our insurance partner requirements.
Perform data analysis and visualization using Power BI.
Ensure the accuracy and quality of all reports while maintaining data integrity.
Provide training and support to end-users for effective utilization of reporting solutions.
Develop additional reports based on evolving business requirements.
