Information about the position
Job description, responsibilities and duties
The PLC Programmer is responsible for developing, testing, and maintaining programmable logic controller (PLC) systems to ensure reliable automation processes in production and support digitalization initiatives. As a central position within Industrial Engineering, this role also sets global standards for PLC programming and maintains specifications for all plants and suppliers
Design, program, and implement PLC systems for production equipment and automation projects.
Modify and optimize existing PLC programs to improve efficiency and reliability.
Collaborate with engineering, maintenance, and operations teams to ensure seamless integration of automation solutions.
Troubleshoot and resolve PLC-related issues to minimize downtime.
Ensure compliance with safety standards and cybersecurity requirements in automation systems.
Document all programming changes and maintain version control.
Support commissioning and start-up of new equipment and systems.
Define and maintain global PLC standards and specifications for all plants and suppliers.
Act as a reference point for best practices and compliance in automation and digitalization projects.

Brief description of the company
Since its spin-off in September 2025 AUMOVIO continues the business of the former Continental group sector Automotive as an independent company. The technology and electronics company offers a wide-ranging portfolio that makes mobility safe, exciting, connected, and autonomous. This includes sensor solutions, displays, braking and comfort systems as well as comprehensive expertise in software, architecture platforms, and assistance systems for software-defined vehicles. In the fiscal year 2024 the business areas, which now belong to AUMOVIO, generated sales of 19.6 billion Euro. The company is headquartered in Frankfurt, Germany and has about 87.000 employees in more than 100 locations worldwide.
