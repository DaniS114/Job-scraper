Frontend Developer (React) - Protect platform
Place of work
Žilina, Slovakia (Job with occasional home office)
Wage (gross)
From 2 800 EUR/month
*Performance bonus 2 times per year up to 10% of the basic salary paid for the evaluation period(usually 6 months).
*The final basic wage component can be higher accordingly to individual skills and experience of selected candidate.
Contract type
full-time
We are looking for a Frontend Developer with strong React experience to enhance the ESET PROTECT platform, focusing on user interfaces for our Cloud Workload Protection (CWP) and Cloud Security Posture Management (CSPM) capabilities.
The role is primarily frontend-focused — backend involvement may happen only in exceptional cases to clarify integration behavior.
What You’ll Do
Develop modern UI components in React for CWP and CSPM features.
Collaborate with backend developers to ensure smooth API and microservices integration.
Contribute to iterative feature development and platform improvements.
Work in an Agile setup (SAFe), actively participating in planning and team collaboration.
Requirements
Strong hands-on experience with React.js.
Proficiency in TypeScript or JavaScript.
Familiarity with npm and modern frontend tooling.
Ability to work collaboratively and communicate clearly within the team.
Nice to Have
Experience with Java (GWT) or Kotlin.
Knowledge of Webpack, Redux, or GraphQL.
Awareness of backend/cloud technologies such as .NET, Kubernetes, Helm, or Azure CLI.
Experience working in SAFe or a similar Agile framework.
What We Offer
You’ll be part of a team that values collaboration, trust, and shared growth. We build things together, learn from each other, and make space for thoughtful discussion as well as hands-on progress. This role gives you the chance to work on a widely used security platform while helping shape how our CWP and CSPM capabilities evolve. You’ll have room to develop your skills, explore new ideas, and be supported by people who care about doing meaningful work well.
