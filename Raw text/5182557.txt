Job description, responsibilities and duties
Are you passionate about cybersecurity and eager to grow your career in a global environment? We’re looking for a motivated Security Operations Engineer who is ready to make a real impact. This is a great opportunity to be part of a dynamic team working on global security projects, where your attention to detail and commitment to excellence will truly matter.
As a SecOps (Security Operations) Engineer, you’ll help bridge the gap between IT operations and cybersecurity — integrating security into every step of our processes, monitoring and managing security controls, and responding to real-world security threats. If you're ready to learn, contribute, and grow in a high-impact role, we’d love to hear from you!
Key responsibilities:
Security policy, compliance & continuous improvement:
you will develop and implement security policies, ensure adherence to industry standards and regulations, analyze security data, identify areas for improvement, and implement changes to enhance the organization's security posture,
Incident response process:
you will investigate security incidents, determine root causes, and implement corrective actions with SOC & IT Teams,
Security monitoring and threat intelligence
: you will monitor the organization's IT infrastructure, networks, and systems to identify potential security vulnerabilities or threats. You will proactively detect and respond to security events or indicators of compromise by logs and other tools collaboration.
Vulnerability and monitoring management
: you will identify security weaknesses in the organization's systems and networks, prioritize vulnerabilities based on their severity, and collaborate with IT teams to patch or mitigate those vulnerabilities in a timely manner. You will monitor the organization's security systems to identify potential security
Security tool management
: you will manage and optimize security tools and technologies deployed within the organization. This includes configuring and fine-tuning security solutions, such as firewalls, antivirus software, intrusion prevention systems (IPS), and endpoint detection and response (X/EDR) tools.
Security awareness and training
: you will develop and deliver security awareness programs based on company & industry standards weakness areas to educate employees about common security behavior, best practices for data protection as well as how to recognize and report potential security incidents.
