Information about the position
Job description, responsibilities and duties
About the Role
As a Senior Data Engineer in the Specialty Analytics team at Swiss Re, you will architect and develop advanced data and analytics solutions that drive key business decisions in our Property & Casualty (P&C) domain. You’ll be part of a forward-looking team using cutting-edge big data technologies to address challenges posed by climate change, geopolitical instability, and emerging risk areas.
You’ll take end-to-end ownership of data solutions, working closely with stakeholders to transform raw data into actionable insights through scalable pipelines and interactive applications.
Key Responsibilities
•	Design and implement end-to-end analytics solutions using Palantir Foundry, including:
o	Scalable data transformation pipelines in PySpark
o	Web applications with Workshop (low-code) and TypeScript
•	Translate business needs into technical specifications in close collaboration with Product Owners and Architects.
•	Troubleshoot and resolve functional and technical data issues for stakeholders.
•	Actively contribute to the evaluation and prototyping of new analytics platform capabilities.
•	Collaborate across a global, cross-functional team to deliver robust and high-impact data products.
About the Team
The Specialty Analytics team delivers tailored analytics solutions across niche lines of business: Agriculture, Aviation, Credit & Surety, Cyber, Nuclear, and Political Violence. We’re part of a global strategic initiative to maximize the value of P&C data and enable data-driven decisions across the reinsurance value chain.
