Information about the position
Job description, responsibilities and duties
This is a senior role within the Resonate Operations Management team, combining strategic leadership with hands-on customer engagement. Role is responsible for delivering strategic leadership as well as delivering the Service management function for Resonate’s Unified Communications and Contact Centre (UCC) Services.
As Lead Service Manager, you will guide a high-performing team of five Service Managers while also acting as a senior Service Manager for a portfolio of key customers yourself. This unique role blends people management, operational excellence and direct customer partnership.
Leadership & Strategy
Provide vision, direction, and coaching for a team of five Service Managers, fostering growth, accountability, and collaboration.
Plan workforce capacity, manage scheduling and on-call coverage, and handle people-related administration (time approval, performance discussions).
Lead, mentor, inspire and develop the team, fostering a culture of accountability, collaboration, and continuous improvement.
Define and execute the strategic roadmap for the Service Management function, aligning with company objectives and customer needs.
Own team performance metrics (KPIs, SLAs, customer satisfaction) and drive continuous improvement across processes and service quality.
Represent the team in senior leadership meetings, shaping decisions that impact service operations across the organization.
Alongside leading the team, you will act as the primary Service Manager for a portfolio of key customers, delivering the full scope of Service Management responsibilities:

Service Performance & Improvement
Monitor and manage service performance against SLA, KPI, and customer satisfaction targets.
Lead service improvement initiatives, coordinating cross-functional delivery teams.
Drive continual improvement of ITIL-based service architecture and processes.
Opportunity Identification

Reporting & Reviews
Deliver accurate and timely performance reports, including analysis and trend insights.
Conduct regular service reviews (monthly/quarterly) with customers and internal stakeholders.
Lead supplier reviews, including ticket analysis and escalation management.
Documentation & Governance
Produce and maintain comprehensive service documentation, including Service Handbooks, RACI matrices, and Service Catalogues, ensuring contractual alignment.
Incident & Problem Management
Lead root cause analysis and major incident resolution, managing SLA breach claims and documentation.
Participate in the on-call rotation to guarantee 24/7 responsiveness.
Service Transition & Onboarding
Oversee onboarding of new services to agreed standards, ensuring operational readiness and formal sign-off.
Contribute to service development and transition projects, including pilots and new deployments.
Proactive & Quality Management
Promote a proactive-first mindset across operations teams to identify and resolve issues before escalation.
Focus on voice quality management, ensuring adherence to proactive and reactive procedures.
