Job description, responsibilities and duties
The IT Business Analyst is part of the Center of Excellence for HR at Siemens Healthineers IT. The CoE’s prime goal is to support the global HR organization with their IT related demands by providing IT solutions and managing & continuously improving global IT services.
Your tasks and responsibilities:
Serve as a functional IT Business Analyst for Talent Acquisition & Onboarding solutions.
Partner with HR leaders to align business needs with IT strategy, acting as a trusted subject matter expert.
Translate business requirements into IT service specifications, ensuring smooth collaboration between business and service management teams.
Oversee implementation of IT solutions that follow IT service management and compliance frameworks.
Take a big-picture view of end-to-end processes, identifying cross-functional dependencies.
Collaborate closely with internal stakeholders and external service providers, driving initiatives from start to finish.
Your qualifications and experience:
Master’s degree in Information Technology, Business Administration, or related field (Bachelor’s degree with significant experience also considered).
Several years of hands-on experience with HR applications, ideally in Talent Acquisition (e.g., Workday, Phenom People, Avature).
Strong understanding of global HR processes, particularly recruiting and onboarding, and related IT solutions/integrations.
Experience working in agile environments and managing IT service providers.
Proven success in leading international, cross-functional HR/IT projects.
Familiarity with compliance requirements for IT projects in highly regulated industries (healthcare a plus).
Your attributes and skills:
Passion for digital technology, cloud solutions, and emerging trends like AI.
Ability to translate business challenges into innovative, value-driven IT solutions.
Strong analytical skills, attention to detail, and commitment to quality under pressure.
Proactive, collaborative team player who takes ownership and drives initiatives to completion.
Excellent communication and stakeholder management skills, with a strong customer-service mindset.
Comfortable working across cultures and organizational levels; international experience a plus.
Fluent in English (written and spoken); German language skills are an advantage
