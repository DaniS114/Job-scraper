Job description, responsibilities and duties
We are looking for an experienced Business Analyst
who will support business model and process analysis, and help us design effective IT solutions.
Key Responsibilities
Lead business requirements gathering and ensure alignment with project goals.
Work with stakeholders to define and document processes, KPIs, and requirements.
Analyze current processes and suggest improvements for higher efficiency.
Translate business needs into clear technical specifications for IT teams.
Create and maintain documentation (requirements, user stories).
Manage backlog, support agile ceremonies, and contribute to continuous delivery.
Support UAT and confirm that solutions meet business objectives.
Provide training and demonstrate product owner skills.
Ensure compliance with company and regulatory policies.
Employee perks, benefits
On-site role in Bratislava.
