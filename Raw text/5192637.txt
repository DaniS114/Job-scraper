Information about the position
Job description, responsibilities and duties
About the role:
In this hands-on engineering role, you'll be responsible for the end-to-end management of our HP Enterprise Managed Print Services ecosystem. You'll balance technical excellence with user experience, ensuring our printing and scanning infrastructure is secure, compliant, and intuitive. Your work will directly contribute to Swiss Re's operational efficiency while supporting our sustainability initiatives through smart print management.
Reporting to the Head of Field Services Service Management, you'll work closely with the MPS Service Manager to align technical implementations with service objectives and business requirements. This collaborative approach ensures our print solutions meet both operational needs and strategic goals.
Key Responsibilities:
- Design, integrate, and maintain HP Enterprise Managed Print and Document Solutions (MPS), including printing, scanning, and copying capabilities globally, ensuring alignment with Swiss Re's technology and security standards.
- Manage and optimize HP print infrastructure components including HP Web Jetadmin (WJA), HP Security Manager, SafeCom PullPrint, and HP SecurePrint.
- Oversee firmware patching, configuration baselines, device compliance, and print driver management in collaboration with global teams.
- Evaluate and document printer hardware and software configurations, ensuring standardization and lifecycle compliance.
- Provide L3/L4 technical support and coordinate with HP Enterprise and vendor partners for resolution of complex issues.
- Develop automation, monitoring, and reporting solutions to enhance reliability and operational efficiency.
- Drive continuous improvement, process optimization and service documentation to ensure a consistent global print experience.
About the Team:
The Workplace Engineering team sits within the Field Services Management Department under Group Technology Services (GTS). Our mission is to design, implement, and maintain workplace technologies that empower Swiss Re employees to work efficiently and securely, wherever they are.
We deliver innovative, user-focused solutions that support the Digital Workplace strategy, combining reliability with security and sustainability. Collaboration and continuous improvement are at the heart of what we do, ensuring that every service we manage contributes to an exceptional employee experience across all Swiss Re locations. Shape
