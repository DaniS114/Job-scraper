Job description, responsibilities and duties
IT Actuarial Business Analyst
We are seeking a highly analytical and technically skilled IT Actuarial Business Analyst to bridge the gap between actuarial teams and IT. This role is pivotal in translating actuarial requirements into robust technical solutions, with a strong focus on leveraging AI and automation to enhance existing actuarial processes.
Your role
As the IT Actuarial Business Analyst, you would have the following responsibilities:
Actuarial Expertise in IT Projects:
Serve as the actuarial subject matter expert within the IT team, ensuring actuarial principles and methodologies are accurately reflected in technical implementations.
Requirements Review & Analysis:
Collaborate with actuarial stakeholders to gather, review, and refine business requirements. Translate these into clear, actionable technical specifications.
Solution Design & Conceptualization:
Develop solution concepts and architecture for actuarial systems, including data flows, system interfaces, and automation strategies.
AI Use Case Development:
Identify and design AI-driven enhancements for existing actuarial solutions (e.g., predictive modeling, anomaly detection, process optimization).
Stakeholder Collaboration:
Act as a liaison between IT developers, data scientists, and actuarial teams to ensure alignment and mutual understanding throughout the project lifecycle.
Documentation & Communication:
Produce high-quality documentation including business requirements, technical specifications, and user guides. Present solutions and findings to both technical and non-technical audiences.

Brief description of the company
Zurich is a leading multi-line insurer that serves its customers in global and local markets. With about 60,000 employees it provides a wide range of property and casualty, life insurance products and services in more than 200 countries and territories. Zurich’s customers include individuals, small businesses, and mid-sized and large companies, as well as multinational corporations.
Zurich Slovakia is a Competence Center based in Bratislava and Kosice providing highly specialized services for Zurich's headquarter in Switzerland and all Zurich Business units around the globe. Our services are focusing on all types of actuarial services – Life and Non-life, pricing and reserving, on data science and engineering, risk management, business intelligence, information technology, software asset management as well as insurance network management and technical accounting. Our Competence Center employs around 400+ staff from 20+ different countries.
