Job description, responsibilities and duties
As an GenAI & ML Developer you will play a leading role at shaping Arcondis AI & ML Platform to bring direct benefits to our employees as well as support on client projects with your expertise and experience in designing and implementing costumer AI-Solution in the pharma, MedTech and public health industries, ultimately helping patients around the world.
Build and deploy GenAI      solutions, including advanced Retrival-Augmented Generation (RAG) systems
Design (generative) AI      applications that can solve a broad range of problems, enhance      decision-making, and optimize processes within the healthcare and life      science sectors.
Own the full development      lifecycle from concept to production
Collaborate with cross-functional      teams to integrate AI/ML solutions into broader IT and business systems      being the integration driver & architect.
Maintain      a proactive approach to learning and applying the latest advancements AI,      ML, LLMs and the infrastructure that supports them.
