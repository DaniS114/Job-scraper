We are seeking an experienced and visionary
Senior UX/UI Designer
, assigned with a specific role,
Chapter Lead
, to drive the
design strategy
and execution across our digital products. This role is pivotal in ensuring
seamless, innovative, and user-centric experiences
for our customers in the fast-paced telecommunication sectors. You will
lead a multidisciplinary team
, foster a culture of design excellence, and collaborate closely with cross-functional partners to deliver outstanding results.
Key responsibilities:
Lead and Inspire:
Manage, mentor, and develop a team of UX/UI designers, fostering creativity, collaboration, and professional growth.
Strategic Design Leadership:
Define and implement UX/UI vision and strategy aligned with business goals, ensuring a unified design language and consistent user experience across all platforms.
Project Oversight:
Oversee the full design process, from research and ideation to prototyping, testing, and final execution, ensuring timely and high-quality delivery.
Cross-functional Collaboration:
Work closely with business specialists, engineers, marketers, and other stakeholders to integrate user-centered design practices into product development.
Experiment with AI-Accelerated Design Delivery:
Leverage cutting-edge AI design tools (Lovable, Replit, v0, Cursor, etc.) to rapidly prototype and iterate on UX concepts, enabling dramatic reduction in concept-to-prototype timelines while maintaining design quality
Rapid Design Synthesis:
Utilize AI-powered workflows to distill complex requirements into refined UI solutions within compressed timeframes.
User Research & Testing:
Guide user research, usability testing, and data-driven design decisions to continuously improve product usability and customer satisfaction.
Design Systems & Standards:
Develop and maintain design systems, frameworks, and style guides to ensure scalability and consistency across digital products. Collaborate with DT central teams Design team.
Performance & Quality:
Ensure the highest standards of design quality and user experience, using analytics and feedback to drive continuous improvement
Stakeholder Communication:
Present design concepts and solutions to stakeholders, advocating for the user and the value of design at every stage.
