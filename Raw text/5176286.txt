The administrator is expected to provide ongoing systems administration support including installation, maintenance and troubleshooting of system software, applications and connectivity components. Provides technical support and advises on the use of database systems and networks. Provides support to address availability and reliability issues on various system versions across multiple locations. Operational responsibilities include remediation of daily incident tickets, system compliance, system run enhancement, testing, staging, policy / procedure enhancement, and operational recommendations.
