Job description, responsibilities and duties
Resco helps the world work better outside the office by making it simple to build complex mobile experiences for frontline workers. Over 800 enterprise and corporate companies from segments like utilities, retail, energy, oil & gas, manufacturing, telecommunications, transportation & logistics, or NGOs rely on Resco to simplify their frontline operations, improve efficiency, and reduce paperwork.
We are looking for a Junior QA Engineer to help increase automated test coverage and reduce the need for manual testing. Your role will involve defining what needs to be automated and implementing tests using modern tools like Playwright.
What you’ll do:
Run & improve regression and smoke tests for core CRM flows
Write and maintain automated UI/integration tests (we use Playwright + TypeScript today — improvements are welcome)
Reproduce, document, and report bugs with clear steps and context; collaborate with developers
Create stable test data and realistic customer scenarios
Reduce flaky tests and keep the test suite fast and useful
Learn and grow into broader testing areas (E2E, performance, observability)
You’re a fit if:
Comfortable with UI testing and validating integrations
Know Git basics, can read logs and debug simple issues
Basic automation experience — Playwright + TypeScript is a plus; open to other stacks (Python/JS/Java)
Curious, communicative, and takes ownership

Brief description of the company
Resco, founded in 1999, is one of the global leaders in developing field operations and mobile enterprise software solutions. The company enables organizations to use and collect business data in the field and digitize paper-based processes through state-of-the-art technology. The solutions enable integration of various CRM and ERP systems, including Microsoft Dynamics 365, Salesforce, or Resco’s standalone platform - Resco Cloud. Today, Resco empowers over 2,500 clients and 200,000 users worldwide and transforms their field operations and knowledge management.
