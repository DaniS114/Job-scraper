IT OSS Senior Solution Designer (f/m)
full-time
Bajkalská 28, Ružinov
From 3 500 EUR/month
the final salary offered will be agreed together on the basis of your skills and experience
Are you an experienced IT OSS Solution Designer ready to transform the OSS domain at T-Mobile CZ and Slovak Telekom? We are looking for a visionary leader who will design and implement our comprehensive OSS transformation, replacing legacy systems with a new architecture based on the TM Forum ODA (Open Digital Architecture) and Open API concepts. This key role will redefine operations support systems and directly improve service delivery, resilience, and flexibility.
About the Role:
IT OSS Senior Solution Designer position is key to our organizations’ strategic OSS Transformation. You will lead the solution design across key operational domains, including Network Inventory (Physical and Logical - PNI/LNI), Workflow Systems, Monitoring, Event Management, and IT Integration. Your expertise will be crucial in strategically replacing existing OSS legacy systems including TIBCO-based IT integration (covering Enterprise Integration services incl. business process logic and Service order management) with a modern, API-driven, and cloud-native OSS architecture, significantly enhancing business agility, accelerating time-to-market, and optimizing operational efficiency.
Key Responsibilities:
Design and execute
strategic roadmaps for our
OSS Transformation
, specifically covering PNI/LNI, Workflow Systems, Monitoring, Event Management, and the replacement of legacy IT Integration.
Lead the design and implementation

new IT integration layer
fully compliant with TM Forum Open APIs and Open Digital Architecture (ODA) principles across our IT ecosystem.
Define and implement integration and orchestration patterns
(API Gateway, service mesh, event streaming ...) to ensure scalability, resilience, and decoupled system communication across OSS domains.
Design and develop
modern
cloud-native solutions
for all core OSS domains, utilizing microservices, containerization, and event-driven architectures as the foundation for this new IT Integration.
Mentor technical teams

best practices
to build organizational capability in advanced OSS architecture and the new IT integration solution.
Collaborate closely with DevSecOps teams
to design and maintain robust CI/CD pipelines, automated testing, and deployment strategies supporting continuous delivery of integration components.
Ensure architectural alignment
between design, development, and operations through API-first and cloud-native design practices.
