Information about the position
Job description, responsibilities and duties
Join a dynamic and innovative Property & Casualty Data Analytics team. Our mission is to harness the power of Data Analytics to create a competitive advantage for Property & Casualty Reinsurance. By generating actionable insights, we aim to enhance data-driven decision-making and improve business outcomes.
We’re looking for an Analytics Product Owner to join our team and own the end-to-end lifecycle of analytics products—from ideation to delivery and ongoing improvement. In this role, you will bridge business and engineering, translating strategic needs into actionable solutions, and driving tangible value creation through data.
If you enjoy a combination of business interaction, hands-on technical work, and product management in a global environment, this might be the perfect role for you! You'll get to participate in a variety of interesting initiatives.
About the Role
As an Analytics Product Owner, you will:
- Own the end-to-end lifecycle of data and analytics products, from idea to implementation, ensuring alignment with business goals and user needs.
- Translate business problems into analytics solutions by collaborating closely with stakeholders, data scientists, engineers, and domain experts.
- Define and maintain the product roadmap, prioritising features and use cases based on value, feasibility, and strategic fit.
- Drive the end-to-end QBR process by preparing content, aligning with key stakeholders, and presenting progress, outcomes, and strategic priorities for owned products, enabling informed decision-making at senior levels.
- Ensure delivery excellence by managing sprint goals, MVP definitions, acceptance criteria, and ongoing feature refinement.
- Drive adoption and measurable impact, ensuring analytics products are embedded into business processes and generate tangible value.
- Act as the voice of the user, collecting feedback, defining user stories, and ensuring a consistent and intuitive user experience.
-Work with cross-functional teams to ensure successful delivery.
- Stay up to date with the latest technologies and best practices to drive sustainable and efficient data products.
Key responsibilities
- Define and drive the product vision for data and analytics capabilities, aligning with business priorities, AI transformation goals, and cross-functional strategies.
- Manage the product backlog and roadmap, prioritising features and use cases based on business impact, user needs, and technical feasibility.
- Translate business requirements into actionable user stories by collaborating closely with underwriters, claims experts, actuaries, and data scientists.
- Leads preparation and execution of QBRs, aligning stakeholders and driving data-informed decisions.
- Ensure high-quality delivery and adoption, by coordinating with engineering teams, validating outputs, and tracking usage and business value across releases.
- Act as a key interface between business and tech, aligning with data governance, platform architecture, and enterprise-wide AI initiatives to ensure scalable, compliant solutions.
