Information about the position
Job description, responsibilities and duties
As a IT Business Analyst in our Corporate Functions InfoTech team, you'll be at the intersection of business and technology, translating complex requirements into effective solutions. You'll collaborate with stakeholders across Legal & Compliance, Audit, Procurement, and Finance to understand their needs and deliver systems that enhance their operational capabilities.
Key Responsibilities
•	Requirement Analysis: Gather, analyze, and document business requirements, translating them into functional specifications for technical teams
•	Solution Design: Collaborate with technical teams and actual hands-on to configure our Expense reporting tool. Based on business requirements.
•	Testing Coordination: Develop test plans and coordinate user acceptance testing to ensure solutions meet requirements
•	Process Optimization: Identify opportunities to improve business processes through technology solutions
•	Documentation: Create comprehensive documentation for business requirements, solution designs, and user guides
•	Change Management: Support the implementation of new solutions, including training and communication planning
About the Team
We are responsible for Swiss Re's Legal & Compliance, Audit, Procurement, and parts of the Finance applications. We work in a scaled agile model with shared leadership teams and broad aligned autonomy for our squads. We are located across the globe but share the same values for passion, openness, respect, humbleness, and curiosity. And we strive to improve our work for our customers every day.

Personality requirements and skills
You're a detail-oriented professional who thrives at the intersection of business and technology. You have excellent analytical skills and can translate complex business requirements into clear technical specifications. Your communication skills allow you to build strong relationships with stakeholders at all levels, and you're comfortable explaining technical concepts to non-technical audiences.
