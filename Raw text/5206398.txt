The Opportunity
As a DevOps & Mobility Specialist at Takeda, you will support the design, implementation, and operation of DevOps and mobility solutions that enable secure and efficient software delivery. You’ll work within a collaborative, scaled agile environment alongside business, product, and engineering teams. Your focus will include automating deployments, supporting mobile application delivery, and continuously improving Takeda’s developer platform and cloud infrastructure. This is a great opportunity for a technically skilled engineer to deepen their expertise in DevOps, cloud, and secure mobile delivery within a regulated, high-impact healthcare environment.
Key Responsibilities
DevOps & Automation
-Support the development, automation, and maintenance of CI/CD pipelines for cloud and mobile applications.
-Assist in configuring build and release workflows for Android and iOS applications, including integration with Google Play and Apple App Store.
- Help implement Infrastructure as Code for cloud resources (with Terraform, Ansible, or similar tools).
Cloud Infrastructure & Platform Support
- Participate in deploying and supporting public cloud infrastructure (AWS, Azure, or GCP).
- Work with team members to maintain container orchestration platforms (e.g., Kubernetes/EKS/AKS) and related DevOps tools.
- Help expand and support the Takeda Internal Developer Portal and self-service tooling.
Security & Compliance
- Participate integrating security and compliance requirements into build and deployment processes.
- Support auditing, monitoring, and documentation of DevOps workflows to meet quality and regulatory needs.
Collaboration & Continuous Improvement
- Collaborate with software engineers, QA, and cybersecurity teams to address challenges and improve platform reliability.
- Contribute to platform documentation, knowledge sharing, and team onboarding.
- Stay up to date on DevOps, cloud, and mobile best practices to support the team’s continuous improvement.

Personality requirements and skills
Required
- Bachelor’s degree in computer science, engineering, or a related field, or equivalent hands-on experience.
- 3+ years’ experience in DevOps, cloud engineering, or systems engineering roles.
- Experience configuring and automating build and deployment processes for Android and/or iOS applications, including distributing apps through Google Play or Apple App Store using CI tools.
-Familiarity with Infrastructure as Code (Terraform, Ansible, or comparable).
- Practical experience with containerization (Docker) and basic Kubernetes orchestration.
- Experience setting up or supporting CI/CD pipelines (e.g., GitHub Actions, Jenkins, or similar).
- Scripting experience (BASH, PowerShell, or Python).
-Good communication and collaborative skills.
Preferred:
- Exposure to public cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, or GCP).
- Prior experience supporting mobile application delivery in a regulated or enterprise environment.
- Familiarity with application life cycle management (ALM) tools.
- Basic understanding of DevSecOps best practices and secure software delivery concepts.
- Willingness to learn and contribute to a high-performance, cross-functional team.
