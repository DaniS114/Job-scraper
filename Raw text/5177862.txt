German-Speaking SAP & IT Specialists
Job description, responsibilities and duties
Step into the forefront of
SAP-driven strategic transformation
and innovation with our dynamic international team. We are seeking skilled professionals fluent in
German
and
English
who possess a deep understanding of SAP systems and project implementations. Your expertise will contribute significantly to our operations and elevate client experiences within the financial sector.
Why Join Us?
Enhance SAP Expertise:
Collaborate on projects that leverage your SAP skills to transform business processes.
Strategic Impact:
Use your proficiency in German to drive solutions that have meaningful client impacts.
Collaborative Culture:
Join teams that value communication, innovation, and stakeholder engagement.
Position Overview:
Be a key player in initiating and implementing SAP solutions that propel our clients towards operational efficiency and business growth. We offer a variety of roles depending on your experience and interests:
SAP Implementation & Management:
Cutover Manager:
Ensures smooth transitions in SAP release and cutover processes, safeguarding operational continuity.
Migration Manager:
Handles SAP migration projects, focusing on S/4HANA, with extensive experience in SAP data migration strategies, tools, and selective data transfer techniques.
Basics & Permissions Specialist:
Manages SAP Basis and authorizations, focusing on technical implementation and system/module architecture.
Software Development & Testing in SAP:
Developer including Fiori:
Focuses on software development within SAP environments, primarily using SAP standard software like S/4HANA or BW/4HANA with necessary enhancements. Requires expertise in programming languages and frameworks such as ABAP or SAP Fiori, including  SAP Business Technology Platform (BTP).
Test Manager
– Finance Trafo S4: Manages testing activities within SAP projects, ensuring quality assurance and risk management compliance. Extensive project management experience with proficiency in software testing methodologies and tools like Selenium and SAP Solution Manager.
Test Management Senior Associate:
Directs comprehensive test management, focusing on SAP environments and tools such as Jira and Micro Focus ALM. ISTQB Certified Tester Foundation Level (or equivalent) is advantageous.
These roles demand strong proficiency in
German
, which is essential for interacting with clients and ensuring successful project deployment.
