Data Analyst
We are on a mission to make recruiting, talent attraction and marketing smarter and more data-driven. As part of our dynamic team, you will design and develop data models and reports, integrate diverse data sources, and create and track key performance indicators (KPIs) across various applicant tracking systems (ATS), while understanding the business needs of our stakeholders. Your work at the intersection of HR, Marketing, and Technology leads to transparency, efficiency, and strategic growth across global markets which helps us to make better decisions.

About
the position
The role is for a Data Scientist, with responsibilities spanning both data analysis and data engineering.
The unit operates within the HR field, focusing on integrating data from various HR activities such as employer branding campaigns, social media, and recruiting into one comprehensive dashboard.
The candidate will build, maintain, and develop this dashboard and contribute towards enabling data-driven decision-making regarding resource and budget allocation.
We work in a HYBRID STYLE of working with the possibility of home office, but twice a week we meet in the office for meetings.
The published salary is the minimum possible offer. The starting salary may be higher depending on the extent of fulfillment of the employee's requirements (education, language skills, required practice, personality assumptions and skills).
