Job description, responsibilities and duties
We are looking for an experienced QA Automation Engineer to join a long-term project for one of our clients operating in the heavy machinery industry. In this role, you will be responsible for ensuring the quality, reliability and scalability of complex software systems through automated and manual testing. You will work as part of a cross-functional agile team, collaborating closely with developers, product owners and other stakeholders.
As a QA Automation Engineer with us you will:
Design, develop, and maintain automated test solutions using Python.
Perform functional, integration, regression, and end-to-end testing.
Analyze business and technical requirements and translate them into automated and manual test cases.
Work on testing of complex systems, applications, and integrations.
Identify, document, track, and verify software defects using bug tracking tools.
Collaborate with Product Owners and development teams to clarify requirements and resolve issues.
Prepare and maintain test plans, including scope, objectives, risks, and deliverables.
Integrate automated tests into CI/CD pipelines.
Execute and report on quantitative and qualitative test metrics.
Ensure testing activities are performed in a timely, repeatable, and effective manner.
Contribute to continuous improvement of QA processes, tools, and standards.
