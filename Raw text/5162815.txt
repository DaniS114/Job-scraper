Information about the position
Job description, responsibilities and duties
Become a part of the team that delivers solutions for Asset Management, builds integration between trading, finance and risk management systems and provides reporting solutions to streamline business processes and adhere to financial regulations. You will work with a variety of tools and technologies and cooperate with multiple teams within the organization.
About the Role
Key responsibilities:
-Develop and maintain the Azure application and associated reporting solution.
-Design and implement scalable, secure, and efficient solutions.
-Optimize application performance and troubleshoot complex issues.
-Create and maintain automated test cases to build & maintain regression test suites.
-Establish and manage the solution documentation.
As a member of our team, you will:
-Work with a range of tools and technologies.
-Analyse requirements, understand specifications and transform them into technical solutions.
-Develop, debug and maintain Azure functions in Python.
-Create and maintain small applications in Palantir's Foundry platform.
-Perform analyses of existing code, identify problems and propose solutions.
-Contribute to an environment that cares for continuous learning and improvement.
-Work closely with business analysts and business stakeholders.
About the Team
We are a small team, part of Group Digital & Technology (GDTO) serving the Asset Management domain within Swiss Re. The team is located in Bratislava, collaborating with business and IT teams in numerous locations worldwide including New York, London and Zurich. The application is a key part of the Asset Management landscape linking trading, valuation and finance platforms and is therefore fundamental to the timely completion of the overnight batch process that facilitates daily financial and regulatory reporting.

Personality requirements and skills
Your passion is engineering and fully recognize the benefits of ETL processes that ensure data is accurate, reliable, and accessible. You thrive when working in a strong engineering culture and have a passion for multi-cultural teamwork and help others in your team to achieve their full potential. You strive to share team expertise and help us increase our Agile maturity. We understand the value of AI in development, but you can work with classes, functions and API calls without ChatGPT.
