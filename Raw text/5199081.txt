Job description, responsibilities and duties
Hey there,
We are looking for a
Solution Pre-Sale Manager
with strong business analysis expertise and a solid tech background in IT or software development companies.
The ideal candidate is a strategic, business-minded professional who can bridge client needs and technical solutions, collaborating closely with Solution Architects and delivery teams to design effective, winning proposals.
This role blends analytical insight, exceptional communication skills, and strategic solution design. You will work directly with international clients, gather and interpret business requirements, and contribute to building scalable, efficient solutions that align with client objectives and delivery capabilities.
Key Responsibilities
Collaborate with clients during the pre-sales phase to understand business challenges, objectives, and success criteria
Translate business needs into clear functional and technical requirements for Solution Architects and delivery teams
Contribute analytical and strategic insights across diverse industries and project domains
Build strong, trust-based relationships with clients and internal stakeholders
Support proposal development, solution presentations, and client demonstrations
Perform in-depth business analysis, including requirements gathering, gap assessments, and process mapping
Requirements
3+ years of experience in Pre-Sales, Business Analysis, Delivery/Project Management, or comparable roles in the IT/software industry
Strong understanding of the software development lifecycle, delivery models, and estimation practices
Solid technical foundation and ability to engage with technical teams
Proven expertise in requirements elicitation, documentation, and business process analysis
Excellent command of English (C1+), both written and spoken
Strong analytical mindset, structured communication, and a client-oriented approach
Nice to have:
Proficiency in an additional EU language besides English

Brief description of the company
Digicode Europe s.r.o. is a custom software, mobile app, and next generation global technologies development company based in Dallas Texas, with development centers in the US, Costa Rica, Israel, Ukraine, and Slovakia.
Nowadays, our team focuses on developing mobile applications, payment systems, back office software platforms and other software products. We specialize in developing for mobile platforms, for iOS, Android platforms, web applications, server platform technologies such as .NET, PHP and more.
Digicode's, main focus is on end-to-end projects as opposed to classic outsourcing models. We provide our customers with high-quality product planning, development, maintenance and support services.
Digicode's, managers and developers have strong backgrounds and years of experience gained by supplying software development services.
We have expertise in working with remote clients and teams. We use cutting-edge time project management methodologies and tools, providing our clients the benefits of ongoing tracking project status, analytics, dashboards and more.
