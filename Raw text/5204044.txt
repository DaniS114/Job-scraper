WE DELIVER TECHNOLOGY SOLUTIONS CONNECTING YOUR WORLD!
Technology Delivery International (TDI) is a key part of Deutsche Telekom, driving innovation across Europe. We deliver the full product lifecycle of telecommunications IT and network technology solutions - from engineering and architecture to development, testing, providing infrastructure, and managing operations. Our international teams work closely with Deutsche Telekom’s national companies to bring these solutions to life, ensuring seamless delivery and continuous improvement.
We operate in a chapter-based model that emphasizes skill development and collaboration. This enables us to combine deep expertise with agile delivery, empowering our people while creating real impact for our customers.
Key responsibilites
Drive security best practices across development and operations (secure coding, vulnerability scanning, threat detection).
Ensure compliance and data protection (IAM, audit logging, regulatory standards).
Automate operations and contribute to Site Reliability Engineering.
Build and scale cloud platforms using Infrastructure-as-Code.
Support lifecycle management, upgrades, and incident response.
Participate in on-call rotation.
What you will bring
Degree in Computer Science, Telco, Information Systems or similar.
2+ years of cloud experience (OpenStack, Kubernetes, or public cloud).
Strong knowledge of Linux, scripting (bash/shell), and CI/CD (GitOps).
Hands-on with security domains: IAM, encryption, logging, vulnerability management, WAFs, certificates.
Proficiency in Go, Python, or similar.
Nice to have: API development, Kubernetes operators, observability tools (Prometheus, Grafana, OpenSearch).
What we value
Curiosity and continuous learning.
Creative problem solving.
Strong ownership combined with team spirit.
Clear communication with different stakeholders.
What you will gain from us
Innovative projects within Deutsche Telekom Group.
Knowledge sharing, mentoring, and long-term career growth.
Flexible hours, part-time and remote options.
Competitive salary and benefits in a supportive, open environment.
