Job description, responsibilities and duties
We are looking for a skilled and talented Business Intelligence Analyst to join our team. Reporting directly to the CEO, you will play a key role in transforming raw data into valuable business insights. Through the use of data analytics, visualisation, modelling and reporting, you will directly impact decisions in marketing, product, sales, finance and IT. You’ll work alongside different teams, interpret and present complex data sets, and be instrumental in guiding our strategic direction.
​This is an exciting opportunity to make your mark in one of the most successful webhosting providers in CEE. If you have what it takes, apply today!​
