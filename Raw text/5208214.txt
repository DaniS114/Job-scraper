Job description, responsibilities and duties
The GDC team leader will be part of the ServiceNow team, supporting GDC employees, business partners and functional manager in different IT business lines and
the Customer Services organization.
You are the main contact for the ServiceNow IT Operations Professionals during regular operations as well as in all escalations, being responsible for ensuring a smooth operations management.
You also support all strategic initiatives and projects, and act as proxy for your functional manager.
Task and Responsibilities:
Manage the designated team of IT Ops professionals in their daily work
Conduct regular one-on-one meetings, follow SHS People & Leadership Practices
Maintain relationship with the Business Partners, seek upselling opportunities
Support strategic initiatives and projects as per requirements provided by Business Partners
Act as proxy for functional manager
Drive or provide support for service-related matters
Support the organization in growth and actively participate in defining and executing its strategy
Operationally manage critical situations and solve escalations to provide seamless delivery
Steer feedback-oriented culture
Supervise, motive and guide team members and assess performance and take necessary measures if required
Communicate management updates among team members
