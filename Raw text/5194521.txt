SAP HCM Consultant (Mid-Level) – Time Management & Personnel Administration/Organizational Management
Place of work
Remote work
Contract type
full-time
POSITION
DESCRIPTION
We are seeking an experienced SAP HCM Consultant with strong expertise in Time Management and Personnel Administration/Organizational Management (PA/OM). In this role, you will be responsible for designing, implementing, and supporting SAP HCM solutions that optimize HR processes and ensure compliance with organizational and legal requirements.
WHAT WILL
YOU DO
• Configure and maintain SAP HCM modules, focusing on Time Management and PA/OM.
• Analyze business requirements and translate them into functional specifications.
• Support system upgrades, enhancements, and integration with other HR systems.
• Provide end-user training and documentation.
• Troubleshoot and resolve issues related to SAP HCM processes.
• Collaborate with HR, Payroll, and IT teams to ensure seamless operations.
WHAT WE
EXPECT
• Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in IT or a related field.
• At least 3 to 5 years of hands-on experience with SAP HCM, specifically Time Management and PA/OM.
• Strong understanding of HR processes and legal compliance.
• Experience with schema and PCR configuration for Time Management.
• Knowledge of integration with Payroll and other SAP modules.
• English language proficiency at upper intermediate (B2) or advanced (C1) level.
• Experience with SAP SuccessFactors or other cloud HR solutions.
• Implementation experience.
