Description
Analyst in Tax Operations - Global Tax Calculation Services
We're seeking a detail-oriented Tax Analyst to maintain tax calculation and to drive innovative strategies that power Amazon's global e-commerce ecosystem. In this pivotal role, you'll transform complex tax challenges into seamless, automated solutions that impact thousands of merchants worldwide.
A Day in the Life
Your day will be a dynamic blend of technical problem-solving and collaborative innovation. You'll dive into intricate tax calculation systems, leverage advanced tools, and work alongside cross-functional experts to streamline global tax processes. Expect to engage in data analysis, system optimization, and strategic improvements that make a tangible difference.
About the Team
Our Global Tax Calculation Services team is a collaborative powerhouse of tax professionals and software engineers. We're passionate about developing end-to-end transaction tax solutions that support Amazon and global e-commerce platforms. Our vision is to simplify complex tax landscapes through technology and expertise.
Key Job Responsibilities
Maintain Tax Calculation Systems and apply any legislative changes
Design user-centric interfaces and identify optimal resource allocation for tax calculation systems
Collaborate with Tax, Legal, Product, and Tech teams to investigate and resolve complex tax-related challenges
Drive continuous improvement by identifying process inefficiencies and developing scalable, automated calculation solutions
Support testing and validation of innovative projects, including machine learning and AI-enhanced tax workflows
Generate comprehensive data analysis and reports using business intelligence tools to inform strategic decision-making
Basic Qualifications
Bachelor's/Masters degree
Skilled in Excel
Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
Excellent written and verbal communication abilities
Understanding of tax calculation principles and methodologies
Preferred Qualifications
Experience with machine learning or AI technologies
Knowledge of international tax regulations
Experience in e-commerce or technology-driven tax solutions
Compensation and Benefits
Base pay for this position starts from EUR 2133 gross per month and salary depends on the skills and requirements, there will be further pay components such as a sign on bonus and the eligibility to participate in a restricted stock unit scheme operated independently by Amazon.com Inc. in USA. Company benefits apply subject to further terms, including annual membership in medical clinic, life insurance, retirement pension, meal voucher card, career development.
Work Arrangement
Office-based role with flexibility for remote work in exceptional circumstances, aligned with Amazon's current work guidance.
Key job responsibilities
Configuration and ongoing maintenance of Amazon.com’s transaction tax calculation software.
Researching transaction tax laws to create and maintain the tax rules (e.g., product taxability) within the transaction tax calculation software.
Participating in projects throughout their lifecycle – from design through delivery – with an emphasis on testing to ensure that projects conform to the business requirements and rules.
Participate in team operational excellence programs and process improvements.
Communicating tax rules and offering subject matter expertise on tax calculation software to internal and external teams.
