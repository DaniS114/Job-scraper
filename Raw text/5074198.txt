Job description, responsibilities and duties
About the Team
Our team is at the forefront of providing solutions for underwriters and analytics for Reinsurance. We build apps and analytical products that focus on a wide range of topics around Contract Wordings.
Joining us, you will learn the technologies that are used in modern apps and data solutions with a focus on cloud technologies and distributed systems, in a collaborative environment with colleagues from Swiss Re's international offices.
As a backend developer, you will collaborate in a team of around 15 developers, implementing new user stories, but also helping to maintain running applications. You will be part of a team catering for apps for our core contract processes, working side-by-side with our business engineers, data engineers and other technology professionals. You will be fully engaged in stories preparation across various technologies, namely Java and Azure.
In your role you are responsible for:
· Participating in story preparation & implementation
· Development of new applications and extending existing together with the team
· Maintenance of existing applications
· Design BE services to support modern and responsive UI/UX frontends
· Monitoring applications
· Staying on top of emerging trends using up to date technologies for development and testing
