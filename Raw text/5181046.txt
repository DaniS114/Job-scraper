Job description, responsibilities and duties
​Senior Network Engineer
is responsible for designing and implementing effective and scalable network solutions that meet the business needs of the organization. This role involves assessing current network infrastructure, identifying areas for improvement, and developing strategic plans to enhance network performance, security, and reliability.​
JOB RESPONSIBILITIES
Network Architecture Design:
Design and develop comprehensive network solutions that align with business objectives and technical requirements.
Ensure network solutions are scalable, reliable, and secure.
Evaluate and select appropriate networking technologies and solutions.
Implementation and Deployment:
Lead the implementation and deployment of network solutions.
Oversee configuration, installation, and maintenance of network hardware and software.
Ensure successful integration of network solutions with existing systems.
Network Performance and Optimization:
Monitor network performance and identify areas for improvement.
Implement strategies to optimize network performance, reduce latency, and increase throughput.
Conduct regular network assessments and audits.
Security and Compliance:
Implement robust security measures to protect network infrastructure from threats.
Ensure network solutions comply with relevant regulations and industry standards.
Conduct regular security assessments and vulnerability testing.
Collaboration and Communication:
Work closely with other IT teams, departments, and stakeholders to understand their networking needs.
Provide technical guidance and support to IT staff and end-users.
Communicate network solution designs and changes to relevant stakeholders.
Documentation and Reporting:
Maintain comprehensive documentation of network architectures, configurations, and processes.
Prepare and present reports on network performance, incidents, and compliance status.
Develop and update network policies, procedures, and guidelines.
Continuous Improvement:
Stay updated with the latest trends, technologies, and best practices in network architecture.
Identify opportunities for continuous improvement in network solutions and processes.
Participate in professional development activities and training.

Brief description of the company
Vissim s.r.o. is a subsidiary of Norway's Vissim AS, a leading market monitoring and traffic management company in ports, oil platforms, and surrounding wind farms. The main advantage of our software is that it can process large amounts of data, integrate and display it in real time for operator needs. In conjunction with the software, the company delivers comprehensive hardware for monitoring and communication. The best example is a project in the North Sea, where Vissim integrates 100 radars on 70 platforms along with AIS sensors and a communications network.
